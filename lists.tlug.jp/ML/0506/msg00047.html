Basically, your CDDB query returned two entries for the CD you entered.
Both of them were identified as the record "Grundsatzliche
Betrachtungen" (roughly: basic considerations/observations - the
correct translation of the German word Betrachtung can vary based on
context) from an artist called "Gunkl".
Moreover, the former entry is classified as Alternative Rock, the
latter as Carbaret.
