and again. They look like this on the pop3-server:

|From tlug-request@example.com Mon Dec 02 11:43:15 2002
|Return-path: <tlug-request@example.com>
[...]
|From: "Jonathan Q" <jq@example.com>

|Content-Type: text/plain; charset=ISO-8859-1
|ext/plain; charset=ISO-8859-1

|Dec  3 23:34:19 melchior fetchmail[745]: message delimiter found while scanning headers

|Dec  3 23:34:19 melchior fetchmail[745]:  not flushed
