The reader arrived just two days after I've ordered it, and I've spent
the better part of this morning trying to get it to work, but with no
luck.  Despite having looked at the Linux USB Guide as well as several 
HOWTOs written by people who got USB devices working on their boxen.
I've compiled the following into the kernel:

USB support
usbdevfs support
UHCI alternate diver support (I have a VIA chipset, this should be right)
USB mass storage support
Michrotech and Sandisk support (clutching at straws)
SCSI disk support
SCSI generic support
MSDOS FAT and VFAT support
MSDOS partition tables support (probably not necessary)

E:  Ad=02(O) Atr=02(Bulk) MxPS=  64 Ivl=0ms
