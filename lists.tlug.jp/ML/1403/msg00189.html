This is of course an option, but not as simple as it seems. I would
have to buy 3 drives in my case (my wife is Chinese).
Just finding them, checking power (if not powered by USB), etc... will
be a challenge. Price is not null too for BD drives,
much higher than DVD drives: Checking amazon gaves me the first price
of EUR 70 for a model suggested before.
This would give EUR 210 if I consider the price will be the same for
the 3. If I cannot find different zones devices in my
country - my first quick search seems to indicate this could be true -
guaranty is another point.

Not easy when travelling too (I want external USB for this reason).
Another reason I keep in mind is that my wife could
want some day to read BD on her own machine.
Having 6 devices (mostly unused, as 70% of BD are region free
according to Wikipedia) instead of 2 is not really appealing
from an IT point of view (not forgetting user and cost - a net loss of
EUR 280/JPY 40,000 according to numbers above).

However, what I understood so far is that region free is maybe
possible. Therefore I will go for testing it first as I wrote in
my previous post, just to keep things as simple as possible in the long run.
Only if I fail, I will start searching Russian web sites (same region
as China) to find region C drive :-)

Thanks to everybody anyway,

Bruno.
