JSTV [1] satellite broadcast (essentially, a repackaging of NHK
kokusai housou).

grabber (recognized as VP1032), which is OK for watching FTA programs
but next to unusable for encrypted streams.  I've got a Cryptoworks CAM
and a regular JSTV card.  Anyone having the same goal and possibly a
success story to tell?
