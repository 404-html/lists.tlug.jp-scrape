Hello, my name's Shigenobu Koufugata of mozilla-gumi which is Mozilla
Users Group Japan.

 Mozilla 1.0 will be released soon. Mozilla-gumi/Japan will be holding a
free conference and party. Everyone is welcome! Attendance limited to
300. Please register below to secure your spot.

Theme     Mozilla1.0 Release Mini-Conference and Party

Date        15:00-19:30, Sat. May 18th, 2002

Place       SONPO-KAIKAN (Near Akihabara Or Ochanomizu JR station,
Tokyo)
      [map]
      http://www.sonpo-k.co.jp/map.html

Host        Mozilla-gumi（Mozilla Users Group Japan)

Sponsors      Mozilla.org,Netscape Communications
Corporation,CreatePower Corporation,Oracle Corporation Japan,Begi.net
Corporation,VA Linux Systems Japan Corporation

Supporters   OEone Corporation,SELSystem

Events
PART I: Mini-Conference

- A. Key Note Speech: "Mozilla 1.0 Release and Beyond" By Mr. Asa
Dotzler [mozilla.org] (Japanese interpretation provided). 15:00-16:00
- B. Panel Discussion: "Mozilla 2.0 and Mozilla movement in Japan:
Dreams come true" By Mozilla-gumi, Netscape, Mozilla.org. 16:00-17:00


PART II: Party (fees; General: 2,000 yen, Student: 1,000 yen)
17:30-19:30

- A. Demonstrations (Mozilla Applications)
- B. Party with beer and light meal.


Please register here
 http://www.mozilla.gr.jp/party/registration.html

Many thanks,
