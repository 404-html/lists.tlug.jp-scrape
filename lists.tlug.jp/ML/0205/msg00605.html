> Ok, so now I guess my next step is to find an xfs patch against my kernel? 
> How do I exactly use the patch?

The patch is for the kernel source.  If you just upgraded your kernel 
using a binary rpm, then all you got was a new precompiled kernel.  As
it is precompiled, and not the source, you cannot patch it.  Learn to
roll your own kernel before trying to continue with XFS.
