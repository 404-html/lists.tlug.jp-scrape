I am using Tokyu.

I recommend that if you are not very sure of what you are doing that you
let them set up a Mac or Windows machine as they do not support Unix
(Japanese machine is best with a Japanese friend, if necessary).

The installer guy orginally balked when seeing the number of machines,
but i told him they wouldn't be connected to the net and winked.

The cable they provide out of the router straight into a FreeBSD box with
2 NICs running NAT and ipfw.  I personally have had few problems.

I do not however download huge amounts of stuff and sit around waiting so
I cannot really say how fast, but it does noticably slow at times.

Good luck,
