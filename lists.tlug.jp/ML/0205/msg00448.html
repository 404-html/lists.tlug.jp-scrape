>> Somewhere around Wed, 1 May 2002 12:45:42 +0900, Tony wrote:
> Hi
> 
> I am using sylpheed and am having some problems.  I want the menus/messages to be displayed in English.  However when I start sylpheed like
> 
> LANG= sylpheed
> 
> I get lots of moji-bake.  The "From" and "Subject" fields display fine but the message body is all garbage.  
> When I start sylpheed like
> 
> LANG=ja_JP.EUC-JP sylpheed
> 
> All works fine, except I want the menus/messages to display in English.  
> Any ideas how to acheive this?

LC_CTYPE=ja_JP.EUC-JP sylpheed

Don't change the LANG value.
