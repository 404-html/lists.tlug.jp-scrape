    Michael> init: timeout opening/writing control channel /dev/initctl

Try kill -USR1 1 first.  That's supposed to reopen initctl.

You could also try SIGINT or SIGWINCH.  If those don't work, SIGHUP
and SIGPWR are last resorts.

See init(8).
