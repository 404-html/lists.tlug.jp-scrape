Ken,
thank you for your information.  ktin1.4 is based on tin1.2.   The 
binary package with PJE is linked to libcurses.so.1 and unfortunately 
won't run on the systems I've used because libcurses.so.1 is fairly
old.  Most systems use ncurses3.4 now.  The PJE packages come with
source code.  Unfortunately, I have not been able to compile the 
package from source.  The patch for ktin is good and offers good 
Japanese support.  Unfortunately, it looks like there has not
been ktin development since 1993.

The development of a unified tin appears to be ongoing.  However, I have
not got it to support Japanese properly yet.
