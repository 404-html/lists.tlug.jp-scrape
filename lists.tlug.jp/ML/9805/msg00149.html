Just as a kind of experience report concerning my previous
question about blind dialing.  I tried ATX3 and it worked
fine (on the 3Com Etherlink III), but interleaving ~ to
force a pause after dialing the 0 (to get out of the
`private branch exchange') and before starting the rest of
the number, didn't work for me.  But due to some vague
recollection of my early modem days, I tried , (a comma)
after the 0 and that worked well.

Thanks for your help,
