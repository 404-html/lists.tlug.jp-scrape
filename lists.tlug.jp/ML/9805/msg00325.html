> ...but where do you get the parts?
In one of the parts shops of Akihabara. If you want a cable
connecting two computers you need two connectors DP-25 (Dsub 25
pin male type ) and two cases for mounting it to a cable. Then
you need a shielded cable with the required number of wires
inside. With that you'd be set. The stuff will cost you about
2000 yen I guess. Good shops are in the corner of the Akihabara
station ( the train station, not the subway station ) at the
southwest exit. There's many small parts shops there where you
can get the necessary stuff.

Radio Garden should have it, too, but he became shitty and
expensive lately. Ten years ago he was a lot better.
