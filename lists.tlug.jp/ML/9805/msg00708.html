Hi All,

Some of you may have heard the speech of myself or my colleague
of Internet Mutual Association of Yokohama (IMASY), a Yokohama-
based non-profit Internet Provider.  We will hold an annual general
meeting on 31 May, at Pan Pacifi Hotel Yokohama, from 13:00.

In the evening after the meeting, we will have a BIG party,
with BINGO taikai - many prizes are already prepared 
(http://www.imasy.or.jp/~fkaji/bingo.html).

Non-members are welcome to join both to the meeting and party.

The idea of non-profit regional ISP is very close to Open Source
code movement (as long as I feel).  We have many Linux and
FreeBSD enthusiasts.

Those who are interested in this "怪しい集団", please feel free
to join.  The details of the meetings are shown at
http://www.imasy.or.jp/~ichiro/meeting/

Seeing you,

Ken
