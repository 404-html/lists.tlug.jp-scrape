 It seems to me that the cause of David's problem is XFree86.3.3.2 or xdm.
>From XFree86.3.3.2 they need to use xdm to start X.

Make .xsession

At the beginning of .xsession write:

#! /bin/bash -login

Then you'll get the original PATH.

An alternative solution is type
'$ source .bash_profile'
after you started X.
