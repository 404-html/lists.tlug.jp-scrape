bad luck, or I have a lot of good luck, but everytime I ask M$ for
support, what annoys me is that they won't stop answering me... They
give me an answer, and I figure that's it... but then, 2 days later,
they ask me how it's going... I thank them for the help and tell them
it's fine, and then 2 days after that, they ask me if I need any more
help with the problem, and I say everything works great, and then the
next day they ask me if I need help with anything else... this is using
the free technical support... although it's nice that they are
interested to know that I am getting along fine, it's equally annoying
to be spammed to death making sure that I got enough support...

that everyone is in it to help the whole effort... which is a reason why
I like working with Linux... of course, there's the stuff that Linux
actually does that I like about it as well though... :D
