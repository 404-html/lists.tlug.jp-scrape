I just sent up a query about the LA-PCI-T card with netboot.  If
that installation ever comes right, I'll be wanting to supply a
Web browser with a Japanese FEP under X to a bunch of X-terms.

Is there a Netscape out there that takes a Jse FEP for input?
The idea is to supply students with something that won't need
explaining, and that won't burn their fingers; I'd rather not
rely on Lynx-jp with a stable of external apps (won't work with
Imagemap sites anyway), or on the Emacs code.

The English browsers can display Jse, but the hotkey menu won't
display a Japanese page title properly, and that's likely to
cause confusion or irritation at some point.

Any advice will be generously appreciated.
