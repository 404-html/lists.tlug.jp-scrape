Reminds me of that old joke. It goes like this.
One day god calls the President of the US, Michail Gorbatchev
and Bill Gates for a conference. He discloses to them that he
intended to end the world soon and leaves it to them to explain
that to the rest of the world's population.

The President of the US calls in a press conference and
declares: "I have good and bad news for you: the good news it
that God definitely exists and the bad that he wants to end the
world."

Michail Gorbatchev calls in a press conference, too: "I have bad
news for you: the first is that God exists - so we definitely
were wrong on this one - and even worse, he wants to end the
world."

Bill Gates also calls in a press conference: "Lots of good news
for us all: God exists - well, we never doubted about this one,
and even better, there is no need to fix the bugs of the new
Windows version anymore. Isn't that great? "

Well, and before you complain about NT in charge of the
'nukes....at least better than Windows 98 in charge of the
'nukes...... In the latter case it would take shiploads of
wonders to help us.....

But serious, the best thing is to get rid of all that 'nuke
stuff as soon as possible.......I'd not even trust Linux and
rock solid, open source applications enough to put that in
charge of 'nukes.
