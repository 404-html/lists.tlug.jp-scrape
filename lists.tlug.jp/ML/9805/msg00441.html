non-graphics-based mail reader... when you get a spam from
'HairyKevin@example.com' advertising the House of Sex, with a big color JPEG
attached, which I assume had something nasty in it, you don't
automatically get it displayed on your screen in the middle of your
office.. Netscape, on the other hand, might get you fired :)
