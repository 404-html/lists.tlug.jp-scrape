On Fri, May 08, 1998 at 07:06:51PM +0900, Jim Schweizer wrote:
> I'm sure this kind of script has been done before, so could
> someone point me to something that will strip only the headers
> out of an mbox file leaving just the contents of the mail. I
> looked through CPAN and didn't see anything. 

I like 'formail' (part of the procmail package) for slicing and dicing
Email.  'formail -Y -I "" -ds <~/.mailbox >~/.newbox' will remove the
headers.
