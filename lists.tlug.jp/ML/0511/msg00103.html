I think it is highly unlikely that that mode is what you want. :-)
As someone said, you should use xrandr to set the rotation, but
unfortunately it seems like many drivers don't support that feature (yet).

According to
http://www.nvnews.net/vbulletin/showthread.php?t=58602
at least the proprietary nvidia drivers does support it, but you
have to explicitly enable it first. (Option "RandRRotation" "on")
