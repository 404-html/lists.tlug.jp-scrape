FWIW, my colleague tells me that there is no GUI menu entry for 
formatting a 720KB 2DD floppy on an XP box, but you can do it 
at the command prompt:

	format /t:80 /n:9 A:

More details are here under "USB Floppy drive YD-8U10":
http://www.yedata.com/support/WinXPcompliance.shtml
