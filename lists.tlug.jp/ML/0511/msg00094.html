[tlug] SuSE 10 at Tokyo PC Users Group Thursday, Nov 10

  • Date: Mon, 7 Nov 2005 09:58:36 +0900
  • From: Jim Tittsler <jwt-tlug@example.com>
  • Subject: [tlug] SuSE 10 at Tokyo PC Users Group Thursday, Nov 10
Newly elected Tokyo PC Users Group President Keith Wilkinson will be demonstrating SuSE 10 at this Thursday's TPC meeting. He will show how the interface can be made familiar to Windows users, how there are options for running Windows applications or move to applications that offer "Office" compatibility, and how you can dual boot in case you aren't sure you are ready to take the leap. Copies of the DVD or CD set will be available at the meeting at cost (see web site for details). 
<http://www.tokyopc.org/>
The meetings are held in the basement meeting hall of Tokyo Union Church on Omotesando. Meetings start at 7PM with the main presentation normally getting started about 7:30. Admission is Y1000 for non-members. 
<http://www.tokyopc.org/faxmessage/#map>
TPC also offers a low volume tpc.unix local newsgroup and mailing list which tends deal with more beginner style questions than pop up on TLUG. 
via newsreader:  news://news.tokyopc.org/tpc.unix
via mailing list:  http://www.tokyopc.org/mailman/listinfo/tpc-unix
via web:  http://www.tokyopc.org/cgi-bin/webnews/newsgroup?G=tpc.unix
