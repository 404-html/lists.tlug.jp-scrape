On Fri, 2 Jun 2000, Subba Rao wrote:

> www             A       10.5.28.11

In the zonefile for yourbogusdomain.com:

www		IN A	10.5.28.11

And in your reverse DNS zone file for 10.5.28:

11		IN PTR	www.yourbogusdomainname.com.

(Be sure not to forget the trailing . there)

Then start/stop named.

You do, of course, have entries for those zone files in your named.conf,
right?  And the machine running httpd is looking to the machine running
bind as its name server?
