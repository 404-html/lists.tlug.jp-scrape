         T L U G - Tokyo Linux Users Group

          *** joint meeting with YLUG ***

Meeting information

  Date: June 16 (Friday)

  Time: 19:00-

  Place: Kawasaki City Takazu Shimin Kaikan
         Meeting Room #6
         (Mizonoguchi Station, Marui Family 12F)

  Topic: Linux Kernel 

All Linux users and supporters of open source code and free software
are invited to attend this Tokyo Linux Users Group (TLUG) meeting.
Membership is open to anyone.  There are no membership dues or entrance
fees (we will accept donations to help cover the room usage costs). 

Thanks to YLUG (Yokohama Linux Users Group), this will be a joint 
meeting to study the Linux Kernel. After the meeting, a "nomikai"
party will be held (place TBD).

*** Important ***

The meeting place has capacity for only 50 people. Registration is
required to reserve a seat. Go to http://www.utage.org/enkai/ and enter
the registration code "kernel000616" (without the quotation marks)
then enter your name, e-mail address and comments (if you have any).
When finished, press the button "touroku" to complete the registration.

For more information about TLUG, access http://tlug.gr.or.jp
About YLUG, http://www.ylug.org

For any instructions or info before the meeting:
    Jonathan Byrne ............... 070-5517-9250 (PHS)
    Alberto Tomita ............... 070-5644-3680 (PHS)

We look forward to seeing you there!
