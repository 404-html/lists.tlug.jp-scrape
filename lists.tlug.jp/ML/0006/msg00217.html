    Tony> cat /proc/parport/0/irq  gives "7". 

I thought I saw "irq = -1" in the log you posted.  Trakker may be
saying "use default" or it may be saying "not irq driven" or it may be
saying "couldn't find an irq".  You may have to set irq for trakker on
the module command line.  see man insmod and man modules.conf

on your system.  ls -l *ft0, please.
