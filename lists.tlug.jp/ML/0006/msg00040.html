Hi

My name is Joachim and I am one of the easyLinux people. 
easyLinux has just been voted No.1 (in front of SUSE and Mandrake) by
one of Germanys leading PC-magazines. 
We claim to be the easiest to install and administer distribution on the
Planet.
 
I am intersted in setting up a branch in Japan to translate easyLinux
and eSamba, etc...
from English to Japanese! It is important to us that everything is
turned into Japanese. Our aim is to provide a product that gives you the
feeling of using a pure Japanese product!
Unfortunately I'm not speaking very good Japanese just yet. But since my
wife is Japanese I think this will change after I move to to your
beautiful country!

I would appreciate if you could send me some of your ideas so I can gain
a better understanding on how to go about this. Also I welcome anybody
who wants join the project!  


Domo arigato gozaimashta!
