> You could also just install a Japanese distro.  As long as you get

ROFLMAO, Stephen... I don't know if I'd necessarily agree with this -
there was a LOT of stuff in TLJ that was broken even with "LANG=C" due to

everything right.  But, some people localize for japanese instead of
internationalize, and if these packages are used, then you have issues.

Chris and the rest of the 1998 TLUG folks set me straight :)
