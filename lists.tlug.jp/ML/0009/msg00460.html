For those joining the impromptu nomikai tonight but who might
not know how to get to The Fiddler, here are maps.

First, go to Takadanoba Station, on the Yamanote Line, or
Nanboku Line (subway).  One other line (Tobu or Seibu, not
sure which) also goes there:

http://www.mapion.co.jp/cgi/m?no=1091988500196750000

On the above map, you can see a sort of traffic circle that
opens onto Waseda-dori, and the Nanboku line station is
right in front of it (the white one).  Cross Waseda-dori
and turn right, so that you are walking with JR Takadanoba
station at your back. 

Go straight down Waseda-dori for a few hundreds meters, and
the Fiddler will be on your left.  It's on the B1F of its
building and has a sign out on the sidewalk with a picture
of a person playing a fiddle.  It's just before the 
intersection with Meiji-dori (this is a major intersection),
so if you get all the way to the corner of Meiji-dori, 
turn around and go back a little bit (20 meters?).

On this next map, you can see the intersecion of Waseda-dori
and Meiji-dori.  The Fiddler is just before the corner where
you can see Sanwa Bank （三和）and is in the next building
after Sanwa Bank:

http://www.mapion.co.jp/cgi/m?no=1091989000196750000


Here is a small-scale English map from The Fiddler's web site,
showing Sanwa Bank and The Fiddler, with reduced detail:

http://www.thefiddler.com/map.html

The phone number there is 03-3204-2698, if you get lost.  They
speak English.

Jonathan
