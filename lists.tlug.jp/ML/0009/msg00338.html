I'm actually a bit concerned with the lifetime of tapes.  Had a DAT drive
some years ago and found the tape lifetime to be abismal.  In that sense I
feel more comfortable with media such as CDs and DVDs (although capacity and
write speeds become an issue).

In this regard, has anyone had experience with DVD-RAM on linux?   There is
a new even higher density DVD-RAM drive/disk which should be available on
the market soon (I think giving something on the order of 7GB per side).
