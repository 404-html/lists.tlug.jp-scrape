Yesterday I attempted to use the SNMP remote disk monitoring facility of the
"mon" package from the Debian (potato) distribution.  It didn't work,
because of a version mismatch between the relevant script in "mon" and the
SNMP version shipped with this Debian distribution.

The mismatch didn't turn out to be a big deal once I figured out what was
going on, but since that took a full day to accomplish, the result might
save someone some time.

If anyone wants the patch, drop me a note.  When I get around to it, I'll
put it into the Debian hopper and report the problem to the maintainer of
"mon".
