>  Which side is Takashimaya and Shinjuku Gyoen?

The different department store names can get confusing - especially
when they're turning into discount electronics stores!  (Sogo at
Yurakucho now a Bic Camera & Mitsukoshi in Kichijoji now a Yodobashi
Camera, etc.)

I think the department stores in Shinjuku are:

East Exit: Isetan, Mitsukoshi
West Exit: Keio, Odakyu (part of which is a Bic Camera)
South Exit: Takashimaya, Tokyu Hands

The closest exit for Shinjuku Gyoen would be either the Southeast Exit
or the New South Exit.

Here's a good map of the exits:
http://www.jreast.co.jp/e/stations/e866.html
