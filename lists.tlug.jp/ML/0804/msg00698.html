I don't know about the one you're talking about but I do know that it was
relatively painless getting an Asus WL-138G V2 working in Slackware 12 with
ndiswrapper and wpa_supplicant. I simply followed instructions given in the
source packages and got the box connected using WPA-PSK/TPSK encryption in
about 10 minutes flat after installing the card physically.
