On 31/03/2008, Attila Kinali <attila@example.com> wrote:

>  But to state the obvious, latex isn't the optimal tool
>  for something like this as latex focus is one something
>  totaly different than making exactly placed lines on a
>  sheet of paper.

Yes and no. LaTeX's stated goal is to make computerised typesetting
work well. While LaTeX typically does not need to control placement by
the millimetre (micrometre?), it provides ways to do this. Granted,
doing that is not exactly straight-forward. :)

The way to go here might actually be to use ImageMagick or some other
tool to produce a gridded image, instead of mucking with typesetting
in LaTeX or PostScript.
