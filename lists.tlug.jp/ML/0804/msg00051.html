There's nothing strange about that. That's how usb-storage is supposed to
work - it presents USB-connected storage devices to the kernel as SCSI
disks whether those storage devices are external hard drives, pen drives or
card readers (they're both exactly the same from the POV of the electronics
in them), ZIP drives etc...
