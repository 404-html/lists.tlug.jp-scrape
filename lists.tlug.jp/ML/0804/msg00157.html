Thanks for response. I also run on debian testing system. I have
searched for the plugin and it is not available through the official
czech mirror which I happen to use. 

There is one package from debian-multimedia.org: flashplayer-mozilla
Unfortunately, it depends on nspluginwrapper and thus further software
is needed. In this case, I would rather find a way how to sync audio and
video in resulting .avi files or investigate the reason for loosing the
sync in the first place. 

I use these primitive commands (which I have found somewhere on the net):
wget http://address.to/get/file.flv
mencoder -oac copy -ovc lavc -o file.avi file.flv

Downloading and converting .flv is not a big problem, since I do not
watch many movies and I have enough free cpu time to use for conversion.
