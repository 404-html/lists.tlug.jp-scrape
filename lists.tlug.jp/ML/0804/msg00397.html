2008 TLUG Officer Elections
-----------------------------

We will be holding the elections for TLUG officers at the May 10th
technical meeting. The positions are as follows.

* President
* Vice President
* Treasurer

If anyone is interested in challenging the incumbents[1], please
post to the tlug-admin mailing list indicating which position by May
3rd. Questions about the Officer positions can be addressed as a
reply to this email on the tlug-admin list. Alternatively you may
contact the incumbent officers directly.

Edward Middleton

1. http://www.tlug.jp/wiki/TLUG:Organization
