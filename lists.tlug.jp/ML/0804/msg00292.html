MS Office prior to 2007 does not support OOXML, i.e. it only supports
binary format.

XML-ZIP is a good format IMO. We work with them easier because all
we have to do is uncompress and parse the XML files.

>  WYSIWYG is not a solution, it is the problem,
I don't think so. It is revolution and one of the solution.
The fact is that this solution is best or good depends on your point of views.

I wrote my papers in Latex because I can't get the Math formula
displayed as I wanted with MS Word. But it took me a lot of time to learn Latex.

Now I am an engineer and everyone in my company uses MS Word (me too).
Because it is *productive* ( means: easy to get the job done, and
everyones has MS Word installed ).
