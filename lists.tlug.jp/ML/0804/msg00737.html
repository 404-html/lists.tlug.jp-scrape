Since we're in a joke/flame thread I must disagree.  That license
is self-contradictory:

> Everyone is permitted to copy and distribute verbatim or
> modified copies of this license document, and changing it is
> allowed as long as the name is changed.

"Public Domain".

international license to be the equivalent of US Public Domain -
"Do what the fuck you want to" everywhere?
