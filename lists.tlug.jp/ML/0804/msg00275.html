On 10/04/2008, Edward Middleton <emiddleton@example.com> wrote:

>  It is however a judgment call. Things like AJAX or flash have similar
> considerations.  Do you go for
>
>  1. lowest common denominator with universal client coverage.
>  2. more functional for most users with problems for some.
>  3. more functional for all with increased development cost.
>
>  The decision will vary base on the situation.

Or:

4. Rich internal model with functionality exposed to users on a
per-device basis.

(4) wins in every case I can think of, because you can basically start
it out like (1), but ramp up to (3) while keeping your development
costs predictable.
