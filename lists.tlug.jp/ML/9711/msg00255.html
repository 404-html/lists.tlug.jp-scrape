On Fri, 28 Nov 1997, Michael Chiu wrote:

> Pardon my ignorance, but I have been reading these messages about "Yutex",
> but I have no idea what is going on...  What exactly is Yutex?

Yutex is an editor written by Gaspar Sinai.  It has basic
editing capability and excellent encoding translation
capabilities.  It is also capable of displaying UTF-8
characters.  It is available in the incoming section
of sunsite.unc.edu.
