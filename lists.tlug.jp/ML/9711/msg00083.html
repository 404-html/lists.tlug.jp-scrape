Hi,
an article in the LJ and OSF web-site indicate that MkLinux 
(Linux built around Mach microkernel) is happily running
on PA-RISC hardware is available for download now, for free.
http://www.osf.org/mall/os/pa-mklinux/hpuxmklinux.html

Hewlett Packard, makers of the PA-RISC chip found in their
UNIX workstations and servers, did that port of Linux around
the MkLinux kernel that was started by Apple.  

I've never tried this yet, but it looks interesting.
