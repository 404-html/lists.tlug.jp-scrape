Hi TLUG folks,

Japanese PostgreSQL community is going to hold the 5th PostgreSQL
Unconference Tokyo on May 30th.

PostgreSQLアンカンファレンス＠東京（2015/5/30）
https://atnd.org/events/64543

If you are interested in PostgreSQL or database technology,
it's a good chance to learn them. We will have lots of short talks
(maybe up to 20 talks) there, 10-15 minutes for each.

As many of you may know, "unconference" is highly interactive
conference, and it's good way not only for studying technology,
but also knowing people and community.

In our unconference, most of talks are given in Japanese, but if you
want to share some idea or experience of PostgreSQL in the community,
we also welcome English-speaking people. Many PostgreSQL developers
and users in Japan are able to have conversation in English.

So, please join us, and learn technology from the community people
with some beer! :)

Regards,
