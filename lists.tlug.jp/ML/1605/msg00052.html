relaying a message from the Linux Foundation:

Dear Tokyo Linux Users Group

The Linux Foundation would like to offer a special 10% discount
to TLUG members for the "LinuxCon + ContainerCon Japan 2016".

If you are interested in attending, please email your information
(name and company or group) to [notake (at) linuxfoundation.org] 
by June 30. We will then send a discount code within a few days.

◇ About LinuxCon + ContainerCon Japan 2016 ◇

July 13-15, 2016 at Chinzan-so Hotel and Conference Center, Tokyo, Japan

Keynote speakers include:
* Chris Aniszczyk, VP of strategic and developer programs at The Linux
Foundation, will discuss the latest innovations in cloud native and
container technologies.

*Brian Behlendorf, Executive Director of the Hyperledger Project, will give
an overview of Hyperledger and growing importance of OpenChain technologies.

*Ibrahim Haddad, VP of R&D and Head of the open source group at Samsung
Research America will talk about how enterprise companies can master
open source software.

*Dirk Hohndel, chief Linux and open source technologist at Intel in a
fireside chat with Linux creator Linus Torvalds.

*Linux kernel developer panel moderated by Linux Foundation Fellow Greg
Kroah-Hartman.

*David Marr, VP, legal at Qualcomm will talk about the
importance of an ecosystem of open source software compliance.

*Gil Yehuda, Senior Director of open source, standards at Yahoo will share
his thoughts on the value of an open source program office.
Linux Foundation Executive Director Jim Zemlin will discuss the state of
open source.

Please see schedule below and find out more!
http://events.linuxfoundation.org/events/linuxcon-japan/program/schedule

Attendee & Student Registration Fees:
-Early Registration Fee: US$350 (through May 31, 2016)
-Standard Registration Fee: US$400 (June 1, 2016 - June 22, 2016)
-Late Registration Fee: US$475 (June 23, 2016 - Event)
-Student Registration Fee: US$125

Please feel free to share this invitation with your members.

Thank you very much!
The Linux Foundation
