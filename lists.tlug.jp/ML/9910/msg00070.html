I thought I would mention that the Taichung 
Linux Users Group is having a Linux/FreeBSD 
InstallFest next weekend. Only weeks ago 
the whole island was rocked with one of the 
largest earthquakes we have seen in recent 
history and now, someone has found the 
time, space, power and physical energy for 
an open source software event. How to describe 
this: resiliency?
