Jens-Ulrik Petersen writes:
 > Simon Cozens <simon@example.com> writes:
 > 
 > > Omega is up and running, and Omega *rocks*. It's lovely, it's
 > > beautiful, it works extremely well - once you've got it properly
 > > installed, of course.
 >
 > <http://www.gutenberg.eu.org/omega/> 
 > 
 > Interesting.  Though I didn't seem any mention of Japanese support.
 
Well, according to the docs I've read, the developers explicitly
intend to support CJK, including vertical text, and I think furigana
also. And they have a scheme to develop complete (i.e., including all
the public characters) Unicode fonts. But they are mainly interested
in Greek/Arabic/Middle Eastern/North African languages, so my sense is 
that it will be up to people who use East Asian languages and like
Omega to develop the necessary support files for it.
