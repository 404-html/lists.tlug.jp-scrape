Ok, assume we have an equation:

        lpd         sendmail
      -------  =  -----------
        lprm           x


Solve for x, please.

And please don't tell me it's (sendmail * lprm) / lpd  :)

Basically I need a program that can remove entries from the mail queue.
We have some mailing lists with bad addresses and I want to get them out
of the queue.
