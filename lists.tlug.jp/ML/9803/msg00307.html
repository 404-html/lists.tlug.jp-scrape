So... with our bandwidth limitations here, we'd like to put the tlug FTP
files up on ftp.pht.com instead of locally here in Japan, since
ftp.pht.com has 10 mbit/sec of bandwidth... we could put some files on the
tlug server and then have ftp.pht.com mirror them over once a week/every
couple days/whatever, though.

What do you think?  I would set up an account on ftp.pht.com for tlug
which basically would be limited to people with root access on the tlug
server (the account would be for putting files up, etc), and downloads
would be via anon-ftp.
