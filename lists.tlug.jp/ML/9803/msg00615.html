interesting point - we got some off-brand SB-16-compat PnP cards in here.
Running turbosoundconfig on one without the --noprobe option causes a
segfault (whereas it works fine on a REAL SB-16 PnP).  Configuring the pnp
stuff manually with isapnptools or turbopnp, however, worked, and then I
ran turbosoundconfig with --noprobe, set up the thing as a SB16 (with the
modular sound thing in 2.0.33), and it worked.

Shouldn't any card with an OPL/3 FM be able to do SB FM stuff, though?  Or
is the compatibility issue with the DSP part of the soundblaster?
