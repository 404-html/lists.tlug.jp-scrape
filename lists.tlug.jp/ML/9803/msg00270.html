Hi All,

It was another great gathering tonight (last night).
I believe Shishamo is not pregnant, but someone told
me it is definetely pregnant.

Some 30 people came to Tengu, the very sophisticated
"nomi" place near Tokyo Station and enjoyed "drink",
"eat" and "chat" on Linux.

Ken Itsumi was carrying Libretto mini-note computer with
him, with fully linux loaded, but he preferred to use the
nice HP100LX with his E-J/J-E dictionary loaded, which
worked excellent in the Nomi chat.

That dict gave us a lot -- "Katsuo" is bonito, "nishin" is
hering, and "shishamo" is not listed.

...

We settled the bill and tried the next stop, "Kita No Kazoku"
as usual, but it was alredy closed unfortunately.  I personally
could catch the last but one train back home to Zushi.

No picture tonight.

See you again all on 14 April.


Regards,
