It should work if you have libc.so.6 on your system.  You did
an ldconfig -v   as root right?

At the shell prompt, can you execute the bash shell...

  bash$ /bin/bash

do you get an error message?   Don't log out yet.   If you do

can you login?   If you can't login, you're going to encounter
difficulties when you reboot.
