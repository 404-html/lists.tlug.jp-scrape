Hallo everyone, 

sorry for disturbing you once again with my little 
audio device problem. 

I used pnp tools to detect my audio device and the 
output of pnpdump tells me the presence of an audio 
device Yamaha. I wrote the issapns.conf file according 
to the informations found in W95. 

Then I installed the module sound with "insmod sound". 

In the file "/dev/sndstat" I can read that 
the Sound Blaster is configured and detected 
(no parenthesis), but the section Audio device 
is empty. 

Instead, if I use compile the kernel without pnptols, 
the audio device is not detected.

Now, with the command dmesg I see that the last line 
gives an error "sb: interrupt on IRQ7"..

I'm sure that I used the same settings as DOS and the IRQ is 
5 and not 7. 

May it be different on Linux? Maybe my audio device is not 
supported at all...
