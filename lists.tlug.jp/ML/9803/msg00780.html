Hi,

Last time I talked about Net Day issue, I might unintensionally
have started politics.
So this time I like merely to bring your attention to April issue
of Linux Journal in which the author of the Cathedral and the Bazaar
contributed the article called "Helping Netscape Make History".

In the article, he explains open source concept and three kinds of
business according to open source concept:

1)the Cygnus/RedHat/BSDI model.
2)the Netscape Model.
3)the Hardware store model.

So following the article's argument, I might have said in 
my last Net Day comment that:
1) I prefer 1) model to 2) model.
2) Net Day thing can be 3) model.

Though I was not sure of it then.
But, I am sure right now.

Regards,
Kei.
