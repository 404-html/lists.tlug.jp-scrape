Does anybody know a source for some *short* documentation of Canna?   The
stuff that comes with it is all .tex stuff, and not in English, I'm fairly
certain:  the accompanying Japanese readme file says that you should use
Jlatex to print it and that it's 200 pages.  Seems a bit of overkill for my
needs :-)  All inquiring minds like mine want  to know are the key bindings
for Kinput2 and Canna.

Craig's excellent info pages have the basic ones for Wnn and Kinput2, but I
couldn't find anything for Canna.  BTW, the link to UCSD's info on Canna is
dead.  Particularly, I want to know what key will fix the kanji and toss
them to the client.  The intelligent choice (just hit enter) seems to have
been by-passed by the creators of Canna in favor of something obscure.  I've
pounded my keyboard trying all kinds of combinations in a vain search, too.

Thanks to great input from all sides, I now have Kinput2 talking to Canna
OK, now I just need to get it to keep my kanji after converting them (at
least that does work off of the space bar)  :-)

Thanks,
