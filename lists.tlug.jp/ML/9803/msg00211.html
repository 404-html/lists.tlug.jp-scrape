  Information on the power supply which I'm using (I hope Steve Carter 
  won't mind too much if I cut-and-paste from the email I sent to him
  earlier)...

  There's one shop in Akihabara which has a switched-mode 
  power supply for 500-yen which is perfect for the job. I think
  the name of the shop is Akizuki and it's at:-
 
        http://www.impress.co.jp/akibamap/map/map_b2.html
 
  The gold-ish coloured box, almost dead centre of the map. It has an
  arrow marked "C Box".
 
  The power supply is about six inches long and an inch and a half in
  height and width. It has a nasty little eight pin connector at one
  end which you probably should remove (solder cables directly to the
  pads on the PCB instead). It comes complete with a mains cable and
  on/off switch (bargain!).

  Piccie at:- http://www2.gol.com/gaijin
