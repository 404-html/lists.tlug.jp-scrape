> Is X the biggest program in the world or what?  I'm building libc5 RPMs
> for XFree 3.3.2, and it's taking 3 hours :)

_and_ the linkkit _and_ the loadable module stuff _and_ static libraries,

3.3 branch for at least six months ;)
