   T L U G   M A R C H  1 1   N O M I K A I
   ****************************************

Date: 	Wednesday, March 11, 1998
Time: 	19:30 inside of Tengu 
Place: 	Tengu TokyoEkiMae
	Chuo-ku, Kyobashi 1-1-6, EchiZenYa Bld. B1/B2 
	Phone: 03-3275-3691
Events: drink and eat (primarily a social activity)
Costs: 	will be divided up among participants, see 
	http://www.twics.com/~tlug/meeting.html for
	information on costs of previous events

If you're coming, send e-mail to craig@example.com  If you want to come and
you don't send an e-mail, it is okay.  However, we will have to squeeze
in.  See the home page http://www.twics.com/~tlug/9801/Ar0100075.jpg
for a picture of just how crowded the last event was.  

Tengu is about 5 minutes away from the Yaesu Chuo exit of the JR Tokyo
train station.  It is on the right hand side of Yaesu douri if you 
are walking out of the station.   If you are walking out of 
Yaesu exit, turn the map upside down.  Note that Starbucks
is underground and Tai Kai Rou is entirely in Japanese Kanji.
Look for the red demon with the big nose.  You will not
be able to see Starbucks from above ground.  I know, the
map should have been in VRML.  ;-)
