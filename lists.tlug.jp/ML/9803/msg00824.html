Hi,
This was the case until the last release of XFree. (Since the date of your
Xserver
is Aug 97, it's probably an older version.) In the new version (3.3.2) the
server is no 
longer SUID, but needs Xwrapper to get started. 
I installed 3.3.2 over a RedHat 5.0 distribution and had no problems
starting X with
the startx script  (which is included in the XFree release). Other or older
startup scripts
may have problems, because they are calling X directly instead of the
xwrapper, so
perhaps a look at the original startx script (released with the server) may
help.
I'm only at a Win95 machine at the moment, so I'm just guessing what could
be the problem.
Hope that helps anyway,
