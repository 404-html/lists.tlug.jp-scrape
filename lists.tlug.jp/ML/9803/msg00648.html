>You have to make a *directory* to mount a partition?!  Does this mean that
>UNIX/Linux don't really understand partitions as different physical units,
>but rather see them as being a single directory in one big file system?
>

You need a directory *entry* to mount a partition.  If you make
/w95 directory and mount your vfat partion to it, you will find all
the directories of your vfat partion under it.  Or, try

$ mount

then you will find how your Linux partions are mounted to your / directory.
