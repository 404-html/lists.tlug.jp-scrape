Could anyone confirm that 219.127.92.0/26 is the only netblock from which
TLUG mail is going to come?

I'm getting increasing amounts of spam from Japan now and it's time to
blacklist the country along with China, Korea, Hong Kong, Taiwan and a few
other areas (see http://www.bonivet.net/dnsbl/lookup.php?ip=127.0.0.2)

Obviously, I don't want to lock out hikari.tlug.jp.

BTW, you want to adjust the hostname that MTA gives during the SMTP dialog.
It announces itself as hikari.tlug.gr.jp but rDNS says hikari.tlug.jp. More
and more networks are going to start rejecting mail from servers which
aren't RFC1912-compliant.
