"~" = $Home

>>Wait!  Wait!  Wait!  I found it!  Just for other newbie bozos

user@example.com:~> $ grep ^TempDir ~/.audacity

Wait... "$" means "user@example.com:~>" then?  Let's see....

user@example.com:~> grep ^TempDir ~/.audacity
TempDir=/tmp/audacity1.2-user

Ah!  (Dim light bulb goes off above bozo Lyle's head....)
