but it's unlikely that they're familiar with every little bit.  You
could try a more appropriate list, or you could try again here, but if

(1) explain where the specification "swap two maps representing the
same region" came from---a comment, a detailed spec document, or your
best opinion as to what the code is supposed to do

pseudo-code

at.  There are thousands of functions in the kernel, even somebody who
has studied sanitize_e820_map() might have trouble guessing where it
was (and it might have moved, even to a different file, since they last
looked at it).
