Be creative. 

To get attention do something _really_ unusual, like send a 
letter on paper in an envelope. How do you feel when you get a 
real letter written by real person that makes you think? 
A well crafted paper letter really stands out, especially when 
written to a specific person and caters to what that particular 
cares about. 

How many million emails does Google or Yahoo get each day 
about their service? I would guess that most are from 
clueless users complaining about things stupid things. 
So your clueful email gets lost in the noise. Email is so 
easy to blow off, so play the services against each other. 

You can also stir up publicity on blogs and the like.
