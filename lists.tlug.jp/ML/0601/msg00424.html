On 01/18/2006 09:38 PM, Lyle (Hiroshi) Saxon wrote:
> I have now tried Audacity, I can import files, I can convert them to 
> MP3, Wav or ogg, but what I can't do and what I need to do is to edit 
> out a ten-twenty second clip of a song.  The Save,Cut, Paste options are 
> not active.

Did you click and "drag out" part of an audio track to select it?  After 
selecting the part of the track you want, you can use the "Export 
Selection as xxx..." menu item to just make a copy of that portion of 
the track.  (Alternatively you can use 'trim' to delete the rest of the 
track.)

<http://audacity.sourceforge.net/help/>
<http://audacity.sourceforge.net/manual-1.2/tutorial_common_ed_1.html>
