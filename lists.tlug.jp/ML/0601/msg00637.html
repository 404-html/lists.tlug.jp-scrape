I wrote:

> Here's how you can turn off HTML in the email that your email 
> program sends: 
> 
>    http://www.expita.com/nomime.html#programs

Since most email programs render HTML, that leaves many people 
vulnerable to attack when viewing HTML email. Of course, using 
plain text email programs would greatly reduce the vulnerability 
to malicious email. What GUI plain text email programs 
(preferably open-source) are there for Windows? 

I tried Sylpheed a few years ago on a customer's Windows box, 
but it did not work. Hopefully it has improved: 

   http://sylpheed.good-day.net/ja/download.html#win32
