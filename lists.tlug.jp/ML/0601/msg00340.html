Godwin wrote:

> I find it difficult to believe that one can go from "ltmodem source code for
> 2.6.x kernels" to "Threaded email readers" without there having been a
> radical change in the message content...

You haven't been watching the threads that I participate in. :-)

Consider the change from: 
   Re: [tlug] Nasty Problem: searching for strings that span newlines [1]
to: 
   [tlug] Following Threads in Email [2]
by yours truly. 

It's OK to have a radical change in the message content in a thread, 
as long as the replier is actually replying to _something_ in (or about) 
that which they are replying to. 

If Ian had started a new thread, it would have broken the reference 
to what he was taking exception to. His continuation of the thread 
was appropriate. My changing the subject was also appropriate.
