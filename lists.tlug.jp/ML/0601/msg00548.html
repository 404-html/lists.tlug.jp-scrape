> click on a download in firefox, I get either save to disk, or, open with

> choose open with other, I get starting... in the download window. What
> am I missing with this? I've learnt, that you can also open the
> downloaded .bittorrent file with your client and then start the download

I'm not sure about the command for your distribution, but with FreeBSD
the actual commands would be either bittorrent-console,
bittorrent-curses or bittorrent (which opens up a gui.)

I think I once got it to start from a browser, but usually, I just
download the torrent file and type

bittorrent <filename>

and it starts. 

There's a few bittorrent tutorials around.   

Oddly enough, though it's supposed to be faster, I've always found it
slower than standard downloads of ISOs.  

You might have to open something in your firewall to work, although I'm
not positive about that either.
