I did this:

1) Imported a 4-minute wav file.

2) I found the start point I wanted by clicking in the wave display, 
clicking on play, stopping and clicking a little further forward or 
back, clicking on play again, etc., until I found exactly where I wanted 
the selection to start.  This left a thin black line at that point and 
every click on Play started the song from the exact same point.

3) I clicked on the record button and as it played, both the wave 
display and the empty white display below were shaded as it played.  I 
clicked on stop at the desired end point.

Now - I would like to click on "Export Selection as..." but all of those
