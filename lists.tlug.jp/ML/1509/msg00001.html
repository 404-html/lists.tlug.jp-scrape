Dear TLUGers,

I am happy to announce the release of Volume One of the first issue of
the Journal of the Japanese Association of Digital Humanities (JJADH),
published online on JSTAGE. The URL is

https://www.jstage.jst.go.jp/browse/jjadh

This first volume contains eight articles based on presentations from
the JADH conferences in 2012 and 2013.

The CFP for Volume Two will be released shortly.

Regards,

Charles Muller
Editor-in-Chief, JJADH
