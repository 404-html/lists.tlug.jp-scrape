I don't have much to add to the recent suggestions; the best I can do
is to go back to your original message and confirm that I use Ubuntu
13.10 and Firefox 27 on a few computers (about 4?) and all are fine
with gmail.  Fine with reading and writing e-mails, uploading
attachments, and even uploading files go Google Drive.  As for your
comment to Ariel about it being related to Japanese IME / fonts, I
also have Japanese installed and also have no problems.

As others have suggested, the problem could be with something specific
to your user profile or with plugins which you've installed.  If you
aren't willing to touch that, then create a new user account and use
its Firefox to see if it is a profile problem or a system problem.

I think Ariel has a point.  If you aren't willing to consider removing
Firefox information, as others here have suggested, then you'll
probably have better luck posing your question to a Firefox forum or
maybe AskUbuntu -- users there might be able to give you suggestions
which won't disturb your account so much...

Ray
