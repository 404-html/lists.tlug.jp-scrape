       =========================================================

      Date:     December 22 (**THURSDAY**)
      Time:     19:30-21:30

      Location: Town Cryer Hibiya
      Address:  Hibiya Central Bldg B1
                1-2-9 Nishishinbashi, Minato-ku, Tokyo
      Map:      http://www.towncryer.jp/TChibiya.html

      Cost:     pay-as-you-go
      Menu:     http://r.gnavi.co.jp/b664201/menu3.htm
                http://r.gnavi.co.jp/b664201/menu4.htm
