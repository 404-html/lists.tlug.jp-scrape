Check for a missing period at the end of secondary.server.  It sounds like
secondary.server is being interpreted as a hostname in primary.domain,
rather than an FQDN of its own.  Thus it gets NXDOMAIN when it tries
to find secondary.server.

If you want to mail a copy of the zone file to me privately, I'd be happy
to look at it (I'm leaving the house soon and won't be back for a few
hours, so I'll get back to you this eveningi).
