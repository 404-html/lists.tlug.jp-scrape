From today's edition of LWN:

   Announcing...Mountain View Data. A company called Mountain View Data
   announced its existence this week. The first thing that catches the  
   attention with this company is that its principals are Cliff Miller
   and Iris Miller, the founders of TurboLinux, along with Peter Braam, 
   the designer behind the Coda and Intermezzo filesystems.
   
   The Millers, of course, have been easing out of their roles in
   TurboLinux for a while. Large venture investments have a way of
   pushing aside a company's founders in favor of more presentable (to
   investors) executives. So they are off to Mountain View, which gives  
   them a chance to create another successful open source company.
