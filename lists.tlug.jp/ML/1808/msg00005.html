I have, free to anybody who wants it, a Mitsubishi LVP-HC7000
projector. It's a 1080p theatre projector, so very quiet but not
hugely bright (though it does work ok in a normally lit room). It's
also larger than avarge, about 40 x 40 x 20 cm. The bulb is new a good
for at least 2000 hours on econ mode or something like 1200 or more on
regular ('bright') mode. It has the usual assortment of inputs (HDMI,
component, composite, DSub-15/VGA) and comes with the remote control.

There's a caveat, of course: there's an issue with the green LCD panel
and so a bright, particularly white background will display somewhat
purplish blotches all over the screen. (It's much less noticable if
you're displaying, e.g., colored text on a black background.)
