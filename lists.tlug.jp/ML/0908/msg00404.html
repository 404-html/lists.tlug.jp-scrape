As usual, this month's meeting of TSAC, a group for functional
programmers, will meet at the offices of Starling Software on Thursday,
August 27th at 7:30 p.m. Curt Sampson will be presenting "Haskell in
the Real World," a paper he'll be giving next week at the International
Conference on Functional Programming.

Further information is available from the TSAC web page:

  http://www.starling-software.com/en/tsac.html

And directions to get to Starling Software's office are here:

  http://www.starling-software.com/en/access.html

Everyone interested in functional programming is welcome, regardless of
your level of knowledge.
