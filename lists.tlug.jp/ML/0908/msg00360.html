Speaking of security, is there some simple way to convince the linux
kernel to let a non-root application bind to a port under 1024? This
has been one of my biggest security nightmares when switching from BSD

do this. Currently I'm getting around this with a NAT translation, but
that's just one more thing to silently fail.
