On 2009-08-26 14:42 +0900 (Wed), Stephen J. Turnbull wrote:

> Ulrike says that she is listed as the "owner".  What that means I
> don't know, there are typically several different kinds of contact
> (Administrative, Technical at least).

Admin, technical and billing are just "contacts"; there is also an owner
(or "registrant") of a domain who has the ultimate real world authority
over it. The contacts can change various information about the domain,
but only the owner can change who the owner is.
