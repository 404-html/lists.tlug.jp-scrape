To check openssh is installed by default or not on this Cent OS 5.2 installer, you need to select any group on the right pane and click the "Optional packages" button 
and the openssh package is ticked or not. (The tick box is ticked or empty.)

I think you can check it in the group "Servers/Network Server" on 5.2.
