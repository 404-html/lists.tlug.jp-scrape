Installed  SuSE 8.2 and using KDE, check qtconfig first.
You don't need to be 'root' to do this. Just type

 qtconfig

in your terminal.
check bottom of 'interface' section,

'XIM input Style' should be 'over the spot'. Then save and exit.

start 'kate' or other KDE editor.
