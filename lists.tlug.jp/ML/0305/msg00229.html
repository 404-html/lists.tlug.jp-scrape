>>I was told adding a CDROM (or DVD) as primary slave would slow down the

> 
> That is correct  ... IDE/ATA does not allow simultaneous

True, but this relates to access on the same IDE channel no? I was 
always under the impression that two disks on two different IDE/ATA 
channels can process requests simultaneously on most motherboards. So 
that the "proper" setup for a PC with one HDD and one CD-ROM (and two 
IDE channels (controllers?)) was to put the HDD and CD-ROM on different 
channels to avoid perfomance hits.
