Redhat supplies both OpenMotif 2.2 and OpenMotif 2.1 in packages
that do not conflict.  You should install openmotif21-* from your
distribution CDs (or your favorite Redhat mirror).

When an old library is supplied for backward compatibility, by
convention its version number is appended to the end of the
package name.  That is why you will see Redhat
openmotif-2.2.2-* and openmotif21-2.1.30* packages, which can
both be installed.
