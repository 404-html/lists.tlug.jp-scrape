  Hi,

  This is kind of an unimportant little detail, but it bothers me.
In the headers of all my outgoing mail is the line:

X-Authentication-Warning: tangrending: denismcm owned process doing -bs

 tangrending (=tojinmachi) is the hostname here, and denismcm is my
usual login.  It seems really unnecessary for this line to go out with
every piece of mail I send (besides it sounds like I'm doing something
a little underhanded).  The -bs option to sendmail exists,

-bs         Use the SMTP protocol as described in RFC821 on standard in-
	  put and output. 

but neither the manpage nor the sendmail.cf file have anything on
authentication.   

  Anyone know how to tame the beast?

Dennis McMurchy,
