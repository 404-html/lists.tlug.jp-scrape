Steve:

> What's great about libc6, anyway?  Is it worth changing?

IMHO the most important difference is that Ulrich Drepper is
responsible for it.

their own way; libc6 is where Ulrich pulled it back together into the
single glibc if I understand correctly.

up branching off like this. :-/
