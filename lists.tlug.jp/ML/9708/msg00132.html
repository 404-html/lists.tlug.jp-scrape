% 
% I'm not sure what the "8859_1" means.  Does anyone know?
% 

   ISO encoding 8859_1, usually known as "Western English" or
   "Latin-1", as opposed to 8859_2, the encoding for "European
   English". The latter includes codes for umlaut, cedilla, acute
   and friends. Check out the X11 fonts directory (encoding).
