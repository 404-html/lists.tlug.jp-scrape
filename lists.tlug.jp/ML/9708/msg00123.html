Hi all,

Here are some links I dug up about the anti-spam movement.

Enjoy!

<http://spam.abuse.net>  Probably the best anti-spam site. 
Level- headed and technically impeccable.  Their blocking info
is at <http://spam.abuse.net/tools/mailblock.html>

<http://www.nags.org>  Netizens Against Gratuitous Spamming (an
acronym I find redundant; also, it seems to be the work of one
person).  Has a fairly thorough spam filter for Unix.

<http://www.best.com/~ariel/nospam/>  Another free spam bouncer
for Unix.

<http://digital.net/~gandalf/spamfaq.html>  How to figure out
bogus mail and news headers.


And some other interesting sites:

<http://www.jmls.edu/cyber/statutes/email/>  State anti-spam laws

<http://www.cybernothing.org/faqs/net-abuse-faq.html>  The
mother of all spam FAQs.
