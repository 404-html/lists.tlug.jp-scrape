Hi,
anyone have any preferences for location for 
the TLUG October 11th meeting?  If not, I propose that we
set it for 12:30 meeting at Tokyo station Yaesu chuo 
kaisatsu guchi, eat in the basement, then have the primary
location at Joe Marchak's HSBC office.  If Joe is sick
or has an emergency, we need a backup site.  Can we
get Andy Howell at Tibco to be the backup?  If not, there
might be other places we can walk to from Tokyo station.

I would like to set the meeting place now, so that notices
to Debian-JP, fj.os.linux, and others can have all the information
that potential members need to attend.  Also, we can do
standard press release format information about the group to
Tokyo PC, Computing Japan, International Computer Association.  
We can also send some of the SI houses like
Fusion and EDS a fax.   

Joe spoke to me at the last meeting and he said that he wanted
to make the group bigger and to get stronger Japanese community
involvement.  I am trying to give my support to Joe in this
awesome crusade.  

In addition to the Debian-JP project, there are the Japanese
Extension project and the Japanese RPM project.  Does anyone
know the mailing list address for these?  Thinking, about
it, we should just invite all the JLUG people in Tokyo to the next
meeting. 


Regards,
Craig
