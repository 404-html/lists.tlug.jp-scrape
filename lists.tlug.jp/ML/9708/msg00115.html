I use a very low-tech approach, and have seen the x-rated spam mail
drop to zero, and there is little other trash coming in these days.
Apparently lots of these companies are watching email addresses from
the java cookies and also usenet postings. I just put a bogus email
address in my Netscape preferences, as well as my news reader. The sig
file shows my real email, should someone want to respond to me that way.

Seems like the best way, personally, until the spammers get a better
script. YMMV.
