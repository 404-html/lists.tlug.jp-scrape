The annual JAIA Ham Fair is being held this weekend (8/22~8/24, 10:00~17:00)
at Tokyo Big Sight.  I mention it to this group because the flea market in
recent years has typically contained a lot of used computers, including a
surprising number of used SPARCstations.  I bought my ELC at last year's
event (and carried it home on the bus and subway :-).
