tlug note from Craig Oda <craig@example.com>
--------------------------------------------------------

The August TLUG meeting was a lot of fun.  Thanks to
Mark for bringing a lot of goodies from Caldera.

For the October meeting we have about a dozen copies
of Caldera Open Linux Lite and maybe a half-dozen
copies of Open Dos.  We also have at least one
T-shirt left over.   

We would like to keep to the plan of having the
meeting the second Saturday of every second month.
This months meeting was a special date due to the
guest of honor, Mark, President of the Salt Lake Linux
User's Group.  

The second Saturday is the 11th of October.

After today's meeting we went to Akihabara and looked around.
Joe Marchak had previously got the people of Laser V to
post a TLUG poster.  We're trying to get an even bigger
response for the October meeting.  Try to market the group.
Mark said there were 200 SLLUG members.  

Personally, I'm going to post to both the fj.os.linux
and the debian-jp groups.  

Does anyone have a preferred place for the meeting?  Joe
mentioned that we could have it at HSBC again.  However,
if there is a better place, speak out.  Also, how about
12:30 again for the meeting time.

Regards,
Craig
