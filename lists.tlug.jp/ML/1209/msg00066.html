desktop, an Optiplex ---darn, I've forgotten the model, but relatively new,

legacy.  Just out of curiosity (this wasn't an urgent thing and was
something I was doing while doing other things), I changed BIOS to UEFI and
ran the install again. It installed and booted without issue.
