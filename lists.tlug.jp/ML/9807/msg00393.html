Interesting - kernel 2.0.35's qlogic and fdomain SCSI drivers have headers
similar to those in the 2.1.18+ kernels... which means that pcmcia-cs
3.0.3 won't build against the 2.0.35 source without some patching to the
.c files for those pc card drivers.  Fairly simple, you just have to
rip out the if/else statement that has it check for linux kernel 2.1.18 or
newer, and just operate as if it was 2.1.18 or newer... 

patch files available upon request, if anyone's going to try this.
