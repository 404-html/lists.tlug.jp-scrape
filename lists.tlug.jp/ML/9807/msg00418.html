I have Apple LaserWriter select 16/6100 PS in my work room. It has a
hard dirve that PS and TT fonts installed. It's a AppleTalk and TCP/IP
protocol printer. 

That Apple LaserWriter works fine with Mac, Win95 (with Samba) and LINUX
(TL2.0 and RH5.1). I can print Japanese web pages from English version
Netscape 4.0. 

Apple LaserWriter select 16/6100 PS is not well known outside Mac users.
However it's a good printer if you use Linux(UNIX), Mac and Windows.
Especially, business and DTP use.
