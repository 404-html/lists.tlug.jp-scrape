On Wed, 22 Jul 1998, Manuel Chakravarty wrote:

> Hi!
> 
> I just installed TurboLinux on my desktop machine, happily throwing
> AccelleratedX out, which I used so far for my STB Velocity 3D video
> card (and which every couple of days messed up its font data forcing
> me to restart the server over again).
> 
> Scott once mentioned that the card uses the S3 ViRGE/VX, which is
> supported by XFree's SVGA server and probing also finds this chip, but
> in some video modes -- in particular in 1152x900, which I want to use
> -- starting the X servers freezes the machine and I have to reboot.
> Does anybody have experience with this card?  Interestingly, the
> higher resolution of 1280x1024 works (but the quality is not good -- I 
> guess due to the lower refresh rate), so I'd like to use 1152x900,
> which worked with AccelleratedX.
> 
> I append the XF86Config below.  The spec below uses a color depth of
> 16; I also tried 24 with similar results.  BTW, I vaguely remember
> that I read somewhere that one should contribute new monitor specs.
> Does anybody know where to send it.

1152x900 isn't a 'real' mode, if I remember correctly.  Could be that that
particular card doesn't like it.  My Velocity 128 (much faster card -
trade in your Velocity 3D for a Velocity 128..) has a serious problem with
the XFree86 DGA extension, and it only works in 8, 15, or 32bpp, which is
very .... odd.  Could just be the card - if it works in other resolutions,
I'd suspect that's the case, and AccelX has some kind of workaround in
place that XFree86 does not - Chris would know better than I would,
though.

As for the monitor specs, send them to me, directly to me.  Best to use
the custom monitor cfg in turboxcfg and then send me your XF86Config - are
the correct settings in the one attached here?
