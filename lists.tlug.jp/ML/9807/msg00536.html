On Thu, 30 Jul 1998, Shigeo Honda wrote:

> Hi,
> 
> I'd setup TL2.0J (Pro package) server for our client. Httpd works fine
> and shows good performance.
> 
> http://www.jolf.co.jp
> 
> However I'm still fighting with sendmail setting on the same linux box.
> In my experience with RH5.1, sendmail starts send and receive mails just
> add sendmail.cw.
> 
> So, I put domain name 'jolf.co.jp' into /var/mail/sendmail.cw. I can
> send mail from the server. But the server can't receive email coming
> from other sites.

you need to use /etc/sendmail.cw, not /var/mail/sendmail.cw, on a TL
system (and I thought on RH, too?)

er, actually it's ./etc/mail/sendmail.cw on 8.9.0, it was /etc/sendmail.cw
on previous versions.
