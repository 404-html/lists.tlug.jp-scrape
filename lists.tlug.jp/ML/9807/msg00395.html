Jonathan wrote:
> Also, this disk runs REALLY hot. So hot I can't put my hand on it for
> more than a couple of seconds, and much hotter than the Quantum Maverick
> 540 next to it (/dev/sda).

One thing you should be careful of with the Sparc 1/1+ machines is heat.
There isn't an internal fan (something John Little pointed out several
months ago in his article on sparclinux) and running a hot disk in one of
these can cause it to fail. You may want to put this disk in an external
enclosure which has a good fan.
