I have only been on the TLUG mailing list a few months now, so I'm not 
really sure of the policy, but I hope that you don't find Scott's
blatant advertisement for TurboLinux offensive.... One of his few
faults is his over-enthusiasm. :)

We'll donate a couple of copies of TurboLinux and Applixware to
TLUG, so you're welcome to try them out that way too. Please remember

[We will offer a 10% discount to anyone who wants to buy it *at* TLUG

we're not supposed to undercut our resellers. Anyhow, if you're
interested in getting something from us, please let me or Scott know
before the meetings and we'll bring you a copy.]
