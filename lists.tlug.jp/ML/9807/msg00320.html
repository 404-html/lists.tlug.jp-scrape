If you've got Netscape running on a Linux console, color me impressed.  ;-)

Seriously -- gpm shouldn't be involved at all while you're running X.
Your problems with netscape most likely involve memory, DNS, or java,
but regardless its wrong to take your specific experience and
extrapolate that all Linux systems exhibit poor mouse behaviour.  I can
assure you that every system I've every managed had a mouse that works
just fine.

It definitely sounds like something isn't right with your system.

If you get your netscape problems resolved you might want to take a look
at the man page for xset.  Setting the accelleration and threshold
parameters may give you the mouse responsiveness you're looking for.
