Jonathan,

I should have added this to the last e-mail regarding your need for 
a 4m machine.  The RH 5.1 4c boot disk images are on RedHat's website,
and they apparently work for at least SS2's.  Here's the blurb from
Eric at RedHat:

> There are floopy boot images for Red Hat 5.1 on the SPARC now available. 
> They also work fine on the Sun4c :-) Please test them, and let us know
> if they work. They are available from 
> ftp.redhat.com:/updates/5.1/sparc/images. Copy both images onto floppies
> boot the boot.img disk, and put in the ramdisk.img disk when you're
> prompted for it. You should end up in the install shortly after.
> Good luck!
> Erik
