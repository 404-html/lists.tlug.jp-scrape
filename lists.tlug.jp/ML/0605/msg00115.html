This kind of dictionary is *very* useful:
http://www.polarcloud.com/rikaichan/
It works as a plugin for Firefox. When activated, you just have to pass your mouse over a kanji (or group of kanji) to obtain translations. You can even have detailed informations about kanjis such as on/kin reading, stroke counts, etc. And it is available in english, french and german. And it is GPL. いいね !
