What kernel are you running?

/etc/modules.conf is for 2.4.x and earlier kernels. 2.6.x uses
modprobe.conf instead. You can convert the former to the latter with
generate-modprobe.conf

# generate-modprobe.conf > /etc/modprobe.conf
