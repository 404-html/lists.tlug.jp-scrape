Posting of Linux-related jobs by list members is expressly approved, with
the exception that a recruiter joining the list solely or mostly for the
purpose of recruiting would be frowned upon.  That doesn't mean any list
member who is a recruiter is prohibited from posting, but discussing it
first with the listmaster would be advisable.

Non-member recruiters who have a post they would like put on list 
should also contact that listmaster, who will either approve or deny
it.

Your post is, of course, fine.  TLUGgers looking to help other TLUGgers
find work at there own place of employment are always welcome to post.


Speaking of which, if there are any other TLUGgers in LA who have 
backgrounds in programming or systems administration and are looking
for work, drop me a line.  We may have one or two openings coming up.
