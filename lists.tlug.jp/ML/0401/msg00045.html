Ah yes. Actually, "cjk" isn't very useful as such... It only affects a 
few applications... There are other flags as well, such as "nls" that 
gives locale support to glibc when you compile it, and "canna" and "wnn" 
that makes applications such as kinput2 use canna or wnn. I haven't 
bothered to find out what applications actually use the cjk flag though, 
but I included it for completeness.
