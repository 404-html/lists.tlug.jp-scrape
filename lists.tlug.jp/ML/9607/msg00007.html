provider... especially since you seem to be expecting back
a different string than the one you are sending.

>Jun 27 01:38:17 tsle chat[114]: ^M 
>Jun 27 01:38:35 tsle chat[114]: ATDT7739080^M^M 
>Jun 27 01:38:36 tsle chat[114]: CONNECT 28800/V42BIS^M 
>Jun 27 01:38:43 tsle chat[114]: ^M 
>Jun 27 01:38:43 tsle chat[114]:

send            expect
