     Yes. 3.1.2 does not expire because it's an official
     release. 3.1.2D and 3.1.2E and so on are beta releases
     which support "new video chipsets" that are not officially
     supported in 3.1.2. Yes, they do expire.

     For more info, check out http://ftp.xfree86.org/
