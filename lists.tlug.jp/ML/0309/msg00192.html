Sylpheed works very well with kinput2
But unlike some other apps, just opening it up
in uxterm or kterm won't suffice.
This does the trick, though:
LANG=ja_JP.eucjp XMODIFIERS=@example.com=kinput2 /usr/bin/sylpheed &
(with kinput2 already running).
There's a little mojibake in some of the
menu items. Nothing too serious, though.
Nice mailer for Japanese.
