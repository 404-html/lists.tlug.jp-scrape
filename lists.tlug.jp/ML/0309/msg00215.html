TLUG meeting is not that simple.  First, you have to bring new machines 
and give away to poor tluggers at the auction.  Second, you have to 
make presentation of any kind to get the ticket to nomikai party.  
After the nomikai you are required to sing songs to promote open source 
and free software at karaoke.

For the correct information about the meetings, visit the page for the 
last meeting:
http://www.tlug.jp/meetings/200309/index.php
