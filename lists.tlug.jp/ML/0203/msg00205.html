-=-=-=-=- Tokyo Linux Users Group March Meeting Announcement -=-=-=-=-


All Linux lovers, and supporters of open source code and free software are
invited to attend the next Tokyo Linux Users Group meeting. Membership is
open to anyone. There are currently no membership dues or entrance fees.


Date:	Saturday, March 16th, 2002

Place:	Penguin Village seminar room
	Linux Building 2F
	3-13-2 Soto Kanda, Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo

	Please visit TLUG web site for a map:
	http://www.tlug.gr.jp/meetings/next.html

	Nearest stations: Akihabara(JR), Hirosue-cho (Underground),
	Iwamoto-cho (U)

Time:	Meet us at 1F Linux Cafe di Pronto at 13:30.
	http://www.pronto.co.jp/pronto/new/new_1205.html

Topics: March meeting will feature following speakers.

	1. "Security and Firewall" by Yuki Ikuno

	Yuki works at a company that supports ISPs and ICPs
	infrastructures, and she specialises in firewall administration.
	She will be speaking about fundamentals of Unix system security.

	2. "Linux-based Backup and Recovery Disk" by Frank Bennett

	Frank is a professor of Law at University of Nagoya.  He will
	"demonstrate a multilingual, self-replicating Linux-based backup
	system that is designed for use with laptops and small
	workstations, discuss some of the neat features of the code, and
	some of its more ghastly shortcomings."

After the meeting:

	We will continue chatting over a few beers.
	http://www.teng.co.jp/tenpo/map/map019.html
