Jim Breen wrote:
[...]
> No-one around here has much RH7.2 experience. Any suggestions? One
> suggestion I got was to remove dhcp and reinstall it. All I want is the
> defaults back.

in your /etc/sysconfig/network-scripts/ifcfg-eth0 do you have 
something like:

   DEVICE=eth0
   ONBOOT=yes
   BOOTPROTO=dhcp

and in /etc/sysconfig/network:

   NETWORKING=yes
   HOSTNAME=your.host.name.com

look for 'pump' -- RedHat does not use 'dhcpc'
