> Anyone know what brand or models of different laptops are compatible
> with the Linux freeX86 server?

Laptop brand is unimportant.  Laptop video chipset is very important.

Chipset support status is at http://www.xfree86.org/4.2.0/Status.html

Look for something with an S3 or an ATI chipset.  Try before you buy.
