Given the fact, that these "disasters", crashes etc. happen constantly
every five minutes,
during the most trivial operations ....

Like the "reset button" ages ago.
When the computer goes berserk ... just push that button and everything
goes back to normal.

There was no magic there. A broken system is just broken, and needs a fix. A backup is an option, if you know what you need to replace. An install DVD is another, considering you will loose a lot. Or of course, you may prefer to just fix the problem, and keep your system as it is. This would be my choice. This needs a little investigation however, just by understanding what is the trigger to the issue (at first: system or user related).
