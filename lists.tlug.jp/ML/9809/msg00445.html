Japan Webgrrls is a non-profit chapter of an international 
organization dedicated to promoting women's understanding 
of computers and digital information technology for professional
and personal use. 

Our mission is to help women and girls succeed in the world of information
technology by providing a supportive environment to discuss technology issues,
network, mentor and learn.

No computer knowledge is needed, and women of all nationalities, professions
and ages are welcome. Please pass this flyer to a sister, mother, wife, aunt,
grandmother, girlfriend, friend, co-worker or manager who might be interested
in joining us!

Japan Webgrrls is part of Webgrrls International, an organization with more
than 100 chapters and 4,000+ members world wide.

Join us for six hours of computer skill sharing and fun on September 27, 1998
