>Um, what's so great about ATOK or Kotoeri?  I don't notice Canna

To be really honest, I prefer MS-IME to either, but what's good about all
three is they do a much better job of correctly deciphering a string of kana
(Wnn and Canna both seem to need short strings or they choke a lot), they do a
better job of learning (for かき Canna will often give me 下記 one time and 
書き the next, even though I always select the first of those), and it's very
easy to add new compounds to them.  I suspect (but haven't done anything to
confirm) that they also have larger dictionaries.  Someday if I'm capable, I'd
like to write a floating X toolbar that provides the kind of management
frontend for Canna that MS-IME has.  I hope nobody is waiting though, 'cuz
that could be many years :-)
