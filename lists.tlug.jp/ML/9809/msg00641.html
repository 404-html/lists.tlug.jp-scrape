IBM 10.1 GB IDE drives are under 40,000.  The 6.4 GB units are in the 24,000 -
25,000 range.  The SCSI drives cost quite a bit more, so considering the size
and speed, I would certainly go with an IDE unit for a home PC, unless I
wanted to set up a RAID.

Your experience with the WD drives bears out what I've been hearing, too.  My
three year old WD Caviar is a terrific drive, but I've heard WD isn't what it
once was :-(
