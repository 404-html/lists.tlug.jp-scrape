> What is the appropriate <meta ...> header?  "charset=x-euc-jp" is, of
> course, evil, because of the "x-".

AFAIK the Japanese charsets are:

Name: ISO-2022-JP
Alias: csISO2022JP

so the tag would be

<META HTTP-EQUIV="Content-Type" CONTENT="text/html"; charset=ISO-2022-JP">

I think the currently assigned charsets are at:

ftp://ftp.isi.edu/in-notes/iana/assignments/character-sets
