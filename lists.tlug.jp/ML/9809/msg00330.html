Hi, 

In message "tlug: Cisco 2501 router vs. Japanese ISDN"
    on 98/09/11, Scott Stone <sstone@example.com> writes:
> Is there any way to hook up a Japanese ISDN DSU to a Cisco 2501 router?
> The cisco has that proprietary serial connector (2 of them), and an
> ethernet hookup... I'm sure I'd need some kind of wonky cable to go
> between the 2501 and the DSU, but... does such an animal exist?

With ISDN TA and Serial cable. 
The Cisco serial(HB-60) to TA(DB25) is needed.
e.g. from Black Box catalog,
	EHC130-0006, RS232 cisco cable, 6ft., 16,880yen

and you will look for normal INS64 TA 
(e.g. NEC Aterm IT55) that can use sync 64k mode.
