This is controlled by the mailcap and mime.types files.  Nobody knows
what they is except for the authors of RFC 2045-2049, so Netscape
calls it "Helpers" and doesn't document it very well.  Play with that.
I'm not sure Netscape is smart enough to allow you to tell it to do
the right thing, though.

I'm not sure where they are properly documented; I'm not sure they're
even MIME standards, although I think they are (but everybody does it
different so maybe they're not).
