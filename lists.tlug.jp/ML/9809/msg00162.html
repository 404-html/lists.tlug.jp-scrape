On Tue, 8 Sep 1998, Jonathan Byrne wrote:

> What Ethernet PCMCIA cards have you found to work well with Linux?  What
> are any that are best avoided?

The 3Com 3c589 series works well.  The only ones that I know will NOT work
are modem/ethernet combo cards (except the 3Com 3c563 combo card, which
does work).  The other combo cards will only work as modems, in my
experience.
