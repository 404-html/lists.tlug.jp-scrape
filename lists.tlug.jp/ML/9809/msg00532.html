An unofficial survey of the headers of posters to this ML indicates that
quite a few of you use Mew on Emacs or especially XEmacs for your email.
As a Linux wannabe still mainly using NT, I find Becky! Internet Mail
meets my needs pretty well:
-- polling multiple POP servers automatically
-- filtering and all that
-- displaying html posts
-- multiple, visible signatures
-- template support
-- threaded views
-- simple configuration

How much of this functionality is provided by the Mew-Emacs combination,
how much is provided by other processes, and is any of this not
available? In the last case, what email programs provide all of the
above effectively?
