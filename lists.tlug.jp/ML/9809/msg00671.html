> The mutt folks used to be pretty adamant about not including IMAP
> support.  It's a wonder there is even any POP support.  The prevailing

*laugh* The support is there now, it appears.  The IMAP stuff is in
libmutt.a, at any rate.

> How is balsa handling IMAP?

Not too well.  The config parser for IMAP is horribly broken (i.e., will
SIGSEGV if things aren't exactly right -- why the HELL would one HAVE to

server log), but it thinks that something went wrong and complains about a
bad password.
