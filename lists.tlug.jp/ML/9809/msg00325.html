Dave wrote:
> I was just wondering if anyone knew of and could recomend a nice GUI
> interface mail program for Linux, something along the lines of what you
> get from Eudora on a Windows machine. Right now i'm using Netscape, and
> it kind of bites.

I still think pine is the best mail utility around.  If you want a nice
X-windows front-end, Applix works well for received mail - with a bit
of work you can get it to sort all of your mail into mailboxes as well.

Jonathan wrote:
> I don't have any particular recommendation, but if I did you might not

whenever it has to pull in anything larger than 1Mb.
