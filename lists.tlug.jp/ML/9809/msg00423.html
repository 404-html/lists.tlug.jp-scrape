> Years ago, on an OS far far away I had a shareware GUI world clock that 
> would give me times in 10 different cities around the world. Has anyone 
> seen  anything like this for Linux? 

Not that it probably helps you much, but of the world clock progs i've used 
(not that many) on Mac and Win, TimePalette for the Mac was the best IMHO.
If you have access to a Mac it might be worth having a look at for ideas.   It 
lets you set up a palette with as many locations as you like and has optional
night/day shaded world map.

Brief blurb on the latest(??) ver at:
http://micros.hensa.ac.uk/mirrors/info-mac/per/_Info-Mac_Digest/infomacv16-98.txt

Disclaimer:  I haven't seen the latest version.   I the one I saw was a few
years old.
