???? Just for the 15 V differencence ???? Improbable. I have a
Libretto 30 with PSU here and the PSU gets the output of my
stepdown transformer ( down from 230 V to 115 V ) and works just
fine. Seriously: if a PSU goes bonkers just for that additional
15 V than it wont work very long with 100 V either: the next
glitch on the line's gonna take it out. 

However, there are LOTS of horrible switcher designs out there
and with some I wonder why they work at all.....

The worst thing in bad switcher design I ever saw was in an
Apple Lisa, where they had a horrible circuit - from looking on
it I concluded that putting it under power would make it go up
in smoke. Which it did when I plugged the thing in. In the end I
ended up redesigning the PSU to make it work correctly.....
