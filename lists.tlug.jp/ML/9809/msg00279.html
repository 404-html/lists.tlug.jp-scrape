Acutally,

Within Japan thats a fairly easy two criteria for a company.

All computers purchased will be Japanese Capable.
All Employees should be Japanese Literate.

Even companies with 1 or two gaijin running around WILL be running on
Japanese capable machines, and for one person in a hundred in your
organization, he can ask for help now and again.

Don't put system boot files or the link in MBCS if the OS needs to load
a module to use it though. (Like most Unix, untill you start some
windowing systems many Unix will NOT deal with MBCS on the console, but
how often is that used)

for personal machines its an even easier criterion to meet.

Even a behemoth like MS or IBM, files private to the Japanese office
can be however they want.

-Scott
