Scott wrote:

> It's been a long time since I've used LILO with WinNT.. it worked way
> back when, but.. I've heard people have trouble with it, lately.  AND, I
> don't have NT around here in the shop to test it... anyone know if/how
> it works with NT? (LILO, that is...)

Works fine, as long as the disk is less than 4Gb.  Or at least that
was my experience with lilo 0.20 on several machines here in the office.
The other thing is to use VFAT rather than NTFS, since NTFS is still
read-only under linux.
