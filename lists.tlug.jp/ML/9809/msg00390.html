I figured out how to get the drive, to work, and it is now funtioning 
perfectly..... Here is the steps I followed in case anyone is 
intrested...

1)Upgrade to kernel 2.0.35
2)Recompile kernel with SCSI emulation and Generic SCSI devices enabled.
3)modifiy my lilo.conf to include the line 'append="hdd=ide-scsi"'
4)Rebooted
5)Installed xcdroast

The drive seems to be functioning perfectly for now.  I seriously 
considered obtaining the SCSI version but since it would have costed me 
another 10,000 for a PCI SCSI card, I got the ATAPI version instead.  
The Richo6200A is most likely the cheapest CD-RW on the market at 40,000 
and it has a slightly slow but still descent 6x read/2x write speed.
