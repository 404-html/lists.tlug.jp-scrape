For those who are interested here is the link to the Computernacht
webpage: ccnacht.paderlinx.de. On this page they also have links and
 reports about the worldrecord Linux cluster. It`s in 
German, but maybe there is still some interest.
The project was a coopertion of the German Linux-Magazin and LUGs in
Germany, Austria and Switzerland. They built a 512 node Linux cluster
and ran several programs on it during the night from Dec 5 to Dec. 6.
Among those was the rendering of a movie and several benchmarks which
are aimed to determine the cluster`s place among the worlds 300
fastest computers.
