On Sat, 19 Dec 1998, Sean Bennett wrote:

> [RH 5.2]
> 
> I'm trying to install a group of .rpm packages, but come up
> with the following missing files ("failed dependancies")
> 
>     libgdk.so.1
>     libgtk.so.1
>     gtk+10
>     libc5xlibs
> 
> now the last two are not on my system, but the first two,
> libgdk.so.1 and  libgtk.so.1 are both located in my
> /usr/bin/ directory.
> 
> I've tried re-ordering my /etc/ld.so.conf file and running
> /etc/ldconfig to no avail.  How do I make these files
> visible to the .rpm packages?  Also, where can I find the
> last 2 missing files,  gtk+10 and libc5xlibs ?

the libraries need to be provided by an RPM, not installed separately.
rpm -q --whatprovides libgdk.so.1 should reveal the truth.

libc5xlibs is something for TL.  it's in our tree.
