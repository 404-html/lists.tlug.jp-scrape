I guess everybody's away from their computers, but I'd like to take a
minute to wish the happiest holidays to all TLUG subscribers.

It's been a very busy year for TLUG, we've seen tremendous growth, and
recently have also had some changes and difficult spots, but I think we
are once again in good shape and 1999 will be another good year for us.

I would especially like to thank all of our current and former volunteers
for the hard work they have put in this year and for all they have done
for TLUG.  Most especially, Joe Marchak, Craig Oda, and Ken Harada, but
also Alberto, Chris, the two Jims, Shigeo, and the many others who have
done so much for TLUG this year.

Thanks to everyone for a job very well done!

Cheers,

Jonathan
