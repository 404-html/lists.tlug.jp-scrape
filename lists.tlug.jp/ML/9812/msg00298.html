> Hi,
> 
> I went to buy 2 cards PCI clone RealTek  (cheap), installed them and booted.
> Added the ne2k-pci in the conf.modules, then restarted the kerneld.
> Now the 2 interface are as: (/proc/pci file).
> 
> Bus 0, Device 3, function 0
> IRQ 11
> I/O at 0x1040
> 
> Bus 0, Device 2, function 0
> IRQ 11
> I/O at 0x1020
> 
> Shouldn't the IRQ be different!!??

yes.  Tell your cmos that you do NOT have a plug and play OS, and the bios
should do it for you.  If worse comes to worst, manually assign IRQs to
the slots occupied by the cards in your CMOS setup.
