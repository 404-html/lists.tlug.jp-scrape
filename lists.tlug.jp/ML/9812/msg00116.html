> Or, even better, does someone know a more convenient solution to prevent

Unauthorized booting, or unauthorized instant-root-access?

The easiest way is to dd that kernel image onto a floppy, rdev it for the
correct root partition, and boot from that floppy always.  No lilo, no way
to (easily) pass arguments to the kernel.
