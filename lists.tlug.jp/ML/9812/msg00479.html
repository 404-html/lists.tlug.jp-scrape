Hi,

Since I have some free time, I love vacations :), I've been
tweaking the TLUG home page.

The glimpse search engine no longer indexes the threads.html,
maillist.html, or .mhonarc.db files (hint: use a
.glimpse_exclude file) so searches are speedier and less
unwanted crap is returned.

http://tlug.linux.or.jp/ML/

I installed a smaller search engine on the meetings archive (and
on the tlug-admin archive, but it's broken due to the mail
archive files all having the same title:-( It's pretty cool and
has a summary database so you can track what people are
searching for...

http://tlug.linux.or.jp/meetings/

Enjoy!

Jim S.
