On Mon, 21 Dec 1998, Matt Gushee wrote:

> In any case, I thought I might want a separate font server, that's why 
> I built all of XFree86 and not just the server.

Well, uh, you know ... the next time you do that, you might want to set
BuildServersOnly to 'yes' in host.def -- then cd into programs/xfs and
'xmkmf -a; make'.  That will save a lot of disk space and time.

> Research <;-> suggests that the X-TrueType-enabled xfs should be
> superior to xfstt, but we'll have to see what really happens, won't
> we?

It's faster for me.
