On Tue, 30 May 2006 10:05:04 +0200
Godwin Stewart <godwin.stewart@example.com> wrote:

> Make sure your /etc/X11/xorg.conf contains this near the top:
> 
>     Load "glx"
>     Load "dri"
> 
> And that you have this (in my xorg.conf it's at the bottom but I'm not
> convinced it *has* to be there):
> 
> Section "DRI"
>    Mode 0666
> EndSection


Actually I had these in my xorg.conf. Moving these to the top and
removing the server section of the second head made the glx hardware
acceleration work in a 32-bit debian jail.
Unfortunately, the mesa-glx drivers in the amd64 version of xorg in debian
are broken, so there is no opengl in 64-bit debian yet (resulting in "No
such visual" errors), though this is already fixed in xorg upstream.
Hopefully the fix will appear in debian soon.

Thanks for your reply, Godwin.
