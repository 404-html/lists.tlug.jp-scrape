I don't think you have many choices, other than Zaurus. Someone fill me in.
I am happy with my Z, but I do feel that it was too expensive for its usefulness. As a pda, a palm is probably a better buy, if only because there are decent wireless cards (I curse the day I bought this planex gw-cf11x), a better built-in calendar, etc.
Since you are a linux whiz, the Z will be a great toy, but you cannot play with your pda (reinstalling, etc.) if you are busying using it as a pda, which is why I suggested the palm. The pda needs to always be the pda and functional, and for this reason, I am still using the Sharp ROM in Japanese. Also, I like the handwriting recognition. But the main reason is that I don't have the luxury of down-time with the Z because I am relying on it to run KO/Pi (which is heavenly, let me say again... hail the developers... and suggest that they release an upgrade!) 

Just a humble perspective from a Z user.
