> So, it sounds like the console is working fine, and you are just having 
> problems with Xorg... ?
> 
> I would suggest simplifying your xorg.conf as much as possible.  Comment out 
> all (unnecessary) modules (as well as the DRI section at the bottom).  Once 
> you get it to work, you can then uncomment modules that you want (one at a 
> time) to determine which ones work and which one(s) cause the system to 
> crash.

or even booting in single user mode ("single" on the kernel command
line), disabling the display manager[1] (gdm, kdm or whatever) and
trying startx from the console. It may be easier to tweak xorg.conf and
all the trial and error testing.

[1]: how to do that depends on the distribution but a quick and dirty
way to do it is to add an "exit 0" just after the she-bang line in the
/etc/init.d/[xgkw]dm script
