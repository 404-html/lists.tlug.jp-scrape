Josh wrote:

> [Jim] wrote:

> > Bits per pixel can be controlled with the depth option.
> 
> e.g. in your /etc/X11/xorg.conf:

I was referring to the depth option at the knoppix boot prompt. 
E.g., 

   boot: knoppix screen=1600x1200 vsync=85 depth=24

There are many knoppix boot options. 
Check them out with the "function" keys, 
such as <F2> and <F3> keys when you see the boot prompt. 

Of course, there's probably a pretty strong relationship 
between the knoppix boot depth option and the X configuration file.
