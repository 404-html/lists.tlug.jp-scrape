I'm not sure what is so "wrong" with gnome. I think it is a clean and
uncluttered. My wife has no issue with using it, and she thinks she is
using a computer so all is good ;-)

exploded, or that I have stepped into a pachinko parlour. Hovering

play pachinko - at least the last time I looked at KDE that was the
case.

somewhere between a minimalist one (ala wmii fluxbox and so on) and a
full on desktop like KDE or Gnome.
