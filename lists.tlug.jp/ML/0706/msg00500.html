Is is correct (i.e., does threading then work) to copy the title
verbatim from the digest index and paste it into our (in my case
Gmail) subject line?


No.

Threading has *nothing* to do with the subject header. Messages are
threaded according to relationships between Message-ID:, References:
and In-Reply-To: headers. /../

Or is there a better and more acceptable way? 

The only way to thread correctly is to receive mailing list traffic as
individual messages *and* to have a mail client that includes proper
threading information in replies /../
