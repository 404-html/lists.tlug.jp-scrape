That's good, not only for what you write, 
but for cleaning up what you quote. 

> I generally eyeball it on the first few lines,

That's usually good enough. 

Another technique, is to paste in a ruler line like 
123456789 123456789 123456789 123456789 123456789 123456789 123456789 123456789 
then delete it before sending. The hard part is 
remembering to delete it. Sylpheed has a built 
in ruler along the top of the composition area. 

Sylpheed is available for Windows, and has good support 
for Japanese. It also allowed me to reply to a MIME 
digest with breaking a thread, as mentioned elsewhere. 
Of course, I understand that your organization might 
have concerns about using software that they are not 
familiar with.
