Hi,

The pre-intel Macs all run PowerPC (ppc) processors. When you're
looking for an ISO to download, make sure you get a ppc version.

On 6/12/07, Godwin Stewart <gstewart@example.com> wrote: 
Ubuntu is based on Debian. There is a PowerPC version of Debian
available.


This is true. Ubuntu might not be ported to ppc, but Debian is... and
since Ubuntu is based on Debain, it shouldn't be too difficult for you
to get the hang of it when your friend needs a little help.

I had a brief encounter with Gentoo Linux on an older ppc Powerbook
some time ago. Be aware that some things (namely the wireless card)
may not be supported, or at least may be difficult to get running. Or
maybe not, I tried it a long time ago.

Good luck, hope you find something!
Mike
