Thanks for pointing it out.  We are very much willing to contact the wiki masters that your have indicated.
 
We would like everybody to understand that we do not necessarily favor heavily on contacting only Okinawa LUGs for this matter.  It is just that an  IOSN ASEAN+3 official will be in the area attending an event on June 14 to 16 and that we would like to maximize the stay by networking with local LUGs by meeting up with their representatives.
