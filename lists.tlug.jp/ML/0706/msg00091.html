On 06/06/07, Jim <jep200404@example.com> wrote:

Stephen wrote:

> Note that a 1024x768 screen in 24-bit mode requires ~= 3x(3/4) = 2-1/4
> MB of video RAM ... do you have that much?

Bits per pixel can be controlled with the depth option. 

e.g. in your /etc/X11/xorg.conf:


Section "Screen"
       Identifier "Screen0"
       Device     "Card0"
       Monitor    "Dell20"
       DefaultDepth 16
       SubSection "Display"
               Depth     16
               Modes     "1600x1200"
       EndSubSection
EndSection


Note the DefaultDepth and Depth lines.
