OCN has an official limit of 30GB *upload* per *day*, with no (stated)
limit on download. Which is not too bad (comes out to >2.7Mbps
sustained for 24 hrs) and better than ISPs that throttles you/cut you
off when you reach an unknown limit. (No limit at all would be better,
of course :) )
