We are investigating payment gateways.  We basically need credit card
and PayPal processing to start then may branch out to other forms of
payment.  The options we came up with were.

1. PayPal Website Payments Standard

https://merchant.paypal.com/cgi-bin/marketingweb?cmd=_render-content&content_ID=merchant/wp_standard

2. GMO Payment Gateway

http://www.gmo-pg.com/

3. Softbank Payment Services

http://www.sbpayment.jp/

Has anyone had any experience with using any of these payment gateways
for payment processing?  How easy are they to integrate with? Are there
other payment gateways you would recommend?
