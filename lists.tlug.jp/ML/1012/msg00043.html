On Tue, Dec 7, 2010 at 1:58 AM, Dave M G <dave@example.com> wrote:
> Long story short: The Japanese fonts(?) I've got on my computer are
> giving me different depictions of the same kanji.

The problem is that your system is displaying some text using Chinese
fonts.  From previous posts, I gather that you are running Ubuntu, in
which the easiest way to solve the issue is to simply remove the
Chinese fonts:

$ sudo apt-get remove ttf-wqy-*
$ sudo fontconfig-voodoo -f -s ja_JP
