Hi,

2013/01/23 12:48, Ian Lawrence Barwick wrote:

Hi all

I'm looking at upgrading my ca. 2009-era Emobile USB dongle to WiMax.

Does anyone have any experience (good or bad) with this?

I'm planning to get a portable wifi version, so presumably Linux
compatibility will not be an issue.

My main concern is coverage - Emobile has been very reliable even out
in the sticks, how does WiMax measure up?


Actually, it depends on where you want to go. :)
I've been using WiMAX for two years on my laptop which has a built-in WiMAX module, and it works fine for me so far.
Mostly, I've been working at the east area in Tokyo. And from my experience, I guess 95% (or more) locations where I go are available for WiMAX: including several cafe, large office buildings, and so on. 

And of course, sometimes I can't get the wave. :)
I guess some WiMAX service provides are offering a trial, so I recommend to have a trial before you sign-up for WiMAX. 

Regards,



Thanks for any comments


Ian Barwick
