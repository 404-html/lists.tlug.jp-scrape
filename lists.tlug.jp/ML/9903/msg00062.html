Hi everyone,

I figured out a solution to that problem.  It seems that fbcon doesn't 
like XFree86's hardware cursor option (hw_cursor).  With a Riva 128, 
it's neccecary to manually force it off by adding the following line to 
your XF86Config file in the video card definition area.

Option "sw_cursor"
