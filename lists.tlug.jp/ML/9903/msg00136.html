On Thu, 11 Mar 1999, Hiro Yoshioka wrote:

> I'm not Linux hacker but it seems to me the max process count be
> NR_TASKS (512?)

There is a hard limit, yes.  Thanks for pointing out the omission in my
original answer.

> I could ulimit -u 1000 but does it mean if the system can have 1000
> process?

Not if the ulimit exceeds the kernel hard limit.

This can be altered in <linux/tasks.h>, to a maximum of 4092[1].

-- Chris

[1] Actually, 4096 -- four tasks are reserved for root.  4096 == 0x1000,
    so it's fairly obvious as to why the limit is what it is ...
