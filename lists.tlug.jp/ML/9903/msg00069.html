On Sat, Mar 06, 1999 at 05:42:24AM -0800, Austin Kurahone wrote:
> Does anyone know if it is possible to use a Sun Keyboard on a PC (in 
> Linux of course).  My Compaq keyboard doen't quite cut it and it'll be 
> nice to be able to use the Copy/Paste/Etc Keys with Linux.

You'll need to buy or build an adapter to serve as a protocol converter.

Raritan sells an expensive one.  http://www.raritan.com/

I've seen people document their one-chip microcontroller solutions on the
net, but haven't built one myself. http://www.osk.ch/kbd5pc/
