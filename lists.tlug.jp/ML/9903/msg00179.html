SADP is not just a CD player...

Features  spectrum analyser,  oscillator,  mixer.  Accepts new
parameters and  window size on fly.  Adaptable to odd CD drives, can
change colours.   Supports xcd  data base...  Have I missed smth ? Try
and see.

Tested with Linux 2.0.36 , XFree 3.3.2  and a number of CD-drives and
sound cards.  Requires XForms v0.81+ (version 0.88 is recommended).

Available from:

SADP home page (description, preview, download, links to xforms):
 http://www.alphalink.com.au/~michg/ace/sadp

If you are impatient or short in net time, go straight to the FTP site:
Source   (81K):
ftp://metalab.unc.edu/pub/Linux/apps/sound/players/xsadp-1.2.0.tar.gz
Binary  ELF , libc5, XForms 0.88  dynamically linked (70K):
ftp://metalab.unc.edu/pub/Linux/apps/sound/players/xsadp-1.2.0.bin.tar.gz

Go,  get it,  ehjoy it, and  DON'T FORGET TO SEND ME AN  EMAIL
WITH  YOUR  COMMENTS (better than registration fees, I believe).

P.S. A text mode version (ncurses , gpm) is also available. See home
page.
