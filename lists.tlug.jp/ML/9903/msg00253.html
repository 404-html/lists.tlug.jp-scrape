Remember when Compaq came out with their portable color laptop solution.

Aero 4/33c
CPU: Intel 486sx 33Mhz
RAM: 4 MB / 20 MAX
Hard Drive: 250 MB
Video: SMOS Standard VGA 256K
LCD: Sanyo STN Backlit Color 7.8-inch 640x480 resolution
PCMCIA: 1-Socket. VLSI chipset
Floppy: None, uses PCMCIA external

Turbo Linux Japanese (Keyboard was in Japanese) besides I wanted a japanese

Great nifty solution Small lite and compact.. X runs smoothly without
problems.  Another happy customer..
