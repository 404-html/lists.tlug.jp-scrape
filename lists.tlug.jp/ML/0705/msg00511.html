On 5/29/07, burlingk@example.com <burlingk@example.com> wrote: 

Are there any really good XML tutorials on the web, or perhaps a book that
is actually useful?


To help you in your studies, I suggest reading "Portlets and Apache
Portals" by Stefan Hepper et al.   You can register and download a
free copy of it at http://www.manning.com/hepper/ . While this book
isn't about XML per se, it is a useful resource for showing how to put
XML to work. Also, it is good for getting a better understanding of
the jargon associated with "web services".
