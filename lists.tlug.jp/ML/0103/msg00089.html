Damn, I forgot that - there's a bug in the Perl, and while forgiving
software doesn't mind, giftopnm is strict.

Two solutions come to mind:

(a) if you are only doing a few images, load up the gif in xv and save
it back on top of itself. xv will fix it.

(b) longer term, pass the gif through another utility like gif2png,
then do the pngtopnm/pnmtotiff chain. You may have to grab gif2png from
somewhere, and also install libpng, although I think it's fairly
standard these days. You need to see a parameter to tell gif2png to
ignore errors.
