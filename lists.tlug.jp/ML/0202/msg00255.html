Jonathan Byrne (jq@example.com) wrote:

> I've been hoping to find out a few models of CF and/or Smart Media
> readers sold in Japan that work with Linux so that I can make a
> public list available for the benefit of TLUG (and others). ...

Well, my company's Hexa Media Drive works well with Linux.  The Drive 
can read/write six different media types.  You can use Compact Flash, 
Smart Media, and MemoryStick/SD Card at the same time.  Here are some links:

English: http://www.yedata.com/product/eps11091.html
Japanese: http://www.yedata.co.jp/product/jps11091.html

You can order the HMD from here (Japanese): 
http://www.yedata.co.jp/ydmates/index.htm

The Slim-FBU USB Floppy also works with Linux, but the Tri-Media Reader 
unfortunately does not.

Hope this helps,
