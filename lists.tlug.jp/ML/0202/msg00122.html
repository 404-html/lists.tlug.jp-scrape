This is to remind you that the next TLUG Tokyo Linux User Group nomikai
meeting will be held in Tengu, Tokyo Eki-mae Branch, on February 15th 19:30.

If you need to contact us:

Mauro ......090-2492-0840 (Keitai)
Kato   ......070-5591-7003 (PHS)


Date:           Friday, February 15, 2002
Time           19:30 -

Meeting Place:  Tengu, Tokyo Eki-mae Branch
Chuo-ku, Kyobashi 1-1-6, EchiZenYa Bld. B1/B2
Tengu is about 5 minutes away from the Yaesu Chuo exit of the JR Tokyo
train station. It is on the right hand side of Yaesu Dori if you are
walking out of the station.

Costs:  The costs are divided up among the participants.
Description:    Eat and drink with a bunch of Linux enthusiasts and
their friends! This is primarily a social event, but there is a lot
of casual discussion of Linux and other technical topics between gulps.
Even if you're not a Linux user or a LUG member, but want to hang out
with the Linux/Unix people, please come.

For more information and a map , see the meeting web page at

http://www.tlug.gr.jp/meetings/next.html

Mauro D. Sauco.
Tokyo Linux User Group.
