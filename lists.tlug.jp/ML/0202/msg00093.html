Yeah, that mail fooled my procmail settings too.  It looks like
something on Godwin's end.  Remember, the way SMTP works, the To:
field doesn't really have anything to do with where things are
actually delivered--after all, this mail is going to get delivered to
you in spite of saying "To: tlug@example.com".

Anyway, for more reliable filtering, it looks like you need to filter
on something generated by the list software instead of whatever the
poster fills in. "Resent-From: tlug@example.com" looks like it would
work.  Is hotmail's filter sophisticated enough to work on any part
of the header line?
