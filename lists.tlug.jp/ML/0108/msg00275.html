For anyone needing a small linux distribution, or for
those folks who like NetBSD but want a Linux kernel, 
I've found that CRUX is a well maintained, closer to the 
edge distro. While this distro is not intended for 
newbies, the installation documentation is good enough
for the more experienced newbies to cut their teeth on. 

Crux can be found at: http://radionet.fi/~valen/crux
