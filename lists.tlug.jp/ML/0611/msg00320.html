I can't comment on local availability, but I have a Proxim Wireless ORiNOCO Gold 11b/g card (model: 8470-FC) that works just fine.  It has the Atheros chipset which is supported by the MadWifi driver:
http://madwifi.org/
It also has an external antenna connector if you wanted to get better range out of it.
