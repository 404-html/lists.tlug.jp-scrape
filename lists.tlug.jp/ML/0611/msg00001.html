Matt Gross wrote:

> Does anyone have any recommendations for a laser printer within 
> my price range which will work _well_ with linux?

Postscript is the lingua franca of printers for Linux, 
and gets you the ultimate in compatibility for Linux, 
so get a postscript printer. 

The HP LaserJet 4 series (4, 4+, 4M, and 4M+) are serious 
workhorses that are cheap used now. I bought a used one two or 
three years ago for $20 w/o postscript. I see a postscript 
module for $5 on Ebay. Hmmm. I'm going to have to buy that. 

Buy a good new genuine HP cartridge for about 10,000 yen.
