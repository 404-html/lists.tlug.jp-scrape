No, they haven't.  The card vendors have, under extreme pressure from
Apple and Microsoft.  Apple users also generally use Apple-supplied,
or at least -approved, hardware.  That makes a really big difference.

Also note that a graphics card is far and away the most complicated
peripheral that a desktop user ever encounters, and far more
complicated than the other two realtime peripherals (audio and mouse).
