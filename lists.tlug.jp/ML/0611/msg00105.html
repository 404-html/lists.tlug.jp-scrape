Hello,

I just picked up a Creative Microphoto (8GB, MP3) for 9,000[1] yen on
Saturday. Works well with Linux once you follow one of the many gnomad2
howtos on the net.

My wife has a Creative Zen (6GB, MP3) which worked with Linux straight
out of the box as it was detected as a simple USB storage device.

my library is sufficiently large enough to make transcoding to ogg a
PITA event. However, I did think of doing so until I went shopping for a
decent ogg capable player...
