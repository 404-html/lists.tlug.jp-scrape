terminals and editor windows. XEmacs seems to know this, because the
colours it chooses for syntax highlighting work quite well on a black

colours are used, and the resulting output on a white piece of paper
is, well, unreadable.

Is there an easy way to tell XEmacs to swap its colour palette to
dark-on-light for pretty printing?
