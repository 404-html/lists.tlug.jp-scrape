I was using OCN (NTT) service a few years ago (2000-2003). Don't know
what they offer today, but at that time I had 3 bits IP address space,

That was expensive at that time (30,000 / month) - I had some motivation
to "manage" everything myself ;-)

My answer is surely worthless for you, as it was long ago, but I just
wanted to show that you can get a full IP range with a provider, Yahoo
isprobably not one of these ones.
