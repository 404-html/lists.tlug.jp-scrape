> Can I designate that partition as a subdirectory of my /home,
> e.g., /home/music? If so, when setting up that partition, should I name
> its directory as /home/music,  ...

Yes. mkdir /home/music first, then your /etc/fstab entry will look
something like:
/dev/hdd1       /home/music         ext3    defaults        1 2
