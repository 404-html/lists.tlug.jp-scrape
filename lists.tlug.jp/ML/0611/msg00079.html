Serach for gnupod. You can mount the ipod as a storage device, but you
need to eneter the mp3 into the database in order to play them under
the ipod os. The older nanos could also be booted (dual botted) with
rockbox which would allow ogg vorbis playback. I bought the new 8GB
nano which has encrypted firmware and now I am stuck with gnupod and
the ipod OS. 
Gnupod uses a cli and can be used in scripts. There is also gtkpod
which uses a GUI, but I found not as fast as gnupod.
