This is what I have:

http://www.signeo.co.jp/products/sn-m700/

Great value, great size (about 1.5 cm thick, 2 cm wide, and maybe 5 cm
long), great battery life (20 hours on a charge, recharges off USB),
takes SD (or Mini/Micro SD with adapter), plays Ogg Vorbis, MP3, MP4,
WMA, WAV as well as MPEG4, MPEG-1, and WMV video.

I paid less than 10,000 yen for the 512MB internal memory model.

What *is* important, however, is the comfort of the ear buds. IIRC,
those supplied with the iPod are quite reasonable. Those that came with
my current music player[0] are rather uncomfortable.
