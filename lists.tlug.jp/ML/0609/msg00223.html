On 2006/09/24, at 16:11, Charles Muller wrote:
I have recently tried experimenting with running administrative scripts from cron.daily and cron.hourly instead of my regular cron file (crontab 
-e). But the scripts I put into these folders don't seem to run.



Hello,

Do you have entries similar to these in you /etc/crontab file?

17 *  * * * root    run-parts --report /etc/cron.hourly
25 6 * * * root test -x /usr/sbin/anacron || run-parts --report / etc/cron.daily 47 6 * * 7 root test -x /usr/sbin/anacron || run-parts --report / etc/cron.weekly 52 6 1 * * root test -x /usr/sbin/anacron || run-parts --report / etc/cron.monthly 

Without these entries the scripts in those directories will not run.

Regards, Keith
