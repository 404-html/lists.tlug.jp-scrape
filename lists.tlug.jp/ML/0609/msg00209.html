scott wrote:

> Does anyone know of any simpler software that could help me with 
> [stitching images from http://watchizu.gsi.go.jp/index.aspx?mesh=5339]

Gimp or ImageMagick. 

With such cleanly cleaved sections, almost any image editor should 
work.  I don't even think of combining such cleanly cleaved images 
as stitching. With such cleanly cleaved sections, you could even 
automate the pasting. You might use scriptfu (in Gimp), or even 
ImageMagick on the command line. 

Go for the command line!
