Job Title: Software Development Engineer

Summary Description:
 Software Development Engineer, Amazon.co.jp Anywhere

 Amazon.co.jp is looking for a passionate, hard-working, and talented
software engineer interested in developing transactional software
applications and support tools for our fast-growing mobile device
commerce business.  Candidate will work on location in Shibuya, Tokyo,
Japan as part of a global Amazon Anywhere team based in Seattle,
U.S.A., working internally with software engineers, program managers,
and business owners, and externally with the Japanese keitai industry
business and technical partners.  Key tasks and initiatives include:
developing new user applications and internal tools specific to mobile
devices, supporting existing Amazon technologies on the keitai
platform, building support for next-generation mobile technologies,
and taking broad ownership and involvement in the Amazon.co.jp mobile
business.

 Qualified candidates should have experience developing highly
scalable, reliable, and complex transactional systems on-time and on
budget.  Should be extremely strong in Perl, XML, XSL, and UNIX
systems.  Experience with C/C++, Java, XHTML, object-oriented design
and analysis, relational databases, SQL, and transactional systems,
and software experience with mobile devices is a plus.  Must have good
communcation skills in written & spoken Japanese and English. An
ability to assume responsibility, and achieve results in a fast-paced
and high-pressure environment is critical.
