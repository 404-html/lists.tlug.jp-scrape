Tim Pritlove of the Chaos Computer Club will be giving a "Creative
Chaos" presentation at the Pink Cow in Aoyama on September 14,
starting at around 7:00 or 7:30pm and lasting for probably 60 to
90 minutes.

  http://www.thepinkcow.com/

It will be a great presentation, so please make an effort to be
there if you can make it. If you can attend, please e-mail me
off-list so that I can start to get an estimate of how many people
will be there.

Here again are some details about Tim:

Tim is the events organizer for the Chaos Computer Club (which
he's been involved with for more than 20 years). In particular,
he's the organizer of the annual Chaos Communication Congress
(three-day event attended by around 3000 people each year) and
Project Blinkenlights:

  http://blinkenlights.de/

Blinkenlights started out in 2001 when the CCC took a building at
the Alexanderplatz in Berlin, rigged up a system of floodlamps
behind all if its windows and turned the whole building into a
giant low-res computer screen. People walking by could use their
mobile phones to play Pong with each other on the screen and to
post messages to it. They did it again more recently, with a much
bigger building, at the Bibliothèque nationale de France in Paris.

  --Mike
