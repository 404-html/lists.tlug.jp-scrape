          TLUG - Tokyo Linux Users Group Special "nomikai" feat.
Heinz-M. Graesing
          ==============================================

     Date:   September 8th, 2006 (Friday)

     Time:   19:00-21:00

     Place:  Shinseikiya
             Hotel San Ruto Ikebukuro, B1
             Ikebukuro 1-39-4
             Phone: 03-5396-4153
             http://www.hotpepper.jp/s/H000002930/top.html (Japanese)
             http://www.hotpepper.jp/s/H000002930/map.html (Japanese)

     Event:  drink and eat (primarily a social activity)

     Costs:  pay as you go
             http://www.hotpepper.jp/s/H000002930/food.html (Japanese)

       This event is open to all Linux users and friends, and is
       to welcome Heinz to Japan and hear the fascinating story
       of his role in Treuchtlingen, Germany's migration to Linux in
       2002, making it one of the first institutions in Europe to use
       Linux exclusively.

       Even if you're not a Linux user or a LUG member, but want
       to hang out with Linux (and other Open Source minded) people,
       please come.

       Shinseikiya is within walking distance from Ikebukuro Station.
       If you get lost, or simply want to get in touch with us, please call:

           Josh Glover............080-5061-4266
