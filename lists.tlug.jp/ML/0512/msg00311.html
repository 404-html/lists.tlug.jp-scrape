I find archives to be very useful because:

1. When dealing with support for newbies/discussions I

My vote, yes.
 
I use a lot of yahoo accounts, and am part of a lot (I
mean a lot) of FSUGS/LUG mailing lists around the
world. I, personally find archives to be very useful,
and yahoo does a manageable job of moving spam (if
any) to bulk. So, I am managing discussions quite
comfortably and keep track of them.

Again, its just my thoughts. YMMV.
