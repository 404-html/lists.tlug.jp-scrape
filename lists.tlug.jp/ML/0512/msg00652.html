So glad I did not know about rename last month, I would not have had the 
pleasure of writing a shell script to move and put sensible names onto a 
stack of jpg files from my camera ;)

But contrary to Michael Smith, on my Debian/Sarge, rename is just in 
/usr/bin. And it is a very short perl script, by the man himself. 
Actually I thought things in /usr/bin were "real code", not scripts. 
Live and learn.

And I agree that the hard part is finding the tool-- yes apropos is 
great, but it just never occured to me that there was a powerful re-work 
of mv that did exactly what I had to write the shell script to do.

Thanks everyone.
