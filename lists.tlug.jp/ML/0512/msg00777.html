[tlug] Plan9port is now available in FreeBSD ports.

  • Date: Fri, 30 Dec 2005 23:27:28 -0800
  • From: Uva Coder <uva.coder@example.com>
  • Subject: [tlug] Plan9port is now available in FreeBSD ports.
Good news for the FreeBSD folks that want to use Plan 9 utilities
natively on a FreeBSD machine, see
http://www.freshports.org/devel/plan9port

According to the freshports page: "The port includes the following:

sources for Linux, FreeBSD, and SunOS

lib9 (nee libc), libString, libbin, libbio, libcomplete, libdraw,
liblibflate, frame, libfs, libhtml, libhttpd, libip, libmux, libplumb,
liblibregexp, libsec, thread, and libventi

9term, acme, hoc, plumber, rio (nee 9wm), sam, and samterm, along with
many small utilities
Plan 9 bitmap fonts
manual pages"

More on the topic can be found here as well:
http://groups.google.com/group/comp.os.plan9/browse_thread/thread/266c5f8292ef0f63/498e15a1fc49e6a3?hl=en#498e15a1fc49e6a3
