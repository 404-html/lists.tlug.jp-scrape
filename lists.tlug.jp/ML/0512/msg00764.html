anthy/scim are in Fedora extras for FC4. I don't know if CentOS has 
anything similar. Anyway, the Fedora packages should work on CentOS, I 
think. Here's what I have in /etc/yum.repos.d/fedora-extras.repo:


mirrorlist=http://fedora.redhat.com/download/mirrors/fedora-extras-$releasever
enabled=1
gpgkey=file:///etc/pki/rpm-gpg/RPM-GPG-KEY-fedora-extras
gpgcheck=1

or you could try

baseurl=ftp://ftp.kddlabs.co.jp/pub/Linux/distributions/fedora/extras/3/$basearch
enabled=1
