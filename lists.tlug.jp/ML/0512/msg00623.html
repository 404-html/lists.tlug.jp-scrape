This makes me wonder how anybody ever finds out about the
existence of rename(1) without getting informed about it on a
mailing list or something.

I mean, I read a lot of docs, and I have never come across mention
of it. For example, nothing in "Unix Help for Users" (at least
not that I know of) or in similar docs intended for newbies (the
people who might benefit most from having it).

Is it because it's just too trivial for old-schoolers to
just write a one-line bash script or function for doing it?  Or
because it's just a Perl script? 

Or maybe it is sort of a dangerous thing to publicize too much a
command for doing batch renaming, given what you might end up with
if you make a regex mistake?

  --Mike
