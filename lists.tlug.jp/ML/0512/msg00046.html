In the interest of saving bandwidth and being Ulrich's errand boy[1],
moji is a very similar beast. You activate the moji sidebar by
pressing Alt-M or selecting moji from the View-Sidebars section of the
menu bar, then highlight some text in the webpage and click either
"Kanji" or "Word" in the moji sidebar to perform the lookup.

It looks like it is still being actively developed, since the mozdev
page for it talks about Firefox 1.5, which ain't that old.
