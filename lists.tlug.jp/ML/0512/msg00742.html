vi does a limited (only in comparison to Emacs!) set of things very
well.  Within its sphere coverage is comprehensive.  Its keystroke
syntax is very regular, more so than Emacs's.  It's almost
click-and-point (you say what to do with one keystroke, then you point
at an object with another keystroke to operate on it).
