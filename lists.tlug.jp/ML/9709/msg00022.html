Hey all,

I returned from Hawaii with TriTeal's CDE for Linux, and I must say I
like it.  I have worked with IBM AIX with the TriTeal CDE manager, and
once I loaded it at home, it was exactly the same.  For those who are
looking for a professional window manager that gives you the common
desktop environment, I would recommend it.
