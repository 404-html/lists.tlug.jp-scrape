Sorry, but I didn't get a chance to watch the broadcast.  But, IMHO,
demos of the command line rarely turn out well.  Some technological
problem will ruin things some how.  But the other problem is font
size.  Showing it live versus having a slide that shows this:

>>> print "Hello, world!"
Hello, world!

is no different.  If someone in your audience doesn't believe you and
wants to see it run "live", then they have issues.  :-)  (Or, you can
do the live demo after you had gone through the slides first.)

Ray
