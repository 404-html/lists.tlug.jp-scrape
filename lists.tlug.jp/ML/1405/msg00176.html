 > actually backed off from it because it kept a copy of the entire

 > do since I do a periodic backup of changed files across my entire home
 > directory.  Perhaps there was something I could do to change my

Yeah, what I'd recommend is to put your whole home directory in git
(or some other VC, if you like svn better, say[1]), and let git worry
about backup strategy.  (git itself won't schedule automatic backups
and the like, but you can use cron or .logout for that.)
