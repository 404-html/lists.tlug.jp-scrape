Nope, tmux is not read-only. That is a nice feature that enables stuff
like this:

http://uberconf.com/blog/stuart_halloway/2010/09/notes_on_remote_pairing

and tell your pair programming buddy to login and attach to the shared
session. Now you're remote pairing!
