Yet another followup--for those using SELinux, removing nspluginwrapper
can cause problems with SELinux. 

However, testing indicates that it can be left in. 
So amend earlier instructions.  Rather than removing nspluginwrapper,
just leave it in place and only remove flash-plugin.
