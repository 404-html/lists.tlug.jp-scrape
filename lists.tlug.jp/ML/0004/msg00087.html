On Sat, 15 Apr 2000, Darren Cook wrote:

> Is there a way to monitor cpu temperature, etc. in linux (from the
> commandline)? I was in the bios this morning and noticed it had that info.
> It's an ASUS motherboard and Award BIOS.
> 
> The power supply fan keeps speeding up and slowing down, and I'm wondering
> if it is intelligently cooling. Or just about to fail.

get the lm78 stuff.  it builds as a kernel module, then you just 'modprobe
lm78' and all that kind of info is now magically stored somewhere under
/proc.  Works great if your mobo supports the lm78 spec.
