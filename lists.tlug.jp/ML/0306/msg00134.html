On Tue, 17 Jun 2003, Dan Lindfield wrote:

> Does anyone have any idea on how to get:

I have xterm set like this: 
LANG=en_US.UTF-8
LC_CTYPE="ja_JP"
LC_NUMERIC="ja_JP"
LC_TIME="ja_JP"
LC_COLLATE="ja_JP"
LC_MONETARY="ja_JP"
LC_MESSAGES="ja_JP"
LC_PAPER="ja_JP"
LC_NAME="ja_JP"
LC_ADDRESS="ja_JP"
LC_TELEPHONE="ja_JP"
LC_MEASUREMENT="ja_JP"
LC_IDENTIFICATION="ja_JP"
LC_ALL=ja_JP

open office comes up with Japanese menus. 

kinput2 is another issue, I think. 
But I have it running in openoffice, mozilla, 
etc., just by invoking it from xterm like this: 
kinput2 -canna &

under the same conditions. 

You might try something similar 
and see if you can get it to 
work that way.
