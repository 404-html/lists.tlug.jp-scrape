I have xterm set like this: 
LANG=en_US.UTF-8
LC_CTYPE="ja_JP"
LC_NUMERIC="ja_JP"
LC_TIME="ja_JP"
LC_COLLATE="ja_JP"
LC_MONETARY="ja_JP"
LC_MESSAGES="ja_JP"

LC_TELEPHONE="ja_JP"

kinput2 -canna &

under the same conditions.

and see if you can get it to 
work that way.
