The switch is a Cisco switch isn't it?
If it is, did you try to enable the Store-and-Forward switching mode? This 
will increment the latency but it will check the CRC for all the frame. I 
think it's a way to try to isolate the problem. If the corruptions stops, it 
demostrate that the cables are bad in some point.  Are you sure the cables 
are not near a high power AC font or something that can produce interference?
