However if you are going to use it on Android you have some issues to think about.
One is the data rate, which unless that SIM card has a 'packet hodai' deal, you're looking
at a 40000+yen bill easily.
That price is with a japanese android phone. If you want to use your own, since it's not
one of 'their' 'smartphones' then they will count it as using your SIM on a computer and
charge you that rate, which is almost double.
Not sure how these renting companies do it... but be careful about this.
