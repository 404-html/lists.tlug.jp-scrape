There really do seem to be a lot of people around--on this list and
elsewhere, who have switched to Ubuntu, dealing with its conception of
average user, because of lack of time--or perhaps, as we age, it's not

drag RH and Fedora into it, to point out how they're trying to be like
Windows.)

worked has stopped working, with no obvious documentation about it,
especially when one has been spoiled by FreeBSDs UPDATING section.
