They are just trying to sell the data plan. The phone works fine on
wifi alone (for data, you still need the SIM for voice). The only
problem is registering the phone in the first place without a sim. You
can do that by using the debug cable and bringing up the settings
dialog, where you can activate Wifi and continue with the registration
with that. The instructions are easy to find on the net.
