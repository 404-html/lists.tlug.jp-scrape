TLUG,

My 8GB microSDHC (class 6) card arrived today, and I'm happy to confirm
that despite DoCoMo saying that the HT-03A only goes up to 4GB, that it
seems to be handling 8GB just fine.

After I had first changed the card, I had to toggle the "USB debugging"
on and off, because the phone would not mount by USB. However, once I
did that, it mounted fine, and no connection troubles after that. I'm
guessing the change in size might have confused some saved settings on
either the phone's side, or the computer's.

It seems a little slow, actually, to transfer files to the Android.
Definitely noticeably different. Given that this is a faster class card
(the previous was 4, this is 6), is it possible the phone has issues
dealing with a larger sized card that might cause it to slow down?
