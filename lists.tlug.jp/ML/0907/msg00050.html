On Wed, 08 Jul 2009 12:27:08 +0900, scott <scott@example.com> wrote:

> I would like to set up a facebook type website on the slackisland.org

I tried with Elgg
http://elgg.org/

It's GPL and pretty stable. The only inconvenience is that the template
available are few and the template creation is a little bit hard.
I wanted to use it for a foss coders network (fosscoders.com) but by a lack
of time I still cannot do it.

>From my point of view, the advantages of elgg is the architecture, (lamp)
and the license.
Other services like ning.com slave you with a lot of restrictive licenses
and policies.

Just read elgg specs, it's huge, you can do almost anything, blogging,
forum, send messages between users, videos and so forth.

Hope this help.
