It's 3 man (31,500, IIRC) with a 2 year contract and 6 man otherwise.
If you cancel you contract you have the pay the difference to 6 man.

the maximum is 5000. The voice plans are the standard Docomo ones,
starting from 1900 or something (SS). So if don't talk much and are
online all the time, expect to pay something like 7000/month.
