Do you have all the libs and goodies that jdk needs?  You'll
notice that jdk1.1.5 has gone the way of glibc6.  This has
prompted the great migration for me.  I feel like a bird
travelling great distances in the winter.   

Anyway, you may have to upgrade some stuff.  To see the
lay of the land on your Linux plot, look at

  ldconfig -v

or you may want to do a 

  ldconfig -v |tee blains_libs.log.erase.me.after.use

and then peruse the libs you have and compare them to the
ones that jdk is dependent on.
