I know both Bic Camera and Yodobashi Camera could convert VHS tapes to
files for me -- I've done that before.  I guess they can do the same
with DVDs.

The problem is if you can't read them, I'm not so sure they can,

they can discard the originals...but I am sure they won't accept that.
 [The VHS tape I copied was a home video.  They had no problems
copying that for me to a file.]

You're best bet is to perhaps do it yourself...
