On my acer aspire one I have /boot and / on the 8G ssd, and /home on
the sd card expansion. I was glad of that when I got > 1G of xsession
errors a few weeks ago. For a decent-sized disk, though, I generally
go with /boot and /  only. In the past I have regretted giving too
much to /home and running out of space on /usr.
