   TPC/TLUG tour to Sawanoi Sake Brewery
   ===========================================

   Date: May 19 (Saturday)

   Time: 13:00 at Sawai Station (JR Ome Line)
         or 12:30 at Ome Station (JR Chuo Line)

   Cost: 4,500 - 5,000 yen

   This will be a joint activity between the Tokyo PC Users 
   group (TPC) and the Tokyo Linux Users Group (TLUG). Members 
   of these groups and their sake-loving friends are 
   welcome to participate.

   Founded in 1702, Sawanoi is one of Tokyo's most famous sake 
   breweries. It is located in the scenic Oku-Tama region, west 
   of Tokyo, where there are plenty of sources of mineral water 
   that is used in the brewing process.

   Itinerary:
   12:45 Meet at Ome Station, around the waiting room in the platform
         http://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E3%83%95%E3%82%A1%E3%82%A4%E3%83%AB:Waiting_room_of_Ome_station.jpg
   12:51 Take train on Ome Line bound to Oku-Tama
   13:05 Arrive at Sawai Station
   13:10 Walk to Sawanoi-En (Sawanoi Garden)
   13:15 Tour of the brewery
   14:15 Sake tasting
   15:00 Move to a restaurant nearby
   15:15 Eat, drink and chat with the brewery's president, Ozawa-san
   16:00 Head back to Sawai Station

   Registration:
   http://tlug.doorkeeper.jp/events/1008-tpctlug-tour-to-sawanoi-sake-brewery
