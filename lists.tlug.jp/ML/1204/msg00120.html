I've used a service, they did not care what the book was, or even that it was a hard cover.

What I got as a result though was just a very large PDF file made of images. There was an OCR service as an option but it was a joke. A plain text file with ridiculous line-breaking, there was no way to reglue it back into paragraphs. Not even if you are an awk master.

In short, I didn't get an ebook from my books. I'd stay away from it until services that claim to produce specific ebook formats appear. That would indicate that they got the OCR polished enough.
