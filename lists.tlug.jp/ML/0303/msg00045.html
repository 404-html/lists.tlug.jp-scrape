If you are interested in discussing the Zaurus PDA family
(including, but not limited to the sexy Linux-based models) be
sure to check out the Zaurus Otaku Kurabu (ZOK).

    Most ZOK members are not Japanese, yet located in Japan.
    This is hardly a membership requirement, but is often
    reflected in discussions centering around the availability
    of old and new software tools for the traditional Zaurus
    platform, along with usage hints especially for foreigners
    sometimes at odds with its proprietary OS and strictly
    Japanese GUI.
