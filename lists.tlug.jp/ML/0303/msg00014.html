On Thu, Mar 06, 2003 at 07:38:01AM -0500, Subba Rao wrote:
> Is it possible to setup different VPN connections simultaneously
> to more than one different enterprise networks?

Sure, as long as the two private subnets you want to connect to are
non-overlapping (eg 192.168.x.0/24 and 192.168.y.0/24 with x!=y).
You just need to set up two tunnels in your ipsec.conf.
