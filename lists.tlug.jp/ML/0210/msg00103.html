I used YDL 2.0 for quite a while on an old iMac. I haven't tried the 
newer versions or on older/slower hardware. You may want to check out 
Vine for ppc as well, as it is made for the Japanese market. IIRC, one 
of the recent issues of one of the Linux mags had the Vine ppc version 
on cd. Couldn't hurt to give it a whirl. Compatibilty chart is here:
http://vinelinux.org/ppc/compatibility/
