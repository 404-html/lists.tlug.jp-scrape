Also a lot of sites (especially the ones with high traffic) cap the
speed of a single link so that one broadband user doesn't suck all the
bandwidth on their site. Also some smaller sites might not have
bandwidth equal to 30Mbps for their whole site.

If you want to milk the best results from your connection you need a
download program like prozilla that will connect multiple times to a
site and each connection will dl part of the program. The windoze
equivalent is Download Accelerator.
