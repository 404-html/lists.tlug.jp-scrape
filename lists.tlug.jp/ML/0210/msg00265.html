From reading the list in this past week, I would like to remind anyone who
is about to post to TLUG of the following policies:

1) This list is not an appropriate forum for venting about how hard Linux is
   to learn / use / understand. I would suggest Microsoft advocacy lists for
   that sort of thing.

2) Top-posting is almost never appropriate.

3) Any technical question that is posted here is seldom welcome without at
   least a Google seach by the would-be questioner. And if you search
   Google but do not get results, say so. Otherwise, expect to be ignored
   at best, flamed at worst.


If you did not understand the above, please re-read the TLUG list policy
document:

http://www.tlug.jp/listpolicy.decorated.html


Scott Robbins, the internationally renowned netiquette expert, also has some
wise words for mailing list membership at:

http://home.nyc.rr.com/computertaijutsu/newbieguide.html
