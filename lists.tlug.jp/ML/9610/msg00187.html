Browsing around RedHat's web site, I found this:
 
"Attention Linux User Groups

Has your Linux User Group been looking for a way to sneak a peek at some
of the newest office automation tools for Linux? Are you looking for ways
to attract more people to meetings and events? Why not offer a copy of the
Applixware Office Suite for Red Hat Linux as a door prize at your next
meeting? Or demo it at you next event and draw even more people! Please
contact Lisa Sullivan by email at sulli@example.com for more details on how
to obtain an evaluation or door prize copy of Applixware. This offer will
only be around for a short time, so act quickly if you are interested."

A description of what Applixware is can be found at
 
	http://www.redhat.com/products/apx.html
 
If it does everything it promises, we won't need MS-Office anymore, and
we could finally delete Win95 from our hard-disks. How about contacting
RedHat and see if we can get an evalution copy for the next TLUG meeting?
