> It seems like most archive
> sites are being mirrored in country nowadays, so we shouldn't need to
> waste extra time on overseas connections.

  I'm certainly no expert of any kind on this, but my subjective 
impression is that links outside the country are vastly better (to
the U.S. anyway - not to Europe) than local ones, at least with
my 28.8 modem (may be different for those with T1).

  Also, I remember reading in "Computing in Japan" last fall, that
because of the primitive infrastructure here at lot of connections

for the course), but actually a net drain on the net (no pun intended -
really).  

  What's the story?
