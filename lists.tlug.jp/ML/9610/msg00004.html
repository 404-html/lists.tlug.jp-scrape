where the correct place is.... I've tried all the places listed in the
ghostscript docs...


I have been trying to get the Wadalab fonts to work with Ghostscript
4.01. First I tried jis encoding by defining:

/Wadalab-mincho-0-8	(Wadalab-mincho-0-8.gsf) ;

Gs cores dumps if I try the following:

/Wadalab-mincho-0-8 findfont 40 scalefont setfont
100 100 moveto <3021> show showpage

Maybe GS runs out out memmory, as it load the whole font. I tried to
understand the stack trace below, but I didn't get very far.
