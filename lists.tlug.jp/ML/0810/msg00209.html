On 21/10/08 12:12, Kenneth Burling wrote:

> /dev/sdX vs /dev/hdX is or used to be anyway a way to tell a scssi from
> an pata (ide).  Now some systems will report them all as sd something.

That's libata at work. It virtualizes all drives and hooks them up to a
driver that talks to the physical interface on one end and to the kernel
SCSI driver on the other. Thus, all drives, PATA, SATA and SCSI show up as
SCSI drives now.
