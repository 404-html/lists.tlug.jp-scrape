You are at the right point on the learning curve to benefit from reading: 

   The Practice of Programming
   by Brian W. Kernighan and Rob Pike
   Addison-Wesley
   ISBN 0-201-61586-X

   http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Practice_of_Programming

As you go through each section of the book, you'll have fun 
going through your program again making it better each time. 
It will become smaller and clearer. 

You'll come back with other questions like what are good 
ways to wait or clear the screen.
