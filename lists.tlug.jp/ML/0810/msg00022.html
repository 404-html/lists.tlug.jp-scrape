Hi TLUG,

Tomoyuki Sakurai, the organizer of the cross cultural Engineer party[1]
and frequent TLUG presenter[2][3], will be chairing a panel discussing
Japanese career development from a global perspective, and global career
development from a Japanese perspective.

The event is organized to commemorate Pasona TECHs' 10th anniversary and
will be held on Saturday 11th of October (this Saturday) from
17:00~18:30 at the UDX Gallery Akihabara.
