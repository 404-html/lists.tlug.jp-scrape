On Fri, Oct 17, 2008 at 9:06 AM, Nick Bikkal <nickbikkal@example.com> wrote:
> I have a problem downloading pictures from my digital cameras to Ubuntu.
> I lose the dates; they get changed to the moment I download from the
> camera. Is there a way to fix this setting?
It is not a good idea to download files directly from your camera to Ubuntu.
I usually plug my CF card into a card reader, mount it with "defaults"
option in /etc/fstab,
then copy files to my computer. In this way, time stamps of files are preserved.
