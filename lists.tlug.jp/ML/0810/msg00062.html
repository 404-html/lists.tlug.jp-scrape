The next meeting of the Tokyo Society for the Application of
Currying[1], a technical group interested in functional programming,
including languages such as Haskell, will be held at 7:30 p.m. on
Thursday, October 9th, 2008 at the new offices[2] of Starling Software
in Nishi-Shinjuku, Tokyo. Curt Sampson and Stuart Croy have recently
returned from ICFP 2008[3] and will be giving an overview of what was
interesting there and answering questions. Copies of the proceedings
will be available for perusal.

Please note that the new office is in the same building, but at a
different number; it's moved from suite 904 to 201. This is a much
larger space than the previous one, so there should be no worries about
crowding.

[1] http://www.starling-software.com/en/tsac.html
[2] http://www.starling-software.com/en/access.html
[3] http://www.icfpconference.org/icfp2008/index.html
