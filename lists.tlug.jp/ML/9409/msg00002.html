Re: Seyon.

If you are envoking Seyon from the menu (using openwin), then you can add the
switches and params to the menu.  The following is
/usr/openwin/lib/openwin-menu-t:

"Tools" TITLE PIN

"Xfilemanager (File Manager)"		exec /usr/bin/X11/xfilemanager
"Xfm 1.2 (File Manager)"		exec /usr/bin/X11/xfm
"Xman (View Manual Pages)"  		exec /usr/bin/X11/xman
"Seyon (Communications Package)"	exec /usr/bin/X11/seyon -modem /dev/modem
"Xcalc (Calculator)"        		exec /usr/bin/X11/xcalc
"Xspread (Spreadsheet)"	    		exec /usr/bin/X11/xspread
"Xxgdb (Debugger)"	    		exec /usr/bin/X11/xxgdb
"Xconsole (Console messages)"   	exec /usr/bin/X11/xconsole
"Xmag (Magnifying glass)"   		exec /usr/bin/X11/xmag
"Clocks" MENU				$OPENWINHOME/lib/openwin-menu-clocks

--- end of /usr/openwin/lib/openwin-menu-t

and I just call it from the openwin menu.
