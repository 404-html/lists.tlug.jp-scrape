You can use pfSense as your internet access router and put FreeSwitch on that, there is an install and setup procedure for this within the pfSense GUI. If you have this on both ends, you can do edge-to-edge IPsec.

[SIP-client] --LAN-- [FreeSwitch/pfSense] --IPsec/WAN-- [FreeSwitch/pfSense] --LAN-- [Sip-client]

In addition to being vastly more secure, this is also far more reliable and of superior quality than any free cloud service could ever give you. The downside is that you will frequently have to call people who do not have IPsec, nor a SIP client at their end, so you still have to be ready for Skype/Google/Whatsapp or whatever the other party is using.
