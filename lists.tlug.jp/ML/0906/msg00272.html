But the pathology in question is not Linux or open source, it's GNU,
which is not responsible for Linux (the GNU kernel is the still
unusable HURD), and apt to get really hinky if you suggest that they
have any relation to open source.  GNU is about "free software" as
defined by a cult figure, whose more general views about freedom are
pretty disgusting to me (eg, he believes that laws to protect children
of any age from pedophiles are anti-freedom and should be repealed,
search for "paedophile" in his blog).

The basic belief that true-GNU supporters have about software is
extremely communistic.  They believe that no harm can possibly be done
by denying property rights in software, and that someone who
contributes a gift that goes on giving forever can be adequately
rewarded with wage slavery.  The cult figure himself doesn't hesitate
to compromise his beliefs by refusing to apply them to "near software"
(documentation and and entertainment data), using economic arguments
that he has rejected many times for anything written in C.  And,
interestingly enough, even within free software the true-GNUbies have
chosen the least free alternative.
