if there is no driver to install then it's very likely a usb mass
storage device.

> I googled a bit but came up empty, and they seem rather new so probably

Not that specific one but in my experience (cameras and psp) sony
gadgets present themselves as mass storage devices. They may have some
restriction related to the directory layout but nothing more than that.
Sorry I can't confirm what you ask for though. :P

plug the walkman in to see what happens. Even using a wintendo computer
you should be able to see[1] if the usb mass storage driver is the one
owning the device.
