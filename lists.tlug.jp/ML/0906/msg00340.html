2009 TLUG Officer Elections
-----------------------------

We will be holding the elections for TLUG officers at the July 11th
technical meeting. The positions are as follows.

* President
* Vice President
* Treasurer

If you are interested in becoming an officer or have questions about
what is involved, you can address them to me, to the admin list, or to
the other officers[1].  If you are interested in challenging the
incumbent officers,  please post your candidacy to the tlug-admin
mailing list by July 4rd.

Edward Middleton

1. http://www.tlug.jp/wiki/TLUG:Organization
