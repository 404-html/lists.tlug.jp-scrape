> TLUG,
> My Canon Pixus iP3100 printer is no longer printing colour correctly. It 
> prints everything in shades of blue.
> Well... black and white printouts still come out fine. Just if there's 
> any colour, it goes off.
>   

What makes people annoyed is that ink jet or bubble jet
printer that is trademarked by Canon requires regular cleaning.
If you have left the printer for a year without cleaning
or printing, chances are in many cases the printing head died.

Some printer makers like HP have one that you can put a
new color ink cartridge inside separately and even the head
inside separately.  I recommend purchasing some printer from
the vendor.
