Dave M G wrote:

> I am developing a web page that is designed for viewing on an Android phone.

Think broader; don't limit yourself to just writing for just 
Android (or an iP(hone|ad)). Write your web page so that it works 
well on all RFC compliant browsers. 

If you have found an problem in a browser, report it as such 
to the maintainers, but make sure it's an problem in their browser 
beforehand, not a misunderstanding of the browser or RFCs on your part. 

> I asked this question on the Android developer mailing list, but it's 
> gone ignored, quite possibly because they think it's beneath them to answer.

Be careful about attributing motives. 

   http://catb.org/~esr/faqs/smart-questions.html#id383575
