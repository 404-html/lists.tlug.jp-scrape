    FB> Short version: Is there a PDF->text stripper of some sort that
    FB> will work with Japanese PDF files encoded in Shift-JIS?

I don't know about the Shift-JIS, but I don't see why that would
matter to ps2ascii (despite the name), distributed with Aladdin
Ghostscript, or to pstotext, whose provenance I don't know but one of
whose authors is Russell Lang, who is a wheel in the Ghostscript
world.  ps2ascii produces nicer formatting, pstotext is better at
guessing about word boundaries in ASCII.

If they don't work, let me know and I'll have a hack at it.  I don't
have a Shift JIS PDF offhand, an URL for testing would be cool.
