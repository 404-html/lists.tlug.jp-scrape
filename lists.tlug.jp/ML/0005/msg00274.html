                INTERNET THINK TANK - 7th FORUM MEETING

The Seventh ITT gathering is scheduled for Tuesday, May 30 from
6:15pm to 8:00pm at the Westin Hotel, Level B1, Kiri Room.

*Invite others to meet with Guest Speaker, Jim Hunter of IMRglobal*
________________________________________________________________________
MEETING DETAILS

Time:           6:15pm - 8:00pm
Date:           Tuesday, May 30
Location:       Westin Hotel, Level B1, Kiri Room
Directions:     from JR Ebisu station, take Sky Walk to Ebisu Garden
                Square.  Cross the Ebisu Garden Square to Westin Hotel.
                Take escalator down to level B1 to the Kiri room.

Admission:      4,000 yen (Students: 2,000 yen)
                Please provide business card at Registration table.

Host: Hasshi Sudler

Any questions regarding time and location,
please email postmaster@example.com
or call 090-8312-3156.
________________________________________________________________________
MEETING AGENDA

Reception from 6:15pm-7:00pm: snacks and drinks available

ITT meeting: 7:00pm - 8:00pm

Opening Remarks (Hasshi Sudler): Sharing data & Moving business

Presentation 1: Applying Web based Project Management and Processes
(Guest Speaker, Jim Hunter)

Jim Hunter, Project Management specialist, will explore the world of
project management and explain the impacts of web based project
management to organizational processes.  Jim will walk us through
the elements of project management.  He will also look at Microsoft's
recent web based Project Office application and how it can enhance your
business.  An extremely valuable presentation for start up firms using
project management.  Also critical for professionals seeking to learn
how Microsoft's web based Project Office can change the way your
organization manages projects.

About the Speaker:
Jim Hunter, based in Tokyo Japan, is Uvae President of the Business
Technologies Consulting group at IMRglobal Corp., Ltd. He has over 25
years of experience as an international project and business manager.
He served as a visiting professor at the University of California,
Los Angeles (UCLA) while managing large-scale IT projects for global
organizations such as Time-Warner, M&M Mars, and Shell Oil.
Jim Hunter is the author of numerous technical articles ranging from
Implementation of an International Data Transfer Network, for the
Micronesian Government, to EIS Strategy for Legacy Systems, for the
Saudi Arabian Computer Society.


Presentation 2:  Internet Think Tank, Inc. - "Incubating the Net"
(Hasshi Sudler, ITT)

President of Internet Think Tank, Inc. will introduce ITT's
products, services, incubation activities and long term strategies.
ITT was recently formally establishing in Tokyo, Japan as an
Internet information provider and incubator firm.  Learn how your
company may benefit from ITT's information and incubation services.
