> In a nutshell, what is the difference between Emacs and Xemacs. 

Xemacs -> a bit more user-friendly in x, shipped with more packages (but
this could be seen as bloat).

Other than that, there are some different commands and keybindings.
It's only a matter of tastes, anyway.
I like Emacs more, but I use Xemacs more because it's easier for me (as
beginner).
