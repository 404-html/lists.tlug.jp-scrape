On Tue, 30 May 2000 limacat@example.com wrote:
> > Wrong interpretation of $. Hint:
>  
> > son-of-bofh:~$ fuser -v /dev/dsp
> > son-of-bofh:~$ apropos fuser
> 
> By the way, on RH 6.1 (at least) it is a command that gives out output
> only if used by root.

Like many handy commands usable by any user (such as ifconfig and
traceroute), it's not root-only, it's just stashed in the sbin directory.
You can do what I do (which is tack /sbin and /usr/sbin onto your path),
or type it directly:

$ /sbin/fuser -v /dev/dsp
