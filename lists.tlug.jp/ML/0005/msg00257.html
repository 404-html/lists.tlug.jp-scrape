XFree86 -- see 
http://club.pep.ne.jp/~mkhatto/LTGP/linux.make.boxs-1.html
(in Japanese). This could, however, be an outdated writeup.

If by any luck Power 9100 refers to the Weitek Power 9100 chipset, then
it is supported.
