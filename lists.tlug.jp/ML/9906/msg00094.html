> I had one of these when I was in Japan.. works as a standard serial
> device as far as Linux is concerned.  Just make sure you recompile ppp.o
> (or the whole kernel if monolithic) and take out that one line that
> turns off the header compression or what have you... AIWA's firmware is
> broken.. windows doesn't care because it doesn't do the optimizations,
> but under Linux you'll get consistent 50% packet loss.
