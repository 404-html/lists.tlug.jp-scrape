trying to get japanese e-mail to work correctly with kinput2 and some
flavor of vi or vim, I've given up and moved to Xemacs-Mule, which seems
to handle japanese with nary a hitch. Unless you count learning emacs.

mailer for Xemacs that can also handle japanese text intelligently.
