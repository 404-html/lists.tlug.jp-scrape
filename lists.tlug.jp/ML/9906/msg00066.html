Hello everyone,

The TLUG MUD(Multi Usere Dungeon) is now officially open.  It is meant to be 
a nice place for TLUG'ers and other lovers of Linux and Free Software to 
hang out and have fun.  Accounts are currently open for everyone, so come 
and join in.

To access the TLUG MUD, telnet to www.tlug.gr.jp port 4000.  Please, Please, 
PLEASE follow the policy (Once in the game type 'policy') lest we have to 
chage it to something much stricter.  Cases of abuse should be reported to 
any one of the Implementors(See 'wizlist') who will deal with them as they 
see fit.

Although there might be no one else on, you can still explore the large 
world.. (Yes there are monsters and other NPCs, and the Cleric's Guild does 
have a bar.. ;-) )

If you have any questions please feel free to e-mail me.

Yours,
Austin K. Kurahone
