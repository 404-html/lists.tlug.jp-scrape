Since I am known as a champion of the "anything-but-sendmail" MTA,
I should mention my latest favorite.  Exim <URL http://www.exim.org/>
is very easy to configure, has some very good documentation, and a
helpful author.  It is also more "traditional" in handling configuration
and things like .forward files than my previous favorite, Qmail
<URL http://www.qmail.org/>

Exim also comes with a simple X monitoring tool that allows you to watch
the status of Exim and the various message queues.  Good for hours of fun.
:-)
