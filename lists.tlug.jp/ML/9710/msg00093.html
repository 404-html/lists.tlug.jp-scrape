Fernando,

   Don't try to change the ethernet address in the NVRAM. That
   particular address is guaranteed to be unique.

   On disks... Check out the Akihabara map at:-

	http://www.impress.co.jp/akibamap/map/map_00.html

   Look at block B2. The central block (behind Laox) has a shop
   marked as "OA Recycle" when you click on the enlarged view. If you
   actually go there, there are two shops next door to each other
   on the *second* floor (look for the narrow stairway beside the
   cogs painted on the wall). Those shops both usually have a good
   selection of second-hand and new hard drives.
