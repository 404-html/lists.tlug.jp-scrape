I saw some neat demonstrations at this summer's 1394 conference.  They
can do it, but you need a lot of storage bandwidth.  (I've also heard 
that some European countries are legislating limits on the digital stream
from the cameras... so be careful buying one in Germany, say.)

Miro talked about their system (based on a host adapter using the TI
chips) at a recent Tokyo PC Users Group meeting.  Canon and Epson are
using the Adaptec solution.
