Hi All,

I've been saving my favorite TLUG messages to a mailbox for over a year now and
it was getting harder and harder to find messages I wanted to re-read. The
messages were stored as 1, 2, 3, etc so grepping through them was the only way
to find something.

Enter MHonArc, a perl email to html converter, which I've decided I love. It's
available from ftp://hhobel.phl.univie.ac.at/MHonArc

It converts either the above mentioned archive style or mbox style archives to a
dated and threaded html archive of your mail. It's great!

If TLUG ever gets it's own server I promise to make all the archives of TLUG
mail available on the web....

Regards,
