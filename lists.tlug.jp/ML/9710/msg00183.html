> You don't really want the writes to be 'separate', since you want one
> ISO9660 file system that contains the contents for the entire disc.
> When writing the next session, you need to get mkisofs to merge what
> was already in the other sessions (-M).

  Thanks for the tip, Jim.  I'll try using the free-standing 'mkisofs',
although there may be a hitch even with this.  I see the manpage remarks:

              "Support  for  this  is not yet 100% complete,

              mkisofs  and cdwrite in order to determine the next
              writable address on the cdrom." 

  I'm also not sure that 'cdwrite' supports my Yamaha vt 200 Writer.
I'll keep you posted though.
