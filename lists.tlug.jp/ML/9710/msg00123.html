Hi,
I was going through the mailing list admin to dereg a user
and I stumbled across some stats.  There are 119 members on the
main TLUG mailing list.  Of these 49 end in .jp and 16 are from
a provider like TWICS or GOL that offers a .com e-mail address,
but caters mainly to people living in Japan.  So, there are
at least 65 TLUG members in Japan.  In addition there are several
e-mail addresses like tibco.com and sbi.com where the TLUG member
is using the mail server on an U.S. company, but is physically
in Japan. 

Also, I didn't look at the digest members.  So, I would say
that a rough estimate would place over 80 TLUG members in Japan.
Amazing, we continue to grow.
