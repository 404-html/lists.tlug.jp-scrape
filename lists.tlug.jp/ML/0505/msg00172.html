I agree with Chuck that you should look in the archives, to which I can 
now add that my Samsung SCX-4100 laser printer, stand alone copier 
(enlarge/shrink, 2 up, etc), and scanner works great with CUPS. Debian 
is not explicity supported and the installer complains, but can be 
pushed to install anyway. Its a great little machine. But no fax modem, 
not that I want one.

Just pick one up the next time you are out of the country, because they 
are not sold here, AFAI can find out.

I tried hard to find a non-ink jet, reasonable priced unit here, 
including calling some gaijin specialists, and concluded that there was 
nothing at all Linux friendly, and indeed nothing at all close to the 
us$ 145 price, after the $50 rebate. (for a laser, and a scanner, and a 
stand-alone copier-- incredible.)
