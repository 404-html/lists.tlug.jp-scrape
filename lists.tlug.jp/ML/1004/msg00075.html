Reminder:
As a general rule, the Technical Meetings are held on the 2nd Saturday of the month and the Nomikai Meetings are usually held on the 3rd Friday of even numbered months. There are, of course, exceptions to this rule, so it is best to keep an eye on the mailing list, or check back the meeting calendar - http://tlug.jp/wiki/Meeting_calendar_2010
