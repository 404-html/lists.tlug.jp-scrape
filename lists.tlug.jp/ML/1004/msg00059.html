I had similar requirements two years ago, when i bought 
a LaserJet 2605DN. Setup with HPLIP is quite straight forward,
printing quality good, and fast. The footprint isnt so small
(a bit wider than A4), so i wouldnt put it onto a tower case.

The only trouble i had was that the dublexer unit. Depending
on what kind of document i printed, it used the dublexer or not.
I couldnt figure out why so far. But i guess it is some bug
in CUPS, though it could be fixed already as i didnt use the printer
in about a year or two.
