I am a real newbie at Linux, and I usually only lurk, but I have built all
of my desktop machines by ordering parts from Tokyo and Osaka (I have only
two at the moment). My advice would be an Asus board, all the way. The
service is really great, and Asus users on the net are very enthusiastic and
helpful. Look at TwoTop
　example.com
, since they have build-it-yourself kits that include case, power supply,
floppy, motherboard, CPU, CD, and sometimes VGA.
