On 18-Nov-98 Ulrike Schmidt wrote:
> Does anyone know of a program that chops texts into sentences, and,

> And of a program that transforms verbs, nouns, adverbs into their

You may want to have a look at the info on the British National Corpus page.
They have some info on a program, CLAWS, which parses text and produces a
tagged SGML file. I could find any info on where to get this program, or even
if it is available -- but I didn't spend too long searching.

also there was some stuff about grammatical tagging

http://info.ox.ac.uk/bnc/whay/gramtag.html

Not supprisingly the British National Corpus only deals with English.
