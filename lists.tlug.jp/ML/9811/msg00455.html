I was faced with the problem today to reboot a machine 
running xdm without knowing the root password.
I tried a couple of things and the first thing that worked
was switching to another virtual terminal (Ctrl-ALT-F1 (or
maybe it was some other F-key)) and press the good
old Ctrl-Alt-Del.
