  ANNOUNCEMENT:    TLUG and FRIENDS NOVEMBER 20th NOMIKAI

************************************************************************


Date:     November 20th, 1998
Time:     19:30 inside of Tengu 
Place:    Tengu TokyoEkiMae
          Chuo-ku, Kyobashi 1-1-6, EchiZenYa Bld. B1/B2 
          Phone: 03-3275-3691
Events:   drink and eat (primarily a social activity)
Costs:    Will be divided up among participants.  Usually around 3,000 yen.


Hi Everyone,

It's been an active and interesting time for TLUG over the past month, and
it's time for a party!  The 10th Bi-monthly TLUG Nomikai is on November
20th, at Tokyo Station.  This event is open to all Friends of Linux, and
Friends of Linux Users.  The goal is to to promote casual linux (and
non-linux) related discussions over good food and a few cold ones.

Even if you're not a Linux user or a TLUG member, but want to hang out
with Linux people, please come.  We have reservations for 25 people, but
more can squeeze in.

If you don't know where Tengu is, someone will be waiting at Tokyo station
Yaesu chuo ticket gate at 19:15.  Here is a picture of Yaesu Chuo ticket
gate: 
   http://tlug.linux.or.jp/9712/tldec00.jpg

If you're coming, send e-mail to tlug-admin@example.com just to let
us know so that we can reserve space at Tengu.  If you want to come at the
last minute and don't send an e-mail, it's okay - we may have to squeeze a
bit, but please come! 

Tengu is about 5 minutes away from the Yaesu Chuo exit of the JR Tokyo
train station.  It is on the right hand side of Yaesu Dori if you are
walking out of the station.  If you are walking out of Yaesu exit, turn
the map upside down. 

If you are coming via the underground mall, look for the Starbucks across
from McDonalds, and take the next exit on the left up to ground level. 

This is a picture of the Tengu sign in front of the Tengu we are going to. 
   http://www.imasy.or.jp/~tam/off/TLUG01/jpg/r0100070.jpg

If you have a digital camera, please bring it and take pictures.  

See you there!
