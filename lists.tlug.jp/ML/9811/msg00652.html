This can happen if you reinstall a TurboLinux server (at least the
beta versions) because it wipes your XF86Config.  It can also happen
if you use a 3-button mouse on the desktop and the (invariably)
2-button device on the notebook itself.

The culprit I have in mind is the presence or lack of Emulate3Buttons
or ChordMiddle in the XF86Config.

    ash> Hmm, that is strange. The other day I suddenly was not able

    ash> hitting a mouse button. It went away when I restarted X. I
    ash> don't have the foggiest as to what went wrong....

This sounds like something changed your translations or keyboard map.
Possibly unrelated to the above, especially if it was all menus.
