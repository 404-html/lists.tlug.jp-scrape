It's trying to kill gpm, and not having any luck.  GPM, the program
that gives you a mouse cursor in a console, and X windows have
traditionally not agreed very well over the mouse.  X locking up
could probably give gpm a fit, and make it stubborn about closing
nicely.  Try switching virtural consoles (cntrl-alt-F3), logging in,
and forcing gpm to be killed , and maybe the program trying to kill
it.  Your shutdown should continue a little behind schedule.  (hey,
