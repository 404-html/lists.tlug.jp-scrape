On Fri, 13 Nov 1998, Howard Abbey wrote:

> How to find out what programs are running:
> ps -aux  | more
> 
> Look for one with either pop or imap in the name.

That won't work when the daemons in question are being spawned by inetd.

Look in /etc/inetd.conf (or /etc/xinetd.conf, depending on how well your
distribution was put together) for pop and imap services.
