I've just been chastised for spamming the list with my moving sale post.

I apologize sincerely if my post offended or bothered anyone.  It was
intended as a somewhat tongue-in-cheek offer to members of a community I
used to feel a part of.  Lately, however, I tire of the rigid rules and
splitting of the lists, and it seems every other thread quickly becomes
confrontational.  What used to feel like a group of friends with a
common hobby now has a strangely different atmosphere.  It's time to
take a break.

I hate to make such a public departure, but I really did want to
apologize before I went, and I wanted to leave people with a way to get
hold of me if they wish.  My last day at Auspex is 12/4, so please use
my personal address to send me mail: "rw@example.com".  I will probably
re-subscribe to the tlug lists sometime in January after I've moved to
the U.S.

Best regards, (and Happy Thanksgiving to any Americans in the crowd!)
