On Thu, 12 Nov 1998, Andrew S. Howell wrote:

> Both libdl.so and libdl.so.1 are symlinks to libdl.so.1.9.5. Not good
> I would guess... What should libdl.so be?

Should be a symlink to libdl.so.2, which will be a symlink to
libdl-2.0.x.so

> So I guess a rebuild to several things is in the works. Maybe I just
> move libgdbm.so to another directory for now, and add it to
> LD_LIBRARY_PATH when required.

You can safely delete libgdbm.so, as long as libgdbm.so.blah still exists
-- the .so is only used by the linker, the .so.blah is used at run-time.
