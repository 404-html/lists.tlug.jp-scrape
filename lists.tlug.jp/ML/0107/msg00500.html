> Hello,
> 
> Every time I start up KDE, it plays a BGM
> (starting theme). And it's really to loud. I tried 
> to low down the volume with the "control center",
> couldn't make it. Can someone please tell me how 
> to control the volume. Thanks in advance.
> It's KDE 2.1.1/RH7.1.

How is your /dev/mixer device setup? Does anyone outside of root have
permission to use it? If your permissions are correct try to make sure
that both the regular volume and pcm volume are set low enough. I use
xaumix to do set mine.
