> The only thing I cant figure out is how to switch from 10 to 100Mbps.

What's on the other end?  10/100 devices are normally auto-sensing, so
they negotiate the highest speed possible.  If the device you're
connecting too is not 100 megabit capable, or it is but it's locked
to 10 megabit speed, 10 megabits will be the speed of the
conection.
