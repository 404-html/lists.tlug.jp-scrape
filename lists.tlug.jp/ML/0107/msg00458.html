> > So how to I get sed or vi to concatenate all lines within a paragraph 
>into
> > one linebut keep the empty lines between paragraphes? (i.e. concatenate 
>all
> > consecutive non-empty lines together)?
>
>$ cat -s input.txt | sed join.sed
>
>join.sed is: BEGIN--cut here--cut here
>#n
>
>/^ *$/!{
>	H
>}
>/^ *$/{
>	x
>	s/\n/ /g
>	s/^ //
>	p
>}
>${
>	x
>	s/\n/ /g
>	s/^ //
>	p
>}
>END--cut here--cut here
>
>Don't forget '#n', or use 'sed -n' instead.
