> Otherwise, you have a car and power is not an issue, just use inverter.

Inverting 12V up to 230V (or whichever mains voltage you're used to)
and down to the twentyish the arbitrary laptop wants is a huge waste
of power. It's better to use a switched-mode DC-to-DC converter from
12V to twentyish directly, as the thing won't spend most of the power
consumed heating up the cabin.
