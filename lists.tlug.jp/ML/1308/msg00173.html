Speaking as a lexicographer, I view the Japanese Wikipedia as a very useful
source for the initial stages of developing a dictionary entry for hitherto
unrecorded terms. Sometimes the articles can be a bit quirky and off-target,
and the XXXXとは.... explanations certainly need checking elsewhere, but
they are correct and relevant most of the time. They are often more lucid
than the explanations found in places like 広辞苑 and 大辞林.
