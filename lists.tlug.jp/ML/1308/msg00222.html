> Just thought of something!  The reason I say "salespeople" is due to
> the situation of going from person to person, place to place, and
> getting a *lot* of practice interacting with a wide range of people in
> a wide range of settings.  Being very busy with travel, etc., they
> don't have much time to sit down and think.  For people in positions
> in life with both a fair amount of verbal experience and some quiet

become salespersons due to the nature of the work not requiring that
skill, or do salespersons in general lose their mastery of the written
word due to not requiring that skill? Which one is the cause, and
which one is the effect?
