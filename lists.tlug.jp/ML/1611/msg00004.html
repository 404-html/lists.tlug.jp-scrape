I just quickly wanted to thank everyone for coming and hope I didn't make my presentation too long.
In case anyone wants to try some of the things I showed, here's the software I used: 

* Jack (I suggest using the qjackctl GUI): http://www.jackaudio.org

* Ardour: https://ardour.org
* Hydrogen: http://hydrogen-music.org/hcms/
* Black Pearl drum kit for Hydrogen: https://musical-artifacts.com/artifacts/131 
* LinuxSampler: http://www.linuxsampler.org
* Salamander drum kit (used with LinuxSampler): https://github.com/endolith/Salamander-Drumkit 
* Calf plugins: http://calf-studio-gear.org
* Guitarix: http://guitarix.org
* The tone I used in Guitarix: https://musical-artifacts.com/artifacts/83
* Rakarrack: http://rakarrack.sourceforge.net
* Audacity: http://www.audacityteam.org
Hardware I used: FocusRite Scarlett 2i2 https://us.focusrite.com/usb-audio-interfaces/scarlett-2i2
