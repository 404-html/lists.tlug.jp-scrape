Alberto has arranged for us to use a projector during next Friday's TLUG
anniversary gathering. We would like to have a slideshow showing some of
our history. I've collected a lot of photos from our website and have
add a few of my own, but would welcome contributions.

In rough order of preference:

1) submit links where a script can download photos

https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1kiWxS_TO5d1Z9bKb8xR0x0A6iTwL5GkH76AgZvmpchg/viewform

2) if you have lots of photos, email me a CSV file of rows containing:
  - URL where the script can fetch the photo
  - short caption
  - photographer

3) send me a pointer to your gallery

4) share a directory of photos on Google Drive

5) email me the photos directly

Please only send photos that you are willing to have openly published on
the Internet.

Thanks!
