Hi,

  I have two 1.5TB hard drives (3.5”, WD Green) that I don’t need any more.  I’m trying to decide what to do with them, since right now they are just sitting on my desk.  

  I live in Tokyo, so I don’t have much room.  I’ve always thought about getting a NAS solution of some sort, but really my desktop (only two drive bays, it is a media style case made to be silent, and does a very good job of it.  Both bays are full, with an SSD and 4TB hard drive that replace the two drives in question) suffices.  If I had lots of room to spare, I would put a NAS up in a closet somewhere, but that isn’t the case.

  I don’t think I could put the drives to productive use at home.  I don’t want to just throw them out.  I’m pretty sure they would burn (given a hot enough fire, they never really specify how hot something has to be to be considered 燃え-able) but don’t want to throw them out.

  I guess I could resell them, but that sounds like a lot of work.  I don’t know how long it has been since I’ve logged in to Yahoo auctions, maybe Amazon.co.jp would be ok, but I’m lazy and don’t have boxes for shipping.  

  Are there places I could go to recycle these?  Are there people on this list who live in bigger houses than mine and want some old hard drives?

Dave E.
