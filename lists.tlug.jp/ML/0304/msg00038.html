The other day someone posted about Mandrake 9, install without options, inability to individually define mountpoints, automount on/off not possible etc., etc.

Right I am sitting here in from of the Mandrake 9.1 install, and the fourth point of the menu after Language, License and Security is: Partitioning. The entire variety of options is there.
