good. I do not support any sinister company and what is "W"?
I work as a GNU/Linux system administration and software development teacher. 
In Spain the administrative and university software is little by little 
changing to open source solutions. Two regions of Spain one is Extremadura 
and the other is Andalucia are developping two Linux distributions 
respectively:
Linex http://www.linex.org/
Guadalinex: http://www.guadalinex.org/
Fortunately politicians are understanding the importance of "libre" software 
and the bureaucratic machine is moving to a free solution. The government is 
implanting GNU/Linux in schools, hospitals, universities and the plans are to 
convert everything.
Other regions of Spain have in a short future plans to do the same.
