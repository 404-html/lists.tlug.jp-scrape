The point is that it's a Red Queen's race---all the running you can do
is barely enough to keep you in the same place.  As I pointed out to
Patrick, Japan has the highest rate of business closure in the OECD,
and a relatively large proportion of small businesses are run by
people who are willing to let them drain their net worth (or don't
keep good enough accounts to know that's happening).

Running a small business on Walmart margins is like drinking and
driving---friends don't let friends do it.
