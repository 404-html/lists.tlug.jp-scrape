To make it work properly, you need to apply a patch. Can't recall the
URL, but should be easy to find. I think there was even a link from
www.mutt.org. You also need a jp SLANG library, which I think is the
reason why you can't even read japanese.
This is caused by the fact that our Terminals use EUC as the charset
and E-Mail is sent ISO-2022-JP (JIS). EUC is mainly JIS (7-bit) with
the 8th bit set so it usually shows on terminals correctly if the chars
are let thourgh untouched.
