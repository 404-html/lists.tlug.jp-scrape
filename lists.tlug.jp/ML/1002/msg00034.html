A few months ago Amazon started building a team in Tokyo to support
its Web Services (EC2/S3/...) and part of their effort is to launch an
official AWS User Group. At the kick-off meeting two weeks ago it was
decided to launch a series of benkyoukai / study group meetings. The
first one  is scheduled for Tuesday, February 23.

While AWS Evangelist Jeff Barr will be giving the main presentation in
English most of the event will take place in Japanese. Please contact
us if you would be interested in giving a lightning talk.
