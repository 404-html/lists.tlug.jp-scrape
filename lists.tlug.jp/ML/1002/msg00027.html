On 2010-02-02 09:50 +0000 (Tue), Godwin Stewart wrote:

> I /wanted/ to get the PRS-505 but was too slow off the mark. I didn't have

Actually, the 600 is really the "upgrade" of the PRS-700; the 300 is
more or less the newer version of the 505, unless you wanted the (only
physically) larger screen or the ability to use card storage.

> The extra, touch-sensitive layer on the screen reduces the contrast of

Trust me, it's nowhere near as bad as the 700 was. :-)

But yeah, the non-touchscreen E-Ink displays are pretty much around
mass-market paperback quality, and you don't get that cramp in your hand
when you're holding the book open one-handed.
