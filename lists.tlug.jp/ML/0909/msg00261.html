On Sun, Sep 27, 2009 at 03:37:15AM +0900, Edward Wright wrote:
> Anyone know how to print using a gtk-2 app without using CUPS?

And, to answer my own question 'case it's useful to someone

add

gtk-print-backends="pdf,cups,lpr"

to 

$HOME/.gtkrc-2.0 or /etc/gtk-2.0/gtkrc

per:
https://bugzilla.redhat.com/show_bug.cgi?id=232009

Thanks for listening.
