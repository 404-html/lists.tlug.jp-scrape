Hello TLUGers,

I'm uploading past tech meetings videos (since 2006) on vimeo at
http://www.vimeo.com/user2115772 and later will also add them to
http://www.vimeo.com/groups/tlugvideos. Below is a list of currently
uploaded videos, but only those with '+' are currently publicly
available after I got permission from presenters. I'd like to get an
'ok' from other presenters before making their presentations public
(maybe somebody would like to 'camouflage' a bit their real names).
Please let me know here or send an email to tlug.videos@example.com
Also, let me know if you have a vimeo account and want to be properly
"credited".
People who have presented in 2008-2009 can also manifest themselves if
they agree with the format.
I've been quite light on tagging, so any help/suggestions are welcome.
Same goes for general comments.

cheers,
Ivan
