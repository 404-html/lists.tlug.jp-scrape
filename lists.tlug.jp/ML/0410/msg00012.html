         TLUG - Tokyo Linux Users Group August "nomikai" 
         ===============================================

       Date:     October 15, 2004 (Friday)
       Time:     19:00-21:00
       Place:    Tengu New-Shinbashi-ten
                 Minato-ku, Shinbashi 2-6-4, Amakasu Bldg. B1
                 Phone: 03-3508-9448
                 http://gourmet.yahoo.co.jp/gourmet/restaurant/Kanto/Tokyo/guide/0401/P059837.html

       Event:    drink and eat (primarily a social activity)

       Costs:    will be divided up among participants
                 (usually around 3,000~4,000 yen)

       This event is open to all Linux users and friends, and is
       to promote casual Linux (and non-Linux) related discussions
       over a few cold ones. Even if you're not a Linux user or a
       LUG member, but want to hang out with Linux people, please come.

       Even if you want to come at the last minute it's okay - we
       may have to squeeze a bit, but please come!

       Tengu is about 2 minutes away from the Hibiya exit of the JR Sinbashi
       train station or 1 minute from the exit 8 of the Shinbashi Metro subway
       station. If you get lost, or simply want to get in touch with us, please 
       call:

          Zev Blut............090-5778-8067
          Alberto Tomita......070-5644-3680 (PHS)

       For more information, access the TLUG web page at http://www.tlug.jp

       See you there!
