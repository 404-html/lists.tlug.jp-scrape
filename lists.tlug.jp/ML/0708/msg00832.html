Stephen J. Turnbull wrote:
> The MS Mincho fonts do Japanese and Czech.
> I assume Arial does, I don't have it at the moment.

The version of Arial that comes with the Core Fonts does not
contain CJK glyphs, but it works well with substitution.
http://www.microsoft.com/typography/fonts/font.aspx?FID=8&FNAME=Arial

Arial Unicode MS supports CJK, though.
http://www.ascendercorp.com/msfonts/arial_unicode.html

Those who are forced to buy a "free" copy of Windows on a laptop
may benefit from foraging up some fonts before wiping the drive.
The fonts have much more value that the OS! ;)

Cheers, Travis
