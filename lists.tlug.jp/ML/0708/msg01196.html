  • Subject: [tlug] Volunteers for Listmaster?
I have overstayed my welcome as Listmaster, and would like to hand off
the position to someone new.

The details of the position are here:

http://www.tlug.jp/wiki/TLUG:Organization#Listmaster_2

If you feel like you can do a good job and are interested, please
subscribe to tlug-admin@example.com and volunteer.

I will continue to act as Listmaster until a suitable replacement can
be found, and will be happy to help the new Listmaster learn the ropes
of our system.

Fluency in English is a must, and fluency in Japanese and experience
with Mailman are pluses. But the most important qualities a Listmaster
must possess are patience, decisiveness, and the trust of TLUG
members, new and old alike.

I think Scott Robbins would be ideal for the position, but I know he
is busy just now, so he may not want to volunteer. I hope he does,
though. :)

Please direct questions to tlug-admin@example.com

Cheers,
Josh
