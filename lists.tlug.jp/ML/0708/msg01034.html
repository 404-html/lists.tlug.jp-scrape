did what they felt they had to do.  One side felt that
the taxes from the other side were unjust.  The other

of people died.  A lot of people gave their lives for
the cause they believed in.  I will resist the
temptation to branch off even further in this 
conversation, as it can easily branch off into some 
ugly debates that would go nowhere.  :P  My own views
do not totally support one side or the other.  Both sides
had justice on their side in their own eyes.
