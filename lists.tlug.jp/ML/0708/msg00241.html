7-11（セブンイレブン => Bun-Bun, get it?）has a Kirin "Chilled Beer & Glass
Set"（ チルドビール3本&グラスセット）for Y749. This set is a summer special including
three "premium" Kirin beers: Ichiban Shibori, Grand Ale, and Maroyaka
Kobo.

This is a great way to try Kirin's mid-range beers, since they come in
glass bottles that don't screw with the taste of the beer. The gift of
a glass is a nice extra. Details in my blog, for those who care about
beer:
