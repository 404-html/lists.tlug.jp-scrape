You could always use the poor man's IME:

1. Browse to: http://www.edrdg.org/cgi-bin/wwwjdic/wwwjdic?1C
2. Type your word in romaji: (e.g. "boku")
3. Check the "Check this box if the keyword is romanized Japanese" box
4. Click the "Begin Search" button.
5. Select the appropriate result (e.g. "åƒ•")
6. Copy
7. Paste into email
8. Rinse
9. Repeat

Time-consuming but fun. :)
