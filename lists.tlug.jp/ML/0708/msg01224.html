It has been awhile since i've booted the Linux installation, but i seem to remember it being based on a really old version of Red Hat (say around 5.x or 6.x, before the numbers started over with Fedora).
FYI, PS2 Linux is based on Kondara 2[1] which is now Momonga[2]. Kondara was based on some version of Redhat
and as you can see in the wiki page[1], the main "seika" of it was a installer ( momonga )[1] and a software management system ( mph-get ) [1]

I loved Kondara 2 because it was very well tested, less buggy and more Japanese-ready than other Linux distribution at that time being,
just to compare with Vine, Mandrake and Redhat.
