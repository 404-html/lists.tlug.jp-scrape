One of the main factors that my wife and I are considering in our move

Ireland is winning right now in our rankings: small military, vibrant

climate, rich history, great economy (per-capita GDP of USD
44,500--USD 500 *higher* than the US), lots of high-tech jobs (Google,
Amazon, IBM, Microsoft, and Oracle all have a big Dublin presence),
and... GUINNESS!
