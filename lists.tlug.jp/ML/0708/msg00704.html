On Fri, 10 Aug 2007, steven smith wrote:

This presentation by Bram Moolenaar got posted to YouTube a while back. It might be of interest:
7 Habits For Effective Text Editing 2.0
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eX9m3g5J-XA 

Or, if you want to get the same thing in fifteen minutes rather than

    http://www.moolenaar.net/habits.html

This is pretty what I'd do for a document or a presentation, so now I
really feel no need to repeat it.

On Fri, 10 Aug 2007, Josh Glover wrote:

But that does not stop me from wanting to pick up little tips on
how to use Vim more efficiently.


A good point that Moolenaar makes is the need for making something a
habit. That means not only finding out about a new trick, but using it
over and over and over again. If you don't use vim that much, you'll not
likely be able to memorize and internalize the good editing tricks.
