Well from what I can see it looks quite like Skype.  Sure there are
some difference but it has similar icons, similar interface, uses
similar terms and menu names etc.

which tells me they are very aware of Skype.  And Skype was created
first.

describe it though as the phrase "rip-off" has derogatory connotations.

sort of user experience end users have with Skype.  They are doing this
using open standards and open source. I assume that they also want to
create a better product than Skype.
