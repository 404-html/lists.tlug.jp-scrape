On the vanilla Mac (little installed from MacPorts, no non-standard
fonts), I get 10 Japanese fonts, mostly near-dupes:
 2 styles of Osaka,
 2 styles of fixed (one for Korean),
 2 styles of Hiragino Mincho,
 3 styles of Hiragino Gothic, and
 1 style of Hiragino Maru Gothic.

Of these the Hiragino fonts and the fixed fonts claim to do both
Japanese and Czech.
