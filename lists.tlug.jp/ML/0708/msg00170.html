Those people probably need to lighten up. I am a hobbyist beer
critic[1], and I enjoy both ale and lager (not to mention stout,
porter, IPA, pilsener, and even mead). Most poor beers tend to be
lagers, as they are cheaper to produce, AFAIK (and please correct me
if I am wrong, AFAYK), so I think this is why lager gets a bad name,
because there are far more bad lagers out there than bad ales.

Beer tasting is a great hobby, because unlike wine tasting, you don't
have to spit it out after you taste. Unless it is some shitty beer
indeed. ;)

If you want to get started, here is an excellent guide:

http://ratebeer.com/Story.asp?StoryID=103
