Tengu House Brew, IMO, is about on par with Kirin / Sapporo / Asahi /
Ebisu "premium" beers; it almost stands on its own, and works quite
well in a izakaya setting where your goal is to eat, drink, and be
merry.

When you get out of the Navy, you really must join us at the Town
Crier, which has a few decent beers on tap, and a few outstanding ones
in the bottle (they occasionally have Sam Smith's Nut Brown Ale, which
for my money is one of the outstanding exports of Merry Ole). People
who know beer can guide you gently towards losing your beer virginity
properly. ;)
