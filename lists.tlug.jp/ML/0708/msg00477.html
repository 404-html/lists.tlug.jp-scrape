> The proposed layout.

Did you see my email on this? Any tagging that is to be officially
encouraged will have to happen in the body of the email, save a few
like [SUMMARY], [C&C], etc. I will actively discourage tagging in the
subject line, as I think it hurts accessibility to new list
members--some of whom may not even be Unix newbies (like Mattia,
Gernot, et al.)--and it is important to TLUG to keep getting fresh
blood.
