On Fri, Aug 24, 2007 at 05:57:52PM +0900, Jason Hall wrote:
> 
> 1) Is it better to find a board with two IDE connectors or is it any
> problem to add a 2,000 yen PCI card to get a couple more? (In that
> case, can I connect 6 drives or just 4?)

Assuming you get two channels on the PCI card, they will happily add to
the one on the motherboard.  This gives 6 drives in total.

> 2) Same for the firewire. Any significant difference between onboard
> or an added PCI card?

None, as onboard controllers sit on the PCI bus, too.

Keep in mind however that not every mainboard today comes with two or
more ordinary PCI slots.
