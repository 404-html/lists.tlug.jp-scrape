           TLUG - Tokyo Linux Users Group August "nomikai" 
           ===============================================
 
         Date:     August 12, 2005 (Friday)

         Time:     19:00-21:00

         Place:    *** British Pub TOWNCRYER Hibiya ***
                   Hibiya Central Bldg B1 
                   1-2-9 Nishishinbashi Minato-ku Tokyo
                   Phone: 03-3519-6690
                   http://www.towncryer.jp/TChibiya.html
  
         Event:    drink and eat (primarily a social activity)
  
         Costs:    pay as you go
                   http://www.towncryer.jp/TCmenu.html
  
        This event is open to all Linux users and friends, and is
        to promote casual Linux (and non-Linux) related discussions
        over a few cold ones. Even if you're not a Linux user or a
        LUG member, but want to hang out with Linux people, please come.
 	  
        Town Cryer Hibiya is within walking distance from Uchisaiwai-cho
        (Toei Mita Line), Kasumigaseki (Chiyoda Line), or Toranomon
        (Ginza Line). If you get lost, or simply want to get in touch 
        with us, please call:
  
            Zev Blut............090-5778-8067
            Alberto Tomita......080-3463-9256
  
        For more information, access the TLUG web page at http://www.tlug.jp
 
        See you there!
