Many of you may know Matthew (AKA Karamoon) Don. He did a number of TLUG presentations[1], Organized the Tokyo Barcamp and helped get the Tokyo Hackerspace started. He is a father with two young children and a really great guy.
He has stage 4 cancer and is raising funds to pay for immunotherapy, which is an experimental treatment that shows promise in treatment such dire cases.
If you can spare money to help support Matthews treatment it would be greatly appreciated. He would also appreciate you sharing his predicament with others who may be able to help. You can find more details here
