> etc/rc.d/rc.#/ (make all those S## and K## links). I'd rather write a

Probably talking about chkconfig. It relies on a comment in the main
script in /etc/rc.d/init.d/ to get it to work. Below is an example.

# chkconfig: 2345 80 30

Have a look at the man page to figure it out :)
