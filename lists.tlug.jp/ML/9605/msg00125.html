  Thanks, I think it's too soon notice, our next big show is Interop.  I'm 
aware of the Jazz, be careful with media, many reports on comp.periphs.scsi 
have problems with the media.  It's as fast as an IDE drive, but not close 
to an Ultra or even narrow fast barracuda.  Shouldn't need extra drivers, 
except for removable media, can linux support MO drives?  If so, should 
support Jazz.
