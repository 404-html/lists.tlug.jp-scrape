If you would like to get your GnuPG/PGP key signed by other
TLUG members, remember to bring along three or four
"keyslips"[1] and photo ID to Saturday's meeting.

Jim

[1] A small printout of your key ID, name, email address, and
key fingerprint so that members can go home and be reasonably
sure what they are fetching from the keyserver or the TLUG
keyring is in fact your key... and then sign it in the comfort
of their own home.  "gpg --fingerprint {my-key-id}
>myfingerprint.txt" will give you a text file that you can
clean up and replicate several times on a sheet of A4 paper
that can be sliced into strips for each potential signer.
