On Mon, May 5, 2008 at 7:51 PM, Dave M G <dave@example.com> wrote:
> TLUG,
>
>  After upgrading to Hardy Heron (Ubuntu 8.04) on my laptop, I get the
> following error every time I boot up:
>
>  Error activating XKB configuration.

Go to your xorg.conf file and add the requisite keyboard
configuration---something like:

        Option          "XkbRules"      "xorg"
        Option          "XkbModel"      "jp106"
        Option          "XkbLayout"     "jp"

And see if that helps.

Cheers, Gernot
