Brian Hughes wrote:

I am setting up Debian 4 for the first time and am having audio and video issues of various kinds within Gnome. There seems to be a lot of info available but what is valid or accurate is difficult for me to determine. 

To set up audio on a new install, I usually do

# apt-get install alsa-utils

and then

# alsaconf

For the video, have you tried running

# dpkg-reconfigure xfree-86


?
