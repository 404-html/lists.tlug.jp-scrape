On 2008-05-02 22:58 -0700 (Fri), SL Baur wrote:

> > Sounds like a name resolution problem to me. Try restarting nscd after
> >  you connect to the wireless network.
> 
> Maybe, but there could well be browser issues involved as well.

It sounds like name resolution to me, too, but it's not hard to confirm
that it is the browser. Just use the ping command-line tool to try to
ping a name, and if it comes up with a name to address mapping and
starts trying to send the packets, your resolver is working ok. (If
you don't see any responses to the pings, don't worry; you're probably
trying to ping one of Josh's machines and he's just trying to make your
problem more difficult to debug. :-))
