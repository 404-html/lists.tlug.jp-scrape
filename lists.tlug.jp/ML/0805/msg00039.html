Walk down the layers.

Can you 'ping' servers on the internet? 
  ping www.heise.de

If yes, looks like browserproblem.
If no, try the stuff below:

Did you actually connect to the wlan?
  iwconfig could show for most network-chips.

Did you get an ip?
  ifconfig

Did you get proper routing, defaultgw set?
  ip route show

Did you get the dns-server properly?
  cat /etc/resolv.conf

etc.
