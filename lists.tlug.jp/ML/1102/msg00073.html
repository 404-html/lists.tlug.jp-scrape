This probably should be posted on the admin list.
Re: volunteering, it depends on what skills you have and what you would like to do. TLUG relies on volunteers to organize, speak at and run technical meetings and nomikai's. Volunteers manage the services we provide. We also rely on members to promote the group and encourage interesting people to attend events and get involved.
Maybe the best way to get started is to tell a bit about yourself and what things you are interested in.
