On Fri, Dec 5, 2008 at 1:25 PM, Sach Jobb <sach.jobb@example.com> wrote:
Hello,

I am looking to setup a SIP server and wanted to know if anybody had any recommendations on good a good ISDN card that will work with NTT. I am looking to deploy trixbox ce on this, but wouldn't mind hearing what other people have used for their own VoIP solutions.


I've used the Digium cards before with both NTT and Softbank T1 lines. I have not used any if their ISDN cards but I'd assume that they would work fine.

The trickiest part was figuring out the Asterisk config settings to make it work with the (Japanese) phone line. You'll probably want to be comfortable working directly with the config files since I've found the trixbox interface to be wanting in this regard.
