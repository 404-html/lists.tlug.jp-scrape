No, Josh (and Scott) run the list *because* they dislike top-posting
enough to be willing to look like the bad guys by posting reminders.
Saving the large number (at one time, 90% or so) of members who agree
but are perhaps a little shy ;-) from having to do so themselves.

you were as crisp as a fresh chapati.

police it strictly.  But it once was, and that's why TLUG has
listmasters who are strict about it.  Not the other way around.
