Then I'll show my .mc file.  Put this under /usr/lib/sendmail-cf/cf and edit, 
and then run m4 ../m4/cf.m4 thifile.mc > sendmail.cf.
My machine is masquerading as MailAndNews.com, although real hostname is tetsu.

divert(0)dnl
VERSIONID(`@(#)rai.mc	8.7 (rai) 5/13/1999')
OSTYPE(linux)dnl
DOMAIN(generic)dnl
MAILER(local)dnl
MAILER(smtp)dnl
MASQUERADE_AS(MailAndNews.com)
MASQUERADE_DOMAIN(MailAndNews.com)
FEATURE(masquerade_envelope)dnl
FEATURE(allmasquerade)dnl
