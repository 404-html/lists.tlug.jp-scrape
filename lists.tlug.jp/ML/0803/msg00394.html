 > I think that Josh's point may be that something as simple as obfuscating
 > the URLs and post parameters you use will break the generic software,

Unfortunately, a lot of spammer programmers are hackers as well as
crackers.  Obfuscation of this can only go so far or you'll confuse
the users' browsers, too, I think, and these buttheads pride
themselves on cracking you, especially if you're creative.

Anyway, we want to get out of that arms race; we can't win it for long
enough to justify much ongoing effort (unless it's just a matter of
pride).  We need big cheap wins.
