Don't worry, this is just to inform you of changes in how you can access
your mail with a mobile phone.

It says they are ending service for the yahoo! mail "appli" on April 9th.
(a downloadable app for your phone).

But it says you can access your mail with your phones browser on the
renewed mobile site. This will work with i-Mode, EZweb or Yahoo-Keitai.
