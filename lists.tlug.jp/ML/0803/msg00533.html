But even if this is the case, Tobias, don't despair. I'd like to
introduce a custom imported from the Irish Linux Users Group
(ILUG--http://linux.ie/), called the Pint of the Day, or PotD. It

the latter; usually the name of the venue and its street address is
sufficient).

maybe you can call a PotD on a datetime that works for you, and
another TLUGger can assign a venue to said datetime.
