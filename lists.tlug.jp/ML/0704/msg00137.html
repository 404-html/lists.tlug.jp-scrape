TLUG,

My SD card reader is still not behaving. When I plug in an SD card, dmesg (*1) reports that it is there, but no icon appears on my desktop, there is no way to access it.

I think I've narrowed down the problem to being either one of SCSI support not properly enabled, or a permissions issue.

I know it's not a USB problem, because all my other USB devices (printer, Bluetooth dongle, MP3 player) are hotplugging just fine.

I took a look at my earlier discussion on this list about SD card problems, because it did come up before.
http://www.tlug.jp/ML/0608/msg00095.html

Unfortunately, my solution then wasn't so enlightening. Dave M G v.1.0 said:
"Strangely, my SD card is now functioning again, though I'm not 100 percent sure what I did to solve it."
http://www.tlug.jp/ML/0608/msg00105.html

Thats not much help, Dave-from-a-year-ago!

Through that previous thread, I was asked to provide the output of lsusb(*2), lspci (*3), and /sbin/lsmod (*4). Comparing the results now don't look a lot different from then.

I'm at a bit of a loss right how to diagnose if SCSI support is not enabled again in my kernel. Or if maybe I am not part of the right group to have access to it.

Any suggestions on what to do next?
