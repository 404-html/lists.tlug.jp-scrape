My lsmod shows:
sd_mod 23428 5
scsi_mod 142348 5 usb_storage,sg,sr_mod,sd_mod,libata

usb_storage 72256 0

Her lsmod shows:
sd_mod                 22656  2
usb_storage            75072  1
scsi_mod              144648  3 sg,sd_mod,usb_storage
usbcore               134912  4 usb_storage,libusual,ohci_hcd

But as far as I can tell, hers doesn't have a usbhid entry, and I do. Maybe that's a problem?

I tried dmesg on her machine:
