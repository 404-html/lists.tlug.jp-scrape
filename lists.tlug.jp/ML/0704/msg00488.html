If this is content you'd like to share with your Japanese students then perhaps the Ties Community System (http://www.tiesnet.jp/) might have a solution that covers all your needs.
 
I don't have a lot of technical details on it but last time I checked they were covering 49 universities throughout Japan (278 teachers, 16.000 students, 10.000+ content/video lectures) and their system includes video conversion options as well as a live streaming service for real-time seminars.
 
Not sure what the requirements are to join the network but my understanding is that part of the lectures are open to the general public.
