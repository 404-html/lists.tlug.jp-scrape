On Wed, 11 Apr 2007, scott wrote:

On Tue, 2007-04-10 at 11:58 +0900, Keith Bawden wrote: 
So, somewhere in the exim configs there should be "user" and "group"
options to run as.

Didn't have any luck finding this in /etc/exim/exim.conf.

exim's config file is called 'configure'. On FreeBSD it's in /usr/local/etc/exim/configure. The options you are looking for are:

exim_user = mailnull
exim_group = mail

(Those are my settings; your sill be different of course.)
