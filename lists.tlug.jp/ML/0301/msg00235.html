Quoth Stuart Luppescu (Tue 2003-01-21 02:29:28PM -0600):
> 
> Does anyone (Ryan? Scott?) know how to build sylpheed on a Gentoo box so it
> can display Japanese correctly?

Well, I am neither Ryan nor Scott, but I know that the usual way to build an
app with Japanese support in Gentoo is to make sure "cjk" is in your USE
flags.

Try something as simple as:

USE="cjk" emerge sylpheed

Hopefully that will do what you want. If it does not, I would recommend
searching the Gentoo Forums (forums.gentoo.org) for "sylpheed AND
japanese" or something like that. The Forums are usually a damned fine
resource.
