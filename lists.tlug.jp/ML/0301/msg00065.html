Please be informed that YBP (Yokohama Business Park) data centre where

Starts:	Jan 24th (Fri) 19:00 
Ends:	Jan 25th (Sat) 19:00

TLUG server will be powered off by 19:00, January 24th - and be back
online at 19:00, January 25th - hopefully without delay :)

Sorry for the inconvenience,
