All,

OpenOffice.org is proud to announce the establishment of the JA (Japanese
Language) Project, led by NAKATA, Maho.  The latest addition to the
Native-Lang Projects, the JA Project breaks new ground in accommodating not
only one of the largest OpenOffice.org user groups but also Japanese: The
project's website is in Japanese, as are mail-list discussions.

We look forward to working with Mr. Nakata's project to further the benefits
and excitement of OpenOffice.org.

The team members making up the JA Project include:
