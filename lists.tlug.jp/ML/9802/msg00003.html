>>>>> "Jim" == Jim Schweizer <schweiz@example.com> writes:

    Jim> Hi all, I don't usually use xemacs unless I'm going to be
    Jim> doing *a lot* of cut and pasting between different files.

    Jim> Anyway, whenever I open a html file there's a quick warning
    Jim> about 'No html user config file' then something about
    Jim> hm-html-load-config. I looked through the tutorial that came
    Jim> with xemacs but didn't find anything.

What version of XEmacs are you using?  There were many config/install
problems with hm--html-menus in pre-20.5 versions.  I don't know how
many of them got fixed in 20.3.

Are you using the PSGML support?  (If you are, you will have a monster 
menubar that extends about 5" past the right border of the XEmacs
frame :-P )  PSGML gives partial validation internally and has hooks
to sgmls to do complete validation.  

    Jim> I'm hoping that the user config file will allow me to remap
    Jim> some keys. In particular, I want to remap & from &amp; to &;

Why?  PSGML gives good keymap support for named entities.  Thus I have
heard, anyway, I don't use entities.

    Jim> Take pity on me, I don't know anything about lisp, and as
    Jim> soon as this project's over, I'm heading back to vi.

Big mistake....  It's your life that's a-wasting, though.
