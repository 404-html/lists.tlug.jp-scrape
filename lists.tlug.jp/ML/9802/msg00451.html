Patches for Japanese pine are available at 
ftp://ftp.noc.titech.ac.jp/pub/tmp/aki 

The README.j from the Japanese patch indicates that the Japanese Pine 
kit has the following features: 

      Japanese online help, 
      Japanese manual page, 
      support for Japanese folder names in the addressbook, 
      encoding and decoding of MIME headers, 
      Japanese support for internal editor [pico], 
      Japanese input support through integration with Canna control routines. 

The Japanese documentation is optional. 


There is additional information at
http://beehive.twics.com/~craig/writings/linux-nihongo/
click on pine.  You need some library files and stuff.  

If you're compiling with glibc6, it gets tricky.  Chris Sekiya
did a binary distribution.  Let me know if you need it and
I'll e-mail it to you.
