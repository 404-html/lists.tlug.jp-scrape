Here's a pretty definitive answer about JWZ's compass, from one of my
colleagues:


>Yes, I do know thew answer. The answer is that the Netscape "Compass" is
>hard-coded to jwz's URL... There's no way for anyone else to use the
>compass. It was a perk they threw in for him... because he designed
>practically the whole mail and news system in Netscape 3 and 2 (not
>Communicator, though).
>
>The "ANIM" tag and "HASH" and "KEY" attributes are smoke screen jokes
>designed to throw off those who are trying to figure out how he did it.


I guess that means we won't be doing that ourselves. At least, not unless we
get the source code and release a TLUG version of Communicator :-)
