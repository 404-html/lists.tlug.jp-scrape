Just in case there are some people in this mailing list who
don't know Eric Raymond's essay on the Linux development
process:

  http://sagan.earthspace.net/~esr/writings/cathedral-bazaar/

It seems this article was a major influence for Netscape to
decide that they release the source of their browser.  The
essay is much fun to read.
