Hi Mikio,

You mean tlug.gr.jp?

Mikio wrote:
> Just out of curiosity, how much does it cost per year to secure a name in 
> Japanese these days?  Anyone know?  I was thinking about doing one 
> personally, but for the good of the TLUG community, I wouldn't mind donating 
> some money to get a TLUG domain up... provided that it isn't too much...

JPNIC requires 20,000 yen for one *.jp domain at the registration.
They don't need annual fee for that domain name.  The registered
domain should be connected in one year from registration.

http://www.nic.ad.jp/jpnic/
http://www.nic.ad.jp/jpnic/faq/QandA.html#4010
ftp://ftp.nic.ad.jp/jpnic/domain/domain-name-all.txt
ftp://ftp.nic.ad.jp/jpnic/domain/domain-name-fee.txt

All sources up above are in Japanese.


OR.JP domain is for non-profit leagal entities.  I think TLUG can get
only *.GR.JP in Japan, according to JPNIC domain name rules.
For the registration, president and vice president of the group are
required to submit registration form with their seals on it and seal
certifications ($B0u4U>HL@(B).

Probably we can easily get a domain name.  It might be very hard to
find who can host that domain. :p
