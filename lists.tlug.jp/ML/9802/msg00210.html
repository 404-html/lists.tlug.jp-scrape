Ok, two things:

1.  If anyone knows of any big FTP sites that might want to mirror
TurboLinux, let me know.  I'm working on getting it on Sunsite, and we
already have a mirror in Japan at Vector (although it's currently broken -
I'm working on fixing it this week).

2.  Who wants to beta-test TurboLinux 1.4J?  You get a free advance copy
of the CD, among other things.  The beta test period is 3/2 through 3/6,
so let me know soon if you want to.  It'll be all in Japanese, just like
the one I demo'ed at the meeting on 2/14, so being able to read Japanese
is somewhat important.  We will pay for and take care of having the
one-offs sent directly to you, so all you have to do is try it out and
then give us a *detailed* report of any problems you find and your
system's hardware configuration.

Please reply ASAP.  Hey, free Linux distribution, you can't beat that with
a stick, eh?
