Well... here I am in floody California, driving along up the Lawrence 
Expressway near Homestead Rd., and at the stop light, I was looking at the 
bumper stickers of the car ahead of me... the first one I don't get, so I 
was hoping someone would be able to help me... I can't remember exactly what 
it said, but it said something about LSI and how 1 will become 2 or 
vice-versa (like I said, I can't remember)

Anyways, the bumper sticker next to it, in big faded out yellow letters, but 
proudly and promintely displayed was "LINUX"... at that point, I was really 
back home in the Silicon Valley and felt good for the rest of the day...

Anyways... nothing to do with the list really, but I just thought that I 
would share these with everybody... :D  I'll shut up now...
