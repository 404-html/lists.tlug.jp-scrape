Looks correct to me.
ASCII 0 - 26  are ctrl a-z.
Then 27 - 31 are ctrl [, ctrl \, ctrl ], ctrl ^ and ctrl _ respectively
(these are the 5 chars which follow the caps alphabet in the ASCII table,
surprise, surprise:)
