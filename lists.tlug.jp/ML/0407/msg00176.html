Firefox is also an X11 program, since GTK is just an abstraction layer
on top of the native X11 libraries (with which only Steve Turnbull and
Jim Breen are man enough to do battle; the rest of us turn tail and run

ially affect *any* X11 program, GTK, KDE, or not.
