>I set up an email account to forward to another account (at a different
>office), by making a .forward file in the users /usr/home directory. And,
>by golly, it worked!
>
>But now she tells me she still wants to be able to check her original
>account (when she's at this office). Is there a way to do this? I thought
>of creating a second account for her and forwarding her emails to that, eg.
>


in the .forword, write her account name after \, like this one

$cat .forward
\her
her@example.com

then the mail will forword to her@example.com, and still keep in her's mail
box.
