> Hello everyone!

> I asked the question about booting LILO on TL2.0J yesterday, and someone
> suggested to pick MBR for the installation of LILO, so we did that, but

> 
> What is happening is that every time we install and reboot, it will boot
> up to "LI" and freeze.  Does anyone know any other way that we might be
> able to make this work?

How big is this machine?  What floor are you on?  I suggest immediate
defenestration as the best solution in this case.

Seriously, though, is the drive in LBA mode?  Is it on the primary IDE
chain?  If both of these are 'yes', you may have One Of Those Machines On
Which Lilo Simply Refuses To Work, or OOTMOWLSRTW, for short.  In which
case, use the sysboot disk (instructions for making/using are in the TL
user's guide).
