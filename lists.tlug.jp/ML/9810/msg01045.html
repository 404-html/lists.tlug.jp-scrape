Not completely sure if I understand the problem (gave up on sendmail a
while ago).  Your last couple of sentences remind me of something that
has tripped me up in the past though (not sure if it's a help).

Remember that sendmail only reads sendmail.cf at startup.  If mail sent
from the machine itself does what you expect, but mail received via smtp
still maintains the old behaviour, you've probably forgotten to restart
the sendmail daemon process.
