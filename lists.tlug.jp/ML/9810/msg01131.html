> As pointed out elsewhere, however, the TL sales do not include those from

Slackware and FreeBSD are both very popular in Japan.
(fortunately for us, PHT is also the official reseller of the Official
Slackware-J and the Official FreeBSD-J :) -- Laser5 makes their own
version of both, however).  So I'd say that the remaining 17-20% are
Slackware, FreeBSD, Solaris, IRIX, HPUX, AIX, etc, etc..
