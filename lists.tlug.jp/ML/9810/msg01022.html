well, I've seen the new Samba, and the New Samba Is Good.  Probably my
favorite new feature is the /smb virtual filesystem thing - ie, you're on
a lan with windows boxes, you run 'smbsh', then you can go to /smb and see
everything in the neighborhood.  You can even see print spools, and print
things by simply copying files into the right virtual dir on your linux
box.  Kind of neat, I think.
