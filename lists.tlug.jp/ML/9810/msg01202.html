who is.  

His response confirms AH`s and adds some additional info:

FB> How do I get to the PROM prompt at startup? 

#> If he's running with a real sun keyboard/monitor, he needs to
#> hit the L1-A combination (the key "L1", and the key "a"). 
#> That'll drop him into a  prompt even if the system is multiuser. 
#> if it's a serial console, he needs to send a break. Minicom, that's 
#> ALT-F, kermit it's (escape character -- usually ctrl \) and B.
