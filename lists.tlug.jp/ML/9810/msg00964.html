office), by making a .forward file in the users /usr/home directory. And,
by golly, it worked!

account (when she's at this office). Is there a way to do this? I thought
of creating a second account for her and forwarding her emails to that, eg.

   darren@example.com --> darren2@example.com
                              darren@example.com

But that's not ideal as she has to change her POP settings.
