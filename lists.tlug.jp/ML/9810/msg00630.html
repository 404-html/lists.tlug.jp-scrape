> >> I am having a problem with my sendmail.  I think that sendmail won't

> >> DNS.  For example, the one today was from

[...]
> >> and 
> >>     ping dgs.monash.edu.au

> Of course it won't, because it's not a host; it's a mail alias. 

That would be right.  But Michael indicated that he was pulling
his mail with Pop3.  If he's on a serial line or behind a
firewall, his machine might not find the real host either
... over to you, Michael?

when you try to pull, and see what sort of messages issue from
sendmail.  That would give you a bit more info on what's going on
before (as I imprudently suggested in my last note) you start
hacking on the sendmail configuration file.
