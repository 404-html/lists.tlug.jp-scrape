Qvwm looks so much like Window 95 that you could fool more than a few
people at first glance, I bet.  The author believes this will be useful
for people weaning themselves from Windows, and he's probably right.  It's
so good he probably ought to hope MS never notices it.   They might sue
him.  Or on the other hand, they might buy it off him for use in a future
MS Linux product. 

No matter how much you may hate 95-alike WMs, this one does have one real
virtue: it's fully internationlized right out of the box, written by
somebody from Tokyo U.  If your eyes can take it, read more at:

<http://www-masuda.is.s.u-tokyo.ac.jp/~kourai/qvwm/index-en.html>

And of course, if you like 95-alike WMs, you'll probably drool on your
keyboard while you're downloading this one :-)
