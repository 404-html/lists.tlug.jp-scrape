In case any of you missed it, an update from Linux Today. ( Full story at www.hzo.cubenet.de/ioscount ).
LT`s summary:

**
Linux Continues To Dominate European Internet Servers
Oct 11th, 17:57:33

The latest exhaustive CubeNet surveys of European RIPE domains show Linux to be the most widely deployed with 26.3% of the Web servers, 33.9% of the FTP servers and 24.7% of the news servers.

Surveyed were 750,000+ Web servers, 165,000+ FTP servers, and 26,000+ news servers.
**

For some highly visible markets in Germany and Europe at least, Linux is kicking MS`s butt. Way to go, Karl Max Wagner! ;-)
