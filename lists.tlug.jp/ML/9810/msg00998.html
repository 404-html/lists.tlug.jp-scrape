I thought from the Gnus faq that IMAP still wasn't working, but actually it's
coming along and reasonably useable now. YAY. There is a new developer who
picked up the ball and ran with it this summer. It might work with Gnus, but
would probably require some patching. I tossed in the latest Pgnus (0.36) and
it is not bad so far. Seems kinda slow at downloading headers, but that might
be fixable if I look closer.

If you're interested, take a look at what he has:

http://www.extundo.com/gnus-imap/
