In working on a couple of projects, I started trying FrontPage Express, just
to see if Mr. De Hoog knew something better. Personally, I found it clunky
to use. But then I've always been a text-based coder.

Anyhow, in searching freshmeat, I found that asWedit has been updated finally.
It came out in August, and it runs in HTML 4.0 now. It is supposed to handle
non-English languages based on the LANG setting, but I haven't tried that
angle yet. So far it works much more smoothly than its predecessor, and the
copy/paste functions even work from the keyboard now!

If you're interested, go to: 

http://www.advasoft.com/
