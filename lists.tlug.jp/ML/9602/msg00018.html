TLUG,

     In case you haven't heard of it yet, Apple has announced to
sponsor OSF to port the all-mighty Linux to their Power Mac series.
Whether this move has been prompted by the notion that their losing 
the edge in computing against the Wintel tandem, it surely is good 
news to us Linux lovers. We now have a second choice for a cheap
hardware.  And the day will come that Linux will run all Windows,
Mac, and Unix applications. Long live Linux!

     For more info check out

            http://mklinux.apple.com/index.html 

Cheers,
George
