﻿Sat. June 7 and Sun. June 8 – Iwate Kura Beer in Ikebukuro
Folk & Country Festival at Ikebukuro Nishiguchi Park
10 am to 7 pm (to 5 pm on Sunday)

An open-air bar serving Iwate Kura craft beer will be opened in the
park. Foods from the region will also be served, including sausages,
senbei, croquettes and more. On handpump will be an English-style pale
ale and Red Ale, with regular taps serving a Festbier and either Weizen
or Stout. A number of bottled varieties of Iwate Kura beer will also be
served.
