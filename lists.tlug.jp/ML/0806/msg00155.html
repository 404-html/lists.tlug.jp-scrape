I don't suppose there's any way of protecting something once people decide
they want to steal it?

If the copy is on a US server, you could write to the company hosting the video and point out that they have unauthorized copyrighted material on their server. That usually results in it being pulled.

However, technically, there is probably nothing you can do. The browser has to download the video to the viewer's computer in order to play it and, once that's done, there will always be someone smart enough to figure out how to snag a copy.
