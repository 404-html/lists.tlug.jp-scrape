Hi Tlug,

In Firefox, F6 is the shortcut to highlight the adress bar.

Under FF 2.x, when F6 is pressed, the address bar is hightlighted,
but In FF 3.0, the address bar is highlighted only on the *FIRST* press of F6,
if we press F6 key three times, address bar will not be highlighted, and it loses focus.

Even if this is not a bug, I think the behavior is not natural.
