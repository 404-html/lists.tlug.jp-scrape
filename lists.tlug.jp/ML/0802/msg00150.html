On 2008-02-25 22:55 -0800 (Mon), steven smith wrote:

> <meta http-equiv="content-type" content="text/html; charset=UTF-8">

Do keep in mind that if your web server specifies a charset in its
content-type line, this will be ignored. The meta tag is mostly useful
for people saving the file to disk and looking at it again, later, where
they then have no HTTP headers.

processing book.
