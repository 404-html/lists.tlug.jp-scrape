Hey all,

Everyone on this list probably knows, but the Open Source Conference 2008 (http://www.ospn.jp/osc2008-spring/) is this Friday and Saturday (Feb 29th - Mar 1st).  It's from 10am - 5:30pm on Friday, 10am - 4:30pm on Saturday.  It's in Okubo, not far from Shinjuku.  (http://www.jec.ac.jp/college/access_no7.html)  

It's free.  (As in free.)

Btw, I'm flying in from San Francisco, will probably be at the show for a little bit Saturday.  I'll be with Craig Oda, one of the original TLUGers from the 90's.  We're in PR, have a lot of open source clients (Linux Foundation, Eclipse Foundation, SourceForge, Alfresco, Funambol, Groundwork, Mulesource, lots of others...) used to be at Turbolinux ourselves.  

Always looking to connect with Linux / open source people.  Any TLUGers attending who want to do a lunch?  It's not a nomikai, but maybe we can grab some ramen and swap stories from different sides of the lake.  If so, email is probably the best way to connect.
