Hung Nguyen Vu wrote: 
Under linux, we can do

svn co makefile
svn co Makefile

however, we cannot check out neither

svn co makefile
svn co Makefile

under Windows. A simple work around is just rename one of them
like svn FAQ recommends.

But, just curiously, is this a bug or a feature?

Well if you have read the FAQ[1] you will know that it is a bug (i.e. case insensitive case preserving filesystems), but not something the subversion developers are in a position to fix.

Edward

1. http://subversion.tigris.org/faq.html#case-change
