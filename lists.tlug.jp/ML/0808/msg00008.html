The Tokyo Society for the Application of Currying, a functional
programming group, will have it's monthly meeting on Thursday,
July 31st.

This month we'll be talking about our team's entry to the ICFP 2008
Programming Contest. There are some blog entries here:

    http://www.starling-software.com/en/blog/icfpc2008/

and I (Curt Sampson) hope to have a longer article covering it finished
in the next day or two.

We may also look at a new project some of us have started to write
a mailing list manager in Haskell; this should soon be hosting the
official TSAC mailing list.

For more information, please see TSAC's web page:

    http://www.starling-software.com/en/tsac.html
