Whenever I get such an error it suggests to me that the driver is
incorrect. The openprinting database recommends the Canon proprietary
CAPT driver:

http://www.linuxprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Canon-LBP3000

which supports Linux:

http://www.canon.com.au/products/printers/laser_printers_low_medium_volume/lbp3000.aspx

There's a thread on the Ubuntu forums a few pages long:

http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=134937
