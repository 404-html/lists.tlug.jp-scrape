Seconded.  It's going to be a problem with browsers in general, but
specifically, I wouldn't trust an RSS reader to necessarily respect
cookies.  A simple RSS reader might, for instance, just go for the RSS
feed, read it, and be done (think a script with wget for instance) -
one request, therefore you would never see a cookie at your end.
(Though it also wouldn't surprise me if your useragent parsing was
crashing on unexpected input ;-)
