Actually, if I recall correctly, Google Analytics uses Javascript and
cookies to do a more sophisticated analysis than you can do from logs.
In particular, you can do a much better job of distinguishing unique and
return visitors, as well as getting better data about how long people
spend reading pages and what the next page is to which they go.
