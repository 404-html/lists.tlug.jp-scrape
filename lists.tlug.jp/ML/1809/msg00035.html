On Sat, Sep 15, 2018 at 02:23:26PM +0900, Curt Sampson wrote:

Is there a higher-contrast version of solarized available? Does
anybody out there use solarized and have any thoughts on it they'd
like to share? Or better yet, want to meet for a beer and give me a
tour through how it works for you?
* base16-solarflare from this [1] collection of shell colour themes is such a version with higher contrast! You can combine it with the matching base16-solarflare for vim, if you want to [2].
* Personally, I find solarized too low in contrast, and all the blue in it is putting me off, so I use base16-gruvbox-dark-soft from the same theme collection. base16-eighties is also nice.
* base16-shell includes 100s of themes now, some of them very good, and you can switch them quickly by just running a shell command, so I suggest you walk through all of them and see what else does suit you. 

[1] https://github.com/chriskempson/base16-shell
[2] https://github.com/chriskempson/base16-vim
