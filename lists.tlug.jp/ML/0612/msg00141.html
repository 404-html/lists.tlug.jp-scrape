> > - Content: Only Linux/TLUG related content no topic restrictions?

> What does the Planet take as an input?  If it is RSS-powered, blog

> linking in *all* of my content, which includes my thoughts on the 
> Japanese Language Proficiency Test, recipes for oyako-don, and so

Yes, it takes RSS feeds as input. But I think aggregating also the "off
topic" stuff makes a planet so much more fun to read. It becomes much
more personal and shows a much more "complete" picture of the TLUG
crowed. All the planets I know (including the high volume GNOME planet)
do not restrict the content to some topic (though some members might
decide to only feed certain stuff into the planet... personal choice).
