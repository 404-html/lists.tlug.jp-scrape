Hello!

Planet TLUG got a new address (http://planet.tlug.jp/), and is now linked
from the main TLUG web page! (see Members > Blogs)

The planet's job is to aggregate all member blogs and provide them as an
HTML page (showing content from the last 14 days) and as RSS feeds.

So if you've got a blog and subscribe to this mailinglist, it is now time
to get aggregated at Planet TLUG! And if you don't have a blog yet, it is
time to start one!    (yes, all cheesy marketing (c) by myself)
All you need to do to get aggregated is to send me the URL to your RSS
feed and a picture (hackergotchi) that should appear for your blog entries
on the planet.
