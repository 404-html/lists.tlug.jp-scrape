Darren Cook wrote:

> Type:
> cdrecord -scanbus
> 
> Does it list your cdrw drive? If not life is harder.

"-scanbus" by default will only list SCSI devices (or devices
accessible via /dev/sg*).
I think "cdrecord -scanbus dev=ATAPI" seems to be the right
incantation for ATAPI devices.
