That's ok, that's how we learn.  However, have a look at that link of
how to ask questions the smart way.  

 href="http://www.catb.org/~esr/faqs/smart-questions.html

See, right now, we don't know what your ethernet card is, etc. etc so we
can't be of that much help. 

Give us the brand names of the cards, see what happens when you do 
ifconfig eth0  
ifconfig eth1

See if there is anything or if it says no such device or device not
configured, etc. 

If it worked in FC3 it should work in FC6 so I'm guessing there's a
chance that you missed something during installation--it's easy,
especially when you're getting used to these things, to miss various
important settings.  (Actually, when you're experienced, it is also
easy, because you pay less attention, thinking that you know what you're
doing. )  :)
