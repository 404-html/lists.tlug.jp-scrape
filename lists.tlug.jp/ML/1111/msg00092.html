1. Use the magnets inside for many purposes. I used them to dry the futon blankets, as far as you have some line of iron passing inside a wall.
2. Stick notes on the fridge. You will need a screw to pull the note out, the magnets are very strong. So I recommend you to attach the screw with the magnet as well.
3. Breaking other HDD by passing the magnet close.
4. The HDD plates are a perfect mirror. Your gf will be happy to have such a nice mirror in her bag. She will love such a gift.
5. If you don't have a girl friend, the mirror is perfect for some decoration at home, pending them by a cable and projecting a laser beam on them.
6. Also I have one to protect the table from a hot fry pan. The vacuum inside the HDD reduces hot dissipation.
