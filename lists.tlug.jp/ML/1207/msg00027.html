Simon Cozens, long-time TLUG member and Perl Monger, will be coming
to Tokyo for the long weekend. This will be a good opportunity to 
catch up with him in person, since he has been present on-line but
physically living in Kyoto.

      TLUG - Tokyo Linux Users Group "emergency nomikai"
       ==========================================================

      Date:     July 15 (Sunday)
      Time:     19:30-late (the next day is a public holiday)

      Location: Aldgate Shibuya

      Address:  30-4 Udagawa-cho, Shin-Iwasaki Bldg. 3F
                Shibuya-ku, Tokyo
                tel: 03-3462-2983 
                http://www.the-aldgate.com/newweb/map_n.htm
 
      Cost:     pay-as-you-go 

      This event is open to all Linux users and friends, and is
      to promote casual Linux (and non-Linux) related discussions
      over a few cold ones. Even if you're not a Linux user or a
      LUG member, but want to hang out with Linux people, please come.

      Even if you want to come at the last minute it's okay - we
      may have to squeeze a bit, but please come!

      If you get lost,
      or simply want to get in touch with us, please call:

         Alberto Tomita..............080-3463-9256
         Edward Middleton............090-3689-0292 

      For more information, access the TLUG web page at
      http://www.tlug.jp
