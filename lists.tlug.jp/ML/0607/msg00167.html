Hi, 

Please put your replies _below_ each thing that you quote. 
Here is the best explanation I've seen of how to do it: 

   http://www.greenend.org.uk/rjk/2000/06/14/quoting.html

The way you replied, putting your reply before the quote, 
is called top-posting and is strongly discouraged upon on TLUG: 

   http://www.tlug.jp/listpolicy.php#postq
   http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Top_posting
   http://www.greenend.org.uk/rjk/2000/06/14/quoting.html#1.2
