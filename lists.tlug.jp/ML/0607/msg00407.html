* Famous lunch place around ginza who open Saturday noon.

On the course of building DataBase, I found *many* restaurant
around Wall Street Associates, and I think it must be share with
other tlugers. ;-)

** "Miso Katsu" yaba ton (みそかつ 矢場とん 銀座店)

"Miso Katsu" is special Nagoya's local food.
I think Baba san know well. ;-)
It is pork cutlet with "Haccho miso".
"Yaba ton" is one of best famous restaurant of "Miso Katsu",
and just up to recently, We cannot eat it out of Nagoya region.
Recently Ginza branch opened, so now we enjoy best quality "Miso Katsu"
at Ginza.

HomePage(Japanese) http://www.yabaton.com/
Map http://www.yabaton.com/images/ginza_map.jpg
