Well, I'm not quite as n00B as all that. However, it gave me an idea to
suggest. I'm not saying there's anything intrinsically wrong with topics
like customizing your own kernel, etc. However, most people have other
things to do. Perhaps the meeting could have a segment for some mundane
desktop task? Even in an environment like Ubuntu that's been deliberately
streamlined with the intention of broadening its appeal, I still find there
are lots of non-trivial things that are making it hard for me to get rid of
Windows. Perhaps we could collect a list of such tasks and pick a couple of
them to include in each technical meeting? Or perhaps it should be a
deliberately less technical meeting?
