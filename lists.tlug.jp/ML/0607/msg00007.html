HI everyone,

Found a neat tool that will do exactly what I was looking for regarding temperature monitoring and auto-shutdown: the GkrellM monitor. Has a setting that monitors CPU temp from /proc/acpi/thermal_zone/THRM/temperature, then at warning threshold perform some task, then at critical level perform another task (eg. shutdown -h now). Plus it looks nifty. Check it out if you are interested:

http://members.dslextreme.com/users/billw/gkrellm/gkrellm.html

Cheers,

Scott VanDusen
