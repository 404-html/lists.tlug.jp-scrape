> How exactly do I make sure I can boot back to my previous kernel?

Copy your "test" kernel to something like /boot/vmlinuz-test and add a
separate entry for it in your bootloader configuration (I don't know
whether yours is LILO or GRUB). Also make sure your bootloader prompts
for the kernel to load.
