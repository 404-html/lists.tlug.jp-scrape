the habit of making backup copies of your config files whenever you
start making changes, so you always have the option to go back to a
known good configuration.

to revision control systems. See if you can find it in the archives,
if you did not read it the first time. Searching for "alain" and "rcs"
may do the trick. It was a fairly recent thread, either this month or
late last month.
