Joe wrote:

> On Thu, 2006-07-06 at 08:42 -0400, Jim wrote:
> 
> > Press and hold the "Ctrl" and "Alt" keys. 
> > Hit (and release) the "F1" key. 
> > Release the "Ctrl" and "Alt" keys. 
> 
> Okay.  Yes, I've done that a few times in Ubuntu, but didn't know what
> it was called or what I was doing.

When X Windows was messed up, that's how you would restore the 
X configuration file, without even having to reboot. 
init 3; init 5 might restart X Windows. 
<Ctrl><Alt><F7> or <Ctrl><Alt><F5> will usually switch 
to the X Window virtual console. 

Learn these X Windows things: 

   <Ctrl><Alt>+
   <Ctrl><Alt>-
   <Ctrl><Alt><Backspace>                          goodnight Gracie
