> I'm going to keep playing with it... well, I should be working, but I'm 
> being obsessive compulsive at the moment. Anyway, if anyone has a 
> suggestion about what I could do about this connect/disconnect problem, 
> I'm all ears.

Hey Dave,

When you do the:
 
pilot-xfer -p /dev/ttyUSB0 --list

after pressing the hotsync on the palm, does it give you any errors?
Also I'm not sure if it matters, but maybe try running it from a root
prompt? Finally, when it automatically disconnects, what do the logs say
in /var/log/messages? 

Try killing all the processes that might have anything to do with the
palm, kpilotDaemon gpilotd gpilotd-control-applet etc, then press
hotsync and run that command above and see what happens. Again, not sure
if it will help but I always like to hack and hack and hack until the
durn thing works, and it's gotta be something.

Cheers,
Scott VanDusen
