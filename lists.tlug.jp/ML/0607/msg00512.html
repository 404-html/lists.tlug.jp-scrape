IIRC is If I remember correctly.

There's a bunch of lists--usually, when I see one I don't know (Godwin
comes up with them sometimes--I had no idea what YADATROT was---yet
another desparate attempt to remain on topic) I just put it into google.

However, there is a little program called wtf as in What the f**k. It's
available as a port in FreeBSD and Josh has mentioned using it, so I
assume it's in Gentoo as well.  Slackware should have it in the BSD
games, I don't know about other distributions.  

It's available at

http://www.mu.org/~mux/wtf/

as a tar.gz file.

It doesn't cover all of them, but it covers many of them.

It's just a little shell script with a database (the database is
actually just a textfile list).
