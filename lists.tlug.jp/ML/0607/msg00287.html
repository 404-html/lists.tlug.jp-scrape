Botond,

Thanks for responding.


I know that the latest two local roots are serious enough to upgrade but
otherwise I doubt that it is necessary to upgrade every time a new
package version comes out.
The thing is that this isn't something I do just out of habit or anything. If I don't go through the hassle of re-installing IVTV after a kernel upgrade, then it no longer works.
In fact, that's often how I tell there's been a kernel upgrade. Sometimes there will be an icon in the top right corner saying I need to reboot because there are upgrades that require it. So then I reboot, and then sometime later when I'm in the mood to watch TV, it doesn't work. And that's what prompts me to upgrade IVTV. 



What does modinfo ivtv say?
