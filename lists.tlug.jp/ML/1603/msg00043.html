> 3)  Help

> "Failed to execute command. Check setting"

I get that too.

> And EBView refuses to read two important dictionaries.

I had to fiddle the config quite a bit to get all mine working.
The EPWING directory structures can be a challenge.

Most of the time I use the Kenkyusha "KOD" site. It costs
about 1.2万円 a year but has 大辞林, the big 5thEd JE and 14
other dictionaries via quite a good interface - it looks them up
all at once and returns the ones with matching entries. It's
useful for translations as it searches in the Japanese translations
in the big EJ dictionaries, and that way gets a much better hit rate
than the JEs. Also, you get the most up-to-date versions.

Oh, and I keep a text window open with a utility running that fires
off greps on a heap of text glossares including good old Eijiro.
