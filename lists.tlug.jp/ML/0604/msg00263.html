I'm still using a clone of the IBM-style keyboard I bought back in 92
or 93. At the time I thought it was a cheap knock-off, but it's better
than almost every other keyboard I've come across since. It needs a
good clean-out every five years or so, but the keycaps show absolutely
no signs of wear. No "Windows" keys either, and there's even a little
switch on the back to put it into XT mode.

I'm just hoping when the time comes I can find a working PS2 -> USB adapter.
