A few pointers here on the purchasing advice,


5. Be able to use an iPod with it
Rhythmbox is an iTunes clone and seems to support iPod, although I have no personal experience with it. My impression is that you can move songs and playlists although you shouldn't expect iTunes Store or FairPlay support. But then again that might just be a good thing. 


3. Have a dual boot setup with GNU/Linux on one partition and Windows
(any version from Windows 2000 or later) on another


As someone mentioned this is no problem, given Grub or Lilo.
Also, SATA is no problem for new distributions. I recently added a SATA drive and had no problems detecting, partitioning nor mounting such a drive.
