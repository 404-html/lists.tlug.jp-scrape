Someone mentioned searching kanji's by graphemes a while ago in this mailinglist. He also supplied this link: http://www.bibiko.de/kanji/.
However, on this link I see no difference between graphemes and radicals. As a matter of fact, according to the wikipedia definition of graphemes (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grapheme):
`In typography, a grapheme is the atomic unit in written language. Graphemes include letters, Chinese ideograms, numerals, punctuation marks, and other glyphs.' 

a radical IS a (typographic) grapheme!

Am I misunderstanding something here ?
