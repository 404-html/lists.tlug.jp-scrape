I'm building a second PVR, this time using the cheapest video capture card available at my neighborhood shop. Googling suggests the Keian JT-TV7131R PRO is a variant of the KWorld Studio TV Terminator. Video (and audio) capture work fine with the standard saa7134 kernel module (options card=65 tuner=54).
