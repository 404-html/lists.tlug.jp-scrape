I don't use these devices so this is just a shot in the dark... but on my 
RHEL3 system ttyUSB{0,1} show up under /dev/usb, not /dev. You could try:

  find /dev -name ttyUSB0

to see if your distro puts them in a subdirectory somewhere. The log clip 
you posted didn't seem to actually give a full path name. Hope that helps 
shed a bit of light.
