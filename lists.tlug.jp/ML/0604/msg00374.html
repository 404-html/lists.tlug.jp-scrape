A friend who is a hardcore BitTorrent user for television
shows told me recently that he had been having a lot of
problems recently with fake shows and bogus trackers,
particularly for the Sopranos.

So for debugging purposes you may want to try with something
like a nice Linux distribution or NerdTV
  http://www.pbs.org/cringely/nerdtv/
where you can be sure that there is no funny business going
on.

Jake
