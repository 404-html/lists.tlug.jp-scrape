This is only on-topic via the fact that many on this list sometimes visit Akihabara and also have an interest in local culture, etc. Putting those together, I just posted a new page about Yushima Seido, which is just a few minutes from Ochanomizu and from there only ten minutes to Akihabara. If you visit Akihabara between 10:00 a.m. and 4:30 p.m., you might enjoy walking through the oasis of the walled Yushima Seido area (there are three gates), stopping off to pay your respects to Confucius as you pass through.... The link is: 
http://www5d.biglobe.ne.jp/~LLLtrs/PhotoGlryMain/pgb/Yushima01.html
