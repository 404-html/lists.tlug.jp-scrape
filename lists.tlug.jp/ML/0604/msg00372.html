Hi Dave,

Not sure if this will help or not, but I was getting a similar error as yours when running evolution:

dave@example.com:~$ sudo printconf
*** glibc detected *** double free or corruption (!prev): 0x08179528 ***

to fix it I ran evolution with this as prefix:

G_SLICE=always-malloc

and the double free corruption error went away- so maybe if you do

dave@example.com:~$ G_SLICE=always-malloc /usr/bin/printconf

it will work. Of course you might have to change the directory for printconf, it might not be in /usr/bin

HTH
