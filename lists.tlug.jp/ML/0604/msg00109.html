Having got into this discussion before with Jim (to no result), I
would argue that the subject is the title of the topic of conversation
and not necessarily a description of the specific message.  Otherwise,
why would mailers put 'Re:' in at all?  In which case, it should
change when the topic changes and not otherwise.

Jim and I differ in our opinions on this.  He tends to change subject
even when giving a direct reply to a question (and use multiple
different subjects when answering different points in different
emails).  I find both intensely irritating...
