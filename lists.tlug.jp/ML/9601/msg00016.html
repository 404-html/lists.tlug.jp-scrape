 > If you can afford it, get a Pentium 90 or faster, not because of the low

 > afford it.  Not sure where someone got the idea P90 doesn't need it.

There's another option: buy a Pentium 75 and overclock it to run at 90.
It's a 3.3V chip, so overheating is not a problem, and the performance
increase is significative. I did it in my machine at home, and it has been
running fine for over a year now.

I saw some benchmarks of EDO memory, and the performace gain over conventional
memory was of only 1%. Pipeline burst cache could make the difference.
