> In the sendmail sources, the relevant doc you need is cf/README.
> Search for "envelope"; what you want to do is spoof the envelope
> on mail coming out of your sendmail daemon.  The function in
> the m4 configuration macros is MASQUERADE_ENVELOPE() or something
> like that.
