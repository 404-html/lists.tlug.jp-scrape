On 15:36 99/02/01 +0900, Andrew S. Howell wrote...
> 	I'm in the market for email archiving and web bases searching
> software. I would like to be able to search both English and Japanese
> email. I vaguely remember someone mentioning chasen or some such thing
> to do the indexing. Is this the thing to use?

My recommendation is Namazu.
http://saturn.aichi-u.ac.jp/%7Eccsatoru/Namazu/

Runs Japanese and English, has various front ends (CGI, Tk, Win32, EMacs...)
and is very inteligent and fast. Should probably be also the choice for
tlug.gr.jp. Indexing is very fast.
