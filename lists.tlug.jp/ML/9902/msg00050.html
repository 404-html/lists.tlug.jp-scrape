 >     Irene> It sais it misses libjpeg.so.62, libncurses.so.4 and

 > Yes.  All of those libraries are necessary; in fact some older
 > versions of each of the libraries in question have bugs that manifest
 > themselves in XEmacs.

 >     Irene> Would it be safe to uninstall the older packages and try

Really? I have the impression that on a Red Hat system you need
both the old and the new libraries -- unless that was just an error in 
packaging RH 5.1, that doesn't affect other versions.

At any rate, Red Hat 5.2 includes 'libjpeg-6b-7.i386.rpm' (the new
one) and 'libjpeg6a-6a-2.i386.rpm'. I have found I need both, but of
course it depends on what jpeg-using packages you have.
