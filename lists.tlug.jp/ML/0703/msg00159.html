Dave wrote:

> I also had to re-install uim/anthy, and now, although it works in 
> FireFox and other apps, it doesn't work in OpenOffice.

You probably have to do this (replace the en_US with your locale if
different):

sudo apt-get install scim-qtimm
im-switch -z en_US -s scim

This enables scim use in all applications (after logging in and out). It's
a one-time operation.
