They have knowledgeable tech support unlike I had with my previous provider Yahoo BB. Here is the English language contact information:

0570-01-3522 (from fixed telephone line)

This is a family plan (FTTH with FLET'S Home Course, Plan M), not business, but Ashahi Net recognizes that you might be hosting a web site, mail server, using SSH, etc. If you do want to host web sites, this service offers everything you need: non-restricted port usage and fast, symmetric upload and download speeds.
