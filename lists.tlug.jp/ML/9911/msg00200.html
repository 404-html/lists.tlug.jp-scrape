Thank you. I thought I'd killed the list with my stupid question
yesterday.

Before installing Mandrake I added some more memory. I had one empty
SIMM slot, and I inserted a 16M line into it (I have a Tyan AT
motherboard). BIOS recognized the memory and DIDN'T COMPLAIN!!! Last
night I had a closer look at the error messages preceding the kernel
panic, and I just removed those 16M. Guess what! It worked :-)

And, yes, I tested the memory, it was OK.

It was 'too much memory' problem...
