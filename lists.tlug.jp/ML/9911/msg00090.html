Caldera is releasing (scheduled for 11/27 or so) a Japanese version of 
their open linux 2.3.  Reading this list, I have no idea if there are any 
other newbies there besides myself, but with the English version at least, 
installing is quite easy, only taking about 1/2 hour--one first partitions 
the drive with the included partition magic and then during install, it 
finds the partition and puts itself in it.

It's going to cost 12800 yen or so
