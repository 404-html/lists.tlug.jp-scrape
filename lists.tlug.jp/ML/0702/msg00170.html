I am sure for packaged systems like RedHat and Debian there should be a
easy way, and what about source systems like Gentoo and FreeBSD?


A way of doing this with gentoo is to use the equery command. For example:

# equery belongs /bin/ls
[ Searching for file(s) /bin/ls in *... ]
sys-apps/coreutils-6.4 (/bin/ls)
