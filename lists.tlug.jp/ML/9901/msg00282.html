This probably belongs in advocacy but I simply could not 
keep it from you:

Altavista has a translation service and I decided to try
it out to see if it could help me to decipher some of 
the german language on Suse's site.

German:
  Ausverkauft! Wieder lieferbar ab 20. Januar 99.

English translation:
  Sells off! Again available starting from 20 January 99.

Back to German:
  Verkäufe weg! Wieder vorhandenes Abfahren von einem Januar 20 99.

Back to English:
  Sales away! Again available driving off of one January 20 99.

Back to German:
  Verkäufe weg! Wieder vorhandenes Abtreiben von einem Januar 20 99.

And English:
  Sales away! Again available driving of one January off 20 99.

German:
  Verkäufe weg! Wieder vorhandenes Antreiben von einem Januar weg von 20
99. 

English:
  Sales away! Again available propelling away from one January of 20 99 

Guess this babelfish thing is not really ready yet...... ;-)
