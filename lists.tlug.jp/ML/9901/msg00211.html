Jonathan, are you using TurboLinux3.0? If so, could you entertain me
and try using


           $ runlibc5 applix

(substitute "applix" to acrobat or whatever you are trying to use).
This is Scott's (or whoever at PHT) work around for the libc  problem.
It works for libX11.so.6, might do the job for libXt.so.6.
