Hi all,

rt (root-tail) is a neat little app that displays the last 5
lines of a given file anywhere on your X11 root window with a
transparent background. 

So if you want to tail -f messages or syslog on your root window
just stick it in your .xsession or .xinitrc. Grab it at

http://www.csl.mtu.edu/~mjbaker/root-tail/
