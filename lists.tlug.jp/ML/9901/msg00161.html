Hmm... last summer I found a good Japanese site, but I don't remember
where ... it may have been at the Nikkei website. I remember I found
it either through Yahoo Japan or by searching for '2000' in Goo.

Also -- though it doesn't relate to Japan, I'd like to recommend a
book. It's by Edward & Jennifer Yourdon, and the title is ... I think, 
"Time Bomb 2000" (or was it "The Millenium Time Bomb"?). In spite of
the title, it's not a sensationalistic book. The authors go through
the various areas of society that may be affected and lay out various
scenarios (mild to severe) of what could happen and why, without
trying to push any particular view of what *will* happen.
