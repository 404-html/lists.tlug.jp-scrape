On Fri, 22 Jan 1999, Eric S. Standlee wrote:

> Jan 22 13:35:54 petra sendmail[589]: NAA00589: Authentication-Warning:
> petra.mb.infoweb.ne.jp: fwiw3980 owned process doing -bs

It means that sendmail doesn't trust user fwiw3980.

This can be fixed by adding a 'Tfwiw3980' line to /etc/sendmail.cf or
adding 'fwiw3980' to /etc/sendmail.cf (if the /etc/sendmail.ct option is
enabled).
