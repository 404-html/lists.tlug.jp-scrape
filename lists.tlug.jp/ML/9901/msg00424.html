Greetings,

never had problems compiling any of the recent 2.1 and pre-2.2 kernels.
But now, with 2.2.0:

drivers/sound/sound.a(sb_ess.o): In function `ess_init':
sb_ess.o(.text+0xde2): undefined reference to `esstype'
sb_ess.o(.text+0xe77): undefined reference to `esstype'
make: *** [vmlinux] Error 1

Ha ha. Murphy's law. Nobody seems to have tested this patch with sound
compiled into the kernel ;)

Anway, the quick-and-dirty fix seems to be trivial: move the definition of
esstype in sb_card.c out of the #ifdef MODULE section.

Just FYI.

-Klaus
