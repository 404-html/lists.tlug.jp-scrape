Rafael,

	I had a problem with dhcpcd once with TL3.0. It turned out one
of the scripts in /etc/sysconfig/network-scripts was broken. I think
this will fix it...

Change:
SLEEPPIDFILE=/var/run/dhcp-wait-${IFNAME}.pid

to:

SLEEPPIDFILE=/var/run/dhcp-wait-${DHCP_DEVICE}.pid

in /etc/sysconfig/network-scripts/ifdhcpc-done
