I have 50 free one day (Wed. the 29th) tickets--for the seminars as well as
the floor exhibits. These come to us courtesy of IDG and the good offices of
Alex Iwata at O'Reilly Japan.They would cost about 10,000 yen to buy. Most
of the English speaking presenters are speaking that day--the English will
be translated to Japanese, but not the other way around.

If you are sure of coming, I can send one to you by mail, you can pick them
up here at the American Chamber of Commerce in Japan (right at Kamiacho
Crossing--or ???? Make a suggestion.

If you need one for the 2nd day, send email to kimiko@example.com, tell her
you are a TLUG member, and need a ticket for the second day, and TLUG only
has tickets for the first, and she may well send you one--of course thank
her for the tickets she sent for the 29th. They thought we would appreciate
those most, since we are something of a foreign group, and might not be so
interested in the mostly Japanese presentations of the 30th.
