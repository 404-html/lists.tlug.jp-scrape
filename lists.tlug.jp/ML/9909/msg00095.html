"Petersen Jens-Ulrik (NRC/Tokyo)" wrote:

> I recommend installing rpms from JRPM60:
> 
>         <ftp://ftp.kddlabs.co.jp:/Linux/jrpm/JRPM60/ALL/>
> 
> I am using packages from there with Redhat 6.0 and it works pretty
> well for me.
> 
> I recommend installing at least the following:
> 
> Canna-3.5b2-20JRPM60.i386.rpm
> jgrep-2.0jp-1JRPM60.i386.rpm               (optional)
> kinput2-canna-v3-2JRPM60.i386.rpm
> kon2-0.3.7-3JRPM60.i386.rpm
> kon2-font-0.3.7-3JRPM60.i386.rpm
> kterm-6.2.0-9JRPM60.i386.rpm
> less-332jp-6JRPM60.i386.rpm                (optional)
> locale-ja-2.1.1.1-2.noarch.rpm
> nkf-1.70p1-1JRPM60.i386.rpm                (optional)
> 
> [pgp-2.6.3ijp-2JRPM60.i386.rpm              (one way to get pgp)]
> 
> If you don't have XEmacs, but want it:
> 
> xemacs-21.1.4-28JRPM60.i386.rpm
> xemacs-Canna-21.1.4-28JRPM60.i386.rpm      (or xemacs-Wnn or ...)
> xemacs-el-21.1.4-28JRPM60.i386.rpm
> xemacs-info-21.1.4-28JRPM60.i386.rpm
> xemacs-sumo-19990729-4JRPM60.noarch.rpm
> xemacs-sumo-el-19990729-4JRPM60.noarch.rpm
