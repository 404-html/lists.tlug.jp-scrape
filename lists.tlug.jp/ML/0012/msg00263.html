minumum.

gs*-vflib is one of those !#"$% Japanese patches that has never been
acceptable to the upstream maintainer because it violates coding
standards and whatnot, and the Japanese maintainers seem happy to
chalk it up to jinshu sabetsu and not do it right.  (In this case,
both Ghostscript and VFlib are extremely complex, and it may very well
be that Ghostscript's "platform fonts" interface is simply not capable
of handling VFlib's needs.  But Peter told me a couple years back that
no one has ever approached him seriously about incorporating the VFlib
stuff.)
