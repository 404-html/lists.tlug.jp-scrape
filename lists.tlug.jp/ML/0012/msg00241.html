> How do I set my From & Reply-To address in Pine ?

For reply-to, put this in your .pinerc file: 

customized-hdrs          = Reply-To: ranma@example.com 
(but, in the case of mail to tlug, the reply-to field 
of posts the list get set to tlug@ I think). 

AFAIK, Pine looks at the Linux user's name (if you log in 
as ranma, then ranma) and the domain name setting 
(user-domain              = gmx.at) in deciding what goes 
into the From field. 
In the above case, the From field would be ranma@example.com .
