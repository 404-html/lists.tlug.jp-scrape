Well since this seems to be of interest.  I have had B-flets for about 3-4
months now.  It has worked like a charm, and while I didn't have pppoe on
the CD it was trivial to install rp-pppoe, and I had a working box up in
about 20 minutes.  As for the box, you get it from NTT.  Just like flets
you jump through the same hoops as far as connection stuff.  Anyway, it
has worked like a charm, and while I haven't gotten 10Mbit throughput it
has been more than fast enough. I have absolutely no complaints.  Any
questions just ask away.
