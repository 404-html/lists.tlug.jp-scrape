If I didn't at least mention in passing that I don't believe 
Evo will drive out Balsa/Gnomecard or any other similiar
combination anyone currently likes: No I don't think so.

Evo is just too slow and does too many things that so 
many people don't need.  Outlook has not driven out
OE or any of the other popular Windows mail clients, either.

Outlook is basically used in one place: business desktops in
an MS Exchange environment, and not even in all of those.
I expect success for Evo in the same sort of area and for the
same reasons people use Outlook there.  It is, after all,
an Outlook work-alike (only better, IMO).

encoding Japanese headers in UTF-8, but that was a couple
months ago, perhaps. Does anyone know if they fixed that yet?
