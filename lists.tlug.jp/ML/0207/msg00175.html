    bhoward> Is this a missing (or possibly incorrectly specified)
    bhoward> font or do I need to configure Mule something else in
    bhoward> order to display correctly?

Navigate the menus to Tools->Packages->Add, add a download site, then
M-x list-packages, check mule-ucs and install from packages.  Then
RTFM (C-h i m mule-ucs RET should win) and you should be in good
shape.

There are a small number of characters that you need the mule-unicode
charsets for, and Japanese Chinese Greek Russian are not among them.

Neither building from source (no docs---himi hasn't seen fit to add
the ones I contributed yet---for one thing) nor the packages provided
by the vendors are likely to work very well.
