True. I forgot to mention that RTFM supports both "info" 
and "RTFS" modes. They are, unfortunately, exclusive...

ln -s /usr/bin/info /usr/bin/rtfm (for info mode)
ln -s /usr/bin/less /usr/bin/rtfm (for RTFS mode, because 
less is more)

Unfortunately, in RTFS mode, rtfm is not as user-friendly, 
as you have to do nasty things like reading code. <shudder>
