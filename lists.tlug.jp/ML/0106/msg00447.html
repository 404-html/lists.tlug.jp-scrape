> means nothing to me... What's the name of the bar??

Tengu is the name of the place (it's a chain Izakaya) and Tokyo Eki-Mae
is the location of that one.  A map in on the TLUG web site here:

http://www.tlug.gr.jp/meeting.html

Go to Tokyo Station (JR) and go out the Yaesu above-ground
exit.  With your back to Tokyo station, go straight up the large
street as shown on the map.

Tengu is about 200 meters or so up the street, and it will be
on the right, with your back to Tokyo Station.
