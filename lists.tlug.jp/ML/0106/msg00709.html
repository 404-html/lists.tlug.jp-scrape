Dan Lindfield wrote:

>      PCI sound card found on your system VIA TECH IVT82C686_5

USB and your "Soundcard" (which is built into your chipset in this case) are both PCI devices.
PCI device may share interrupts. Normally this should not be the source of any problems
(Except for old windoze drivers, which couldn't cope with interrupt sharing...).

As someone else already pointed out, you should use the Kernel Sounddriver for this Chipset.
