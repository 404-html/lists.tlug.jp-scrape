Dear Tokyo Linux Users Group

The Linux Foundation would like to extend a discount to TLUG members for
Open Source Summit Japan 2017.
If you are interested in attending, please email your information (name and
company/group) to [notake (at) linuxfoundation.org] before May 17.
We will send a discount code within a few days.

* For attendees who register before May 14, the registration fee is US$425.

◇ Open Source Summit Japan 2017 ◇

May 31-June 2 at Tokyo Conference Center Ariake, Tokyo, Japan

LinuxCon, ContainerCon and CloudOpen have combined under one umbrella name
in 2017 and beyond - Open Source Summit.

Confirmed keynote speakers at this year’s event include:
- Brian Behlendorf, Executive Director, Hyperledger
- Philip DesAutels, Sr. Director of IoT, The Linux Foundation
- Arpit Joshipura, General Manager, Networking, The Linux Foundation
- Abby Kearns, Executive Director, Cloud Foundry Foundation
- Jim Zemlin, Executive Director, The Linux Foundation
- Linux Kernel Panel with Alice Ferrazzi, Gentoo Kernel Project Leader;
Greg Kroah-Hartman, Linux Foundation Fellow; Steven Rostedt, VMware; and
Dan Williams, Intel

Session highlights include:
- TensorFlow in the Wild: From Cucumber Farmer to Global Insurance Firm -
Kazunori Sato, Google
- Fast releasing and Testing of Gentoo Kernel Packages and Future Plans of
the Gentoo Kernel Project - Alice Ferrazzi, Gentoo Kernel Project Leader
- Testing at Scale - Andrea Frittoli, IBM
- Device-DAX: Towards Software Defined Memory - Dan Williams, Intel
- Kubernetes Ground Up - Vishnu Kannan, Google

Please see 'schedule' below and find out more!
http://events.linuxfoundation.org/events/open-source-summit-japan/program/schedule

Registration Fees for Open Source Summit Japan 2017:
- Standard Registration Fee: $425 (April 17, 2017 - May 14, 2017)
- Late Registration Fee: $500 (May 15, 2017 - Event)
- Academic Registration Fee: US$125**
*Student/Faculty attendees will be required to show a valid student/faculty
ID at registration.
**PROMOTION: The first 50 students to register for Open Source Summit Japan
will receive a complimentary registration pass!

Please feel free to share this invitation with your members.

Thank you very much!
