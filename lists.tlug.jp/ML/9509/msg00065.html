Someone on the linux-kernel mailing list figured out that the
video corruption after exiting the X Window System in 1.3.28/29
was caused by the custom VGA font utility (setfont).  Removing
it and using the ROM-based fonts avoids the problem.

I just got 1.3.30 this morning and it seems to have solved the
problem.
