			(address unknown)

Description of Events:

We will be meeting at noon at Hachiko and walk about 5 minutes
to a Mexican restaurant that costs about 1600 yen for all you
can eat Mexican food.  After lunch we will be moving like
a herd to the palatial CTI world headquarters for Linux 
demonstrations.  We estimate that we will arrive at
CTI HQ at 2:00 p.m.  

Proposed Topics for the meeting:

	ELF - Pros and Cons of conversion right now
	
	TeX and Japanese

	Getting InfoMagic CD-ROMS from Japan via mail 


Suckers or willing volunteers are still needed to agree
to speak.. :-)
