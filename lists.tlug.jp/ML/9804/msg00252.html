For anyone that's interested, PHT's been running a mud for about 2 years
now.  Just telnet to mud.pht.com, port 3000, if you like muds.  It
averages 40-50 people online simultaneously, and it has some nice
features.  it's a hybrid merc/envy/rom/circle mud with lots of
customization added and new features/etc.

Thought I'd mention it, since I haven't before.  There's also an IRC
server on that machine (access via irc.pht.com).
