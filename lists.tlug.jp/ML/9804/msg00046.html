> If you want to use Canna with Mule, it has to be XEmacs or an old
> version of FSF Emacs (19.34b with Mule compiled in).  FSFmacs 20.x
> supports neither Canna nor Wnn from what I've heard, only Quail.  I
> believe third parties have done the patches for like Debian-JP Linux
> and PJE, but the vanilla FSF version won't work.

the TurboLinux mule (based on 19.28 in 1.0, 19.34 in 1.4) works with Canna
or Wnn6.  The one in 1.4J also works with skk.  You could get our .src.rpm
and rebuild it on solaris from the .tar.gz (ie, use rpm2cpio to extract
the .tar.gz and patch files from the .src.rpm).  It's had a lot of
extensive work done on it to make it work properly with all three feps.
