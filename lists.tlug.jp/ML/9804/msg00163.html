 > Hi!  I'm the new maintainer of edict.el, a LISP package which provides

 > arrangement with Per Hammarlund.  Kudos to Per Hammarlund and Bob

And thanks for yours! I'd say edict.el is in good hands.

 > variable and function names are subject to change.  It is known to

I've got it working on XEmacs 20.3. Had to install it manually. Hmm

finally have a byte-compiled edict!
