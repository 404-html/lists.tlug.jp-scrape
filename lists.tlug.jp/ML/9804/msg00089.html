OK, I'm shooting for an initial beta of TL 1.9 on Monday 4/13.  I will be
making 10 copies of this, all of which I want to send to TLUG members for
testing.  This beta will hopefully work in both English and Japanese
(depending on which boot disk you use).  New features will include:

libc5.4.x AND glibc 2.0.7
XFree86 3.3.2 plus XSuSE GLINT (3Dlabs) server
New X configuration tool (TurboXconfigurator 4.0)
TurboPNP - nice front-end to isapnptools.

The second beta, coming later, will have numerous desktop fixes and
improvements as well.

Basically the first beta will be all about making sure that stuff works
properly under the hybridized libc5/glibc environment.

So, I'm starting my official tester list.  I need ten of you.  Please
reply privately with your name, email addr, and mailing address if you
want to join.  The CDs will be sent to you at our expense.
