My wife wanted me to print out her resume for her.  She made it in
microsoft word for Win95 (I don't have enough space on my linux partition
for applix-beta..) .. ok, so, we have no printer at home.  So I said,
okay, i'll print it at work.  I don't have MS Word at work.  So, I set up
a 'postscript printer on FILE:' in '95 at home, and printed to that,
ftp'ed the .ps file to work.  prints out, but looks awful - fonts are so
low-res that they're barely readable... is there any way to fix the fonts
in a postscript file?  Looking at the output from some .ps files generated
by Linux netscape and emacs, I see embedded fonts in there, but not in the
one generated by Windows... what can I do besides have her retype it on
Applix here at work?
