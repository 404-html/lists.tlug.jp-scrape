Hi all,

I'm in the middle of upgrading the POP mail client XFMail 1.2 to
what will probably be the final 1.3 beta.

Relatively scant documentation is making the configuration a
little trickey. It now supports multiple-pop servers but still
doesn't suppport Japanese. It's got a great interface to the
ispell program, full PGP support, sound finally works, and there
seems to be support for news (but I haven't messed around with
that yet.)

All-in-all I like what I see. Some of the major annoyances (like
crashing when you open another program) seem to have been fixed
- I'm still hoping for Japanese support one of these days.

Regards,

Jim S.
