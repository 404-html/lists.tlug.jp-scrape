I guess I'm somewhere kinda in between... on the one hand, I like things
that are easy to use b/c I don't have to mess around with them, but on the
other hand, I do like them for the same reason as you, Scott... I like to
hack and around and mess things up... I guess one thing that I haven't lost
from my "youth" (after going to a few TLUG events, I'm not sure if that's a
