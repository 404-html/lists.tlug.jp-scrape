  • Subject: [tlug] Announcement, Hiroshima Linux Users Group

On October 2002, the Hiroshima Linux Users Group (HLUG for short) will
be born.  What HLUG hopes to provide is a friendly atmosphere full of
excitement where everyone will be able to help, learn, and work with
each other.

The purpose of HLUG will be to exchange information on the use of Linux
and open source tools and to advocate Linux, the Free Software
Foundation, and Open Source whenever possible.

For more information go to http://hlug.linuxorbit.com
or directly to my site http://www.noacchi.com/hlug.html
