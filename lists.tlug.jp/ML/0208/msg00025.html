Try this place (Interlinks Zoot service):
    <http://www.interlink.or.jp/adsl/index.html>

I am using this service and I am pretty happy. They give me one static 
IP, and I can run my own server (Linux) (mail, web, etc.). I pay YEN 
2000 per month for this.

Have had no real problems so far.

I also think Usen offer your aout 6 static global IP addresses for about 
YEN 6000 per month, but their installation price may be a bit expensive 
(ca  YEN 30000), and their pipe to the Internet may be questionable. But 
it will give you 100 Mbps to their datacenter.
