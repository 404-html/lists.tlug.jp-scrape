More a question than a comment: Since TLUG's official (defined as
"acceptable for posting) languages are English and Japanese, it
raises the question "Why?"  It's your time, so you (and the others)

perhaps a wasted effort, in so far as being able to communicate in

content should be translated into all of the supported languages
(list archives excepted, of course; no on has that much time),
since having only the top page available in multiple languages
