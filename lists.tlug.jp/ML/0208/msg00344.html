<the rest of this snipped>
 
 Sorry to answer my own post, but figured I should follow up on this--I
 got an email from Mr. Bouyer, who seems to be doing much of Gentoo's
 Japanese work.  (He's actually an mlterm fan, however)  :).  

 He wrote back that he hadn't done much work with it, but it would work
 if you called it with
> 
XMODIFIERS="@example.com=kinput2" LANGUAGE=en_US LC_CTYPE=ja_JP kterm -xim -km
euc

I tested this and does work. 

He also mentioned that they're working on several other Japanese
projects--at present, mlterm is in ebuilds, albeit masked (however I
unmasked it and tried it--works without problem) kon etc. I have a
complete list on my page at
http://home.nyc.rr.com/computertaijutsu/jpninpt.html down at the bottom,
in the Gentoo section.  

Anyway, hope this is of interest to some of you.
