> 2. "Xlib: connection to "melnjf.mel.go.jp:0.0" refused by server
>     Xlib: Client is not authorized to connect to Server
>     Error: Can't open display: melnjf.mel.go.jp:0.0"
> 
>    (when I typed:
>      rsh cray "exec /usr/openwin/bin/xterm -display MyPC:0.0 </dev/null &"

You need to authroize yourself to access your Xdisplay.

This can be done from on your pc (melnjf.mel.go.jp) (If that isn7t your
desktop then theres another problem) typing xhost +, or xhost + <cray>

Or, my reccommendation would be:

xauth extract - melnjf.mel.go.jp:0.0 | rsh cray xauth merge - 
then run the rsh with the xterm again.
