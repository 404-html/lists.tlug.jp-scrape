I will be posting the list of all library materials that I cataloged at the
meeting sometime in the next few days (probably this weekend).  If you have
something checked out that you don't see on the list, please drop me a line
and let me know what it is so I can add it to our inventory.

I will also post what is currently checked out, and who has it.  If you have
anything checked out but you don't see your name there, please let me know
so I can add it to the list.

Finally, I have been informed that we have 10,000 yen available to spend on
materials for the library.  Since the library is everyone's resource, I
would like to gather suggestions from all of you on what you would like to
see added to the library. This could be a good opportunity for the
adventurous to try out EVote, too :-)
