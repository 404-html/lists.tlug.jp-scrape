Not necessarily, but at least willing to take a stab at it and be proved
wrong. I merely believe that (borrowing the words of Fred Brooks) there
is more of accident than of essence in the current problems of code i18n.

are outweighed by the 'accidentally difficult'.

>      Matt> Are there good Free fonts for Arabic and Devanagari?

it seems to support Arabic but not Devanagari. Perhaps one of their
non-free fonts does, I haven't looked.

  One interesting thing I did notice in the Cyberbit notes is that a
table is included inside the font, to help sort out the mess of
Arabic initial/medial/final glyph differences. Useful.
