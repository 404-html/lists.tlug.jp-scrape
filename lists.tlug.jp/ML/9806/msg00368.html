ESS AudioDrive soundcards.  Not only do they not work with Linux, they
have no 16-bit DMA setting, which kind of means that they're not even as
good as the basic SB 16 value card..

Also, the DEC 500 (?) AF-revision ethernet chip... the Linux de4x5 module
will see it and use it, but it has problems.  76% packet loss, to be
precise, over a local 10baseT link :)
