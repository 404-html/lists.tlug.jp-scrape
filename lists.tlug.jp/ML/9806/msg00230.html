Dear SunWorld Subscriber:

"Open source" software has been getting a fair amount of press
these days, but many people, even those who sell products built
on open source code, have a hard time saying the simplest things
about it. How many people use Apache? Your guess is as good as
ours. And how many Apache users also run sendmail? Yikes!  In an
effort to provide a modicum of clarity to these muddy waters, we
invite you to fill out our open source reader survey. What
software do you use? Where? What influence does open source
software have on your life? Let us know, and we'll pass the
knowledge on.

http://www.sun.com/sunworldonline/swol-06-1998/swol-06-readersurv
