> How do I get a console to monitor real-time the errors?  In SunOS, we had
> one come up in OpenLook auto, so I never had to config one on my own.  Is
> that just teeing messages written to /var/adm/messages?

Just do a "tail -f /var/adm/messages" and in another console a "tail -f
/var/adm/syslog" and you get everything that's logged. However,
I wouldn't do it unless I had trouble: normal output is
generally pretty boring and just eats desktop space.
