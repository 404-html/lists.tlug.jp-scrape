There's also a pretty good Japanese-language book called
Networking Linux (yes, the title is in English), that I
believe I may have mentioned a while back on the TLUG list.
It's by Yamada Akira and Hane Hideya (Ascii Press, ISBN
4-7561-1291-9, 1997).  The Japanese is clear and not
difficult to understand, and there are a large number of
charts, screen shots, and graphics to assist.  I would
recommend it both to native Japanese readers and non-native
readers with a high intermediate or greater reading level.
Even an intermediate reader could get quite a bit out of it,
I think.  List price = 2,800 yen.
