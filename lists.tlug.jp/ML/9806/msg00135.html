>>>>> "Eric" == Eric Standlee and Family <e-w.standlee@example.com> writes:

    Eric> If I just do the Mule/fonts install is that all I need for
    Eric> Japanese?  Or should I go the entire <distribution>-JP way?
    Eric> I just want the ability to read and write in Japanese, not
    Eric> necessarily have all of my menus and things in Japanese...

No, you need printing too, right?  If just electronic text is enough,
mule is all you need.

There are no working programs to print out of a released Emacs,
unfortunately; there's a beta "ps-print.el" that works with Emacs
20.2.93 or so, and possibly with XEmacs 21, but ....

If you want pretty docs, you probably need pTeX and the rest; and
Ghostscript with VFlib.  There are some text file filters and
Dp-note, a shareware Japanese wordprocessor too.

HTH
