On Thu, 11 Jun 1998, Kei Furuuchi wrote:

> Probably it is easy to send command thru lpr. I don't know how to send 
> mail to fax thru sendmail especially when a fax modem is a modem that
> is connected to ISP thru diald and pppd. How sendmail suspends diald
> and pppd to send a fax?

Oh, OK, that clarifies things.  I'm sorry if I miss understood the
original question.  This I can answer :-)  You can't do that. To send a
fax through the modem, you have to disconnect from the ISP and send your
fax, then re-connect.  If you want an always-on fax line, you could use a
setup similar to what I used to run: a fax modem plugged into an analog
port on an ISDN TA.  My ISP connections were all done through the TA, and
the fax modem was on another serial port as a 7 x 24 fax number using the
dial-in number on my ISDN line.

You could also do this with two POTS lines, of course, but it's much
cheaper to do it with ISDN.
