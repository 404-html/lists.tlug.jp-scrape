Hi all,

During the last week or so I've had the opportunity to work with both
FreeBSD (extensively) and also with Solaris x86.  Although there were
some setup features I liked about FreeBSD, there just wasn't much after
the install was complete.  As for Solaris x86...it cost how much???  I
couldn't believe the setup, the lack of documentation, the lack of
supported drivers, etc.  I can't believe it's actually a commercial
product.  Even FreeBSD supported everything I have in my system.

Today I went back to trusted RH4.2 with CDE1.2.  I'm happy and don't
think I'll be going any where for awhile.  I like hearing others
comments on what they've thought of the forementioned products if you've
used them.

Take it easy.

Blaine
