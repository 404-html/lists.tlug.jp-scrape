Hi, folks--

Just to let everybody know ... I contacted Kakugawa-san (the author)
about my translation of the VFlib Q & A. I sent along a copy of my
draft ... and though I didn't (and don't) consider it really a
finished product, I guess he thinks it's fine as is, except for a

program. Anyway, he says he'll distribute my translation along as part
of the VFlib docs --- when and through what channels, he didn't

arrange to have an HTML version posted on the TLUG website. I'll write 
again when I know more.
