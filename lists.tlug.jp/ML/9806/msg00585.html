Try what Kei Furuuchi already proposed: do a "telnet
<your.internet.provider> 110" and look whether you get a
sensible response. If so, you popclient went bananas. Get the
latest sources ( they are from 1996 or so ) and compile it on
your system ( this is a lot better than just taking some
binaries floating around - believe me, there's nothing like
compiling your own binaries. Saves you a lot of trouble ).
> Also, trying to use popclient to retrieve mail from my e-w.standlee@example.com
> account gives me errors:  doPOP3:  socket:  Unknown error and one other
> similar one too.
Hmmm....go ahead and compile from the sources. This stinks.
> Now, if only I knew how to make smail and popclient get my mail.
> Also, I have to do all of my connection stuff as root.  Is that right?
> Shouldn't I be able to pon as esstand???
Well, you have to. If you set up PPP you have to put it onto a
physical device and they are only accessible to root for very
good reasons. Actually, mailing and fetching mail can be done
with normal user level.
