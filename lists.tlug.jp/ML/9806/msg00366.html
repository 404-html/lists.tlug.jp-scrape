>     Manuel> Hi!  I am using Canna in Mule (2.3).

I just noticed that you are talking about using canna in mule.  What I
said before still applies, but I also know the solution to your problem of
the backspace key.

>     Manuel> * Entering kana, the <backspace> and <delete> key are
>     Manuel> unbound, and I have to use C-h to erase a character.  This
>     Manuel> is very un-Emacs-ish and I always mix up keys.  I could
>     Manuel> start looking for the keymaps, but...

I believe the following is used to be able to use backspace while
you are entering kana.  (In .emacs)
   
      (define-key canna-mode-map [backspace] [?\C-h])
      (define-key canna-mode-map [delete] [?\C-h])
