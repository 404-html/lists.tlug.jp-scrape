Forward feed error correction is that adding codes to signal 
improves reliablity. This is characteristic of the
channel. if there is no accuracy there is no point adding
codes. I really don't know about 660bps. the number is just
coming out of a hat. But, I estimated the number far below
the current modem thruput because I can argue that the
number is nothing to do with 8khz nor 3.1khz which is
charasteristic of telephone channel, which of course relates 
to FEC discussion. I took the number far below because this
means it is free of channel characteristcs but relates to
human capability. This is like bee can see ultraviolet or
bat can hear ultrasonic. Human put ultra because he cannot
see nor hear ultra thing. So it is not channel nor error
correction but, human capability yet still stated in digital 
term to convince that human can communicate other than text
consciously or subconssciously stated somehow in phoney
numbers.

feelings are still based on the information sent thru (660-25) 
bps.
