Maybe everyone but me already knows about this, but the
Linux counter was mentioned in the May issue of Linux
Journal (p. 94), so I thought I would put the URL up here
for those who don't know about it.

If you register yourself there, it will add you to the
official count list and make a custom graphic for you,
showing your registered Linux user number.  You can save it
to your disk and use it on your home page.  Mine is
72,000-something, so it seems that not many people have
actually registered there.  Linus' current guess at the
number of users is 6,000,000, according to this page.
There's a big discrepancy between the number of estimated
users and the number who have registered, so let's see if
TLUG can't change that a little :-)  Register at:

http://counter.li.org/
