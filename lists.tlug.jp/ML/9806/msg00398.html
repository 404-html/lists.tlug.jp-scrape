we need a few people to pictures at the meeting.  We usually have people
bring cameras, however at the April meeting there were no pictures, so
the web page of the event does not have pictures.  These digital moments
of TLUG history are great for fun and enjoyment. The pictures also help to

meeting or for people that want to come, but are afraid of strangers.

pictures to some public location for viewing.

of about 5 numbers for portable phones of members that were going to
