In return for what? The "GnuPG doesn't work for me either"? :)

Well, you already have libcompface installed otherwise you wouldn't see my
ugly mug in Sylpheed.

The other bit is to convert a 48x48 bitmap into a stream of ASCII junk
inserted in the X-Face: header of the e-mail. I see that Jim has beaten me
to it in supplying a link to something which will take care of that for you.
Personally I used this one (long) liner:

xbmtopbm "$@" | pbmtoicon | sed -e 1,2d -e '$s/$/,/' | tr -d '\011\012' | tr ',' '\012' | pr -l1 -t -w22 -3 -s, | sed -e 's/$/,/' -e 's/\(0x....\)\(0x....\)\(0x....\),/\1,\2,\3,/' | compface
