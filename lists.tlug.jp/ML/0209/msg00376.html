On 9/21/2002, "n" <yeehi@example.com> wrote:

>I want to know what you think about the ntt Flets plan for 100 Mbps
>up/downloads.

You will probably never encounter an FTP site that will let you have
anywhere near 100 megabits of bandwidth.  When I had 100 megabits at work,
I never found a site that delivered more than
20 megabits of download bandwidth, and that happened only once.

The strength of B-Flets is that it doesn't have the distance sensitivity of
DSL, nor the crosstalk problems with ISDN that DSL can also have.
