Hehehe, good luck finding a NAS that will do that :-)

A NAS that supports *nix is a cinch to do NFS, but not AFS.

As I described in my previous post, there's nothing wrong with 
NFS, but it should not be used on an outside network.  That's
why I suggested popping a second NIC in each box that wants to talk
to the NAS and (Note: NAS and SAN are two very different things)
and run them on a non-routable (e.g., 192.168.1.0/24) network
and be sure none of them are routing between the inside and outside
networks.
