Quick correction on that (as I tried it and got an error message) :)



To add the uid, you first do gpg edit-key <keyname>.
For example
gpg edit-key Scott

This gives me the key for the scottro@example.com  Then you're given
a  >  prompt.  That's where you can put in the adduid.


You then go through the steps Jonathan describes in his QND guide, real
name, comment if you like, email address.
