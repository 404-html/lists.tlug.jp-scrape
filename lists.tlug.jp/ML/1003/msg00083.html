Curt Sampson wrote:

Giving a presentation can be pretty intimidating if it's in a language
you don't feel you speak particularly well. Perhaps one way around
this would be to have panel discussions so that the burden doesn't
rest all on one person. The panalists could provide the moderator with
discussion topic points in advance, and then talk about what they're
interested in or knowledgable about without bearing the full burden of
the presentation.
I very much like this idea. I think the Open Meeting discussion last weekend went well but it was free-flowing and long (which is the point, I realize). A more focused technical discussion for an hour with a panel and moderator would be an excellent way to get more people involved.
