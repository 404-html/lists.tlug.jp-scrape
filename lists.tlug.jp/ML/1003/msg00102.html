The order of boot device (hard disk, cd, flash drive, ...) should be configured/configurable in the laptop BIOS. If the Ubuntu CD doesn't seem to boot, I'd poke around there to see if maybe the system recovery shuffled something in the BIOS parameters.

Once you get the CD to be your first boot device, and manage to get the Ubuntu CD running you can look up on Google for a way to rescue your Grub (Ubuntu 9.10 and following use Grub 2, and that might require a slightly different procedure from its predecessors).

G'luck!
