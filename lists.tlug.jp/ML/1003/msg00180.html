On Fri, Mar 26, 2010 at 5:52 PM, Edward Middleton <edward.middleton@example.com> wrote:
On 03/26/10 17:32, Deepika Ghuriani wrote:
> No i cant boot frm super grub it gives me errors while recovering the
> partitions.

What are the errors messages.  Looking at your partition table displayed
by supergrub something is seriously wrong.  Your first partion hda1
doesn't have a filesystem type, but presumably is your windows partition
because windows isn't going to fit on your second partition hda2 which
is only 100MB.  I would try to boot into Ubuntu, then backup any
important files.  Then try recovering your windows partition table.
After that reinstall GRUB to your MBR to get dual boot working.
