This quite likely has something to do with ACPI. Try searching Google
for something like: acpi linux "fan doesn't run"

I found the following link which may be helpful:

http://www.naos.co.nz/hardware/laptop/acer-361evi/x94.html#AEN213

You might try disabling ACPI and APM in the kernel, unless it is
already, in which case try *enabling* it.
