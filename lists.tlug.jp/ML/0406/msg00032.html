Smashing, but you rather mis-understood Steve's question. He was asking
about *caching*, which is what your browser does automatically when you
visit a web page. Take a look at Edit -> Preferences -> Advanced ->
Cache in Mozilla to see what I mean.

The nice thing that Mozilla does is caches a dynamic page the way it
was displayed, so you can use the Back button to revisit it and view
it without having to resubmit form data. Firefox seems to want you to
resubmit the form data, though I am not convinced that it is really
doing that due to load times that I have witnessed.
