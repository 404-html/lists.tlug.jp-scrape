Gunar Schnell wrote:

> Yast does not complain - it finds the wireless card and configured it (DHCP)
> but no network connection could be established. Currently it's configured as
> /eth1
> I tried ifdown /eth0 to maybe avoid interference with the LAN card but no
> success.
> 
> Does anybody has an idea where the problem might be and what to look for?

Which driver are you using (ipw2100/ipw2200 or driverloader/ndiswrapper)?

What is the output of "iwconfig" (as root)?

Say your AP's essid is "tuxnet" and your WEP passphrase is
"penguin", then you should be able to configure it using

iwconfig eth1 mode managed
iwconfig eth1 essid tuxnet
iwconfig eth1 key s:penguin
