A: ".... but usually ST gets his phone unlocked when he travels and then switches his SIM card out for an international one so that calls and texts are cheaper."

B: "I'm not sure how we'll be able to get in contact when I'm not at the hotel. My phone doesn't use a SIM card so I won't be able to switch out for an international card. I'll be able to check email on my iPad but only where I can get access to free Wi-Fi. I'm not sure how prevalent that is in Tokyo."
