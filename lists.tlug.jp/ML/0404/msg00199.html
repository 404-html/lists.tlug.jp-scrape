[tlug] [pre-announcement] TLUG May Technical Meeting

  • Date: Sat, 24 Apr 2004 00:08:34 +0900
  • From: Alberto Tomita <atomita@example.com>
  • Subject: [tlug] [pre-announcement] TLUG May Technical Meeting
TLUGers,

With our President Mauro out of the country
(he said he'll be back soon) and the Golden 
Week holidays just on the week when our regular
bi-monthly technical meeting would happen (the
2nd Saturday of May, the 8th), it was suggested
that we change the date to May 15th. 

Given that at this point we still did not
receive confirmations from our guest speakers,
we may indeed want to change the meeting date.
Therefore, please mark May 15 in your calendars
as the new date for the TLUG May Technical 
Meeting.

The meeting location will also change. We thank
Ian Thomson at Wall Street Associates for having
sponsored TLUG until now. In the past weeks TLUG
was given an offer to held its meetings at the
facilities of a Linux-related company located in
Shibuya, and following the Open Source spirit of
giving every one a chance to contribute, we gladly
accept the new meeting location sponsorship.

More details will be sent in an announcement message,
as soon as we fix the meeting agenda. We are trying
to confirm the topics previously scheduled:

   Javascript debugger - Taro Matsuzawa
   Gnome meeting - Shin Michimuko

If anyone wants to propose new topics and volunteer
as guest speakers, you are also welcome.

Summarizing:
   1) new meeting date: May 15 (Saturday)
   2) new meeting place: Shibuya
   3) topics: to be confirmed/looking for volunteers

thank you.
