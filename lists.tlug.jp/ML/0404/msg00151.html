Ok, this was working until I blithely upgraded from mutt-devel 1.5.5i to
1.5.6i.  (FreeBSD 5.2.1-release p4)

In my .muttrc I have


fcc-hook "mpolak"  "~/Mail/NJIT-sent"
fcc-hook .*.  "~/Mail/SENT"

So, things going to mpolak went to the NJIT folder.  

Since the upgrade, they don't.  I've looked through the man pages on
muttrc and it ~seems~ I'm doing it right.  I tried changing the order of
the two as well, since I've found that some send-hooks at least seem to
take the last argument.

Lastly, I tried commenting out the .*. one--then the fcc line in the
header is simply blank.

Looking at their bug reports, it seems all fcc-hook bugs are quite old.
I haven't checked on irc yet.  Looking through the recent changes page
at mutt.org doesn't help.

I'd be grateful for any suggestions.
