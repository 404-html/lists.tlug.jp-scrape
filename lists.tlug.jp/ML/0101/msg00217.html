I use an Optiplex GX1p (450 MHz P III). The on-board video is an ATI Rage Pro
(or something like that), which works with X but is not great. The on-board
sound chip is a Crystal cs4236 and gives a little click before each sound is
played -- very annoying. I can't play more than 1 song on audio CDs -- it just
stops. I sometimes get CD read/seek error on data CDs as well. I've tried other
CD players with the same result: it appears to be a controller problem. The
motherboard has a 440BX/ZX chip set and the IDE controller is
 IDE interface: Intel Corporation 82371AB PIIX4 IDE (rev 01)

Other than that, I've had no problems with the on-board ethernet controller
(3c905B Cyclone) or any other component.
