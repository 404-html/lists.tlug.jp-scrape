Funny you should ask...

I've been calling GOL fairly regularly, trying to expedite my application. 
The first hurdle was NTT didn't like my address written in romanji--had to 
be kanji. That held things up for a day or two.

I called GOL on Wed asking what the status is, and it took them until 
Friday to get back to me--they claim that they have only one 
English-speaking contact at eAccess and she doesn't always reply quickly. 
Also, they said that they have a database from eAccess that tells the 
status of an application, but they can never get it to work and end up 
having to contact the same unreliable person at eAccess.

After waiting three days for an answer, they finally told me that my 
application is now in the hands of NTT, which has to check my phone line to 
see if it can accept DSL...no prospective date when NTT might decide to do 
the check.
