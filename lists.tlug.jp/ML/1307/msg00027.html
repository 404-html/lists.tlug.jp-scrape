Hi all
A lot of you already know me but for those who don't, I work for Axsh and we make an open source IaaS system called Wakame-vdc. We already have a couple of major companies that have built VPS clouds powered by Wakame-vdc but being an open source project, we would like to reach out to the general community a bit more and teach people how to set up their own clouds with Wakame-vdc.
To do so we are starting the Wakame Users Group. It's following a similar format to TLUG technical meetings. They're going to be very informal meetings with a presentation first and nomikai afterwards. I figured that TLUG people might also be interested in that virtualization stuff, especially if it's open source. If any of you are, we'd love to see you at our first meeting on Friday, July 26th. 

Meeting info follows below:

Axsh co.'s Wakame-vdc introduction and demo
---------------------------------------------------------
During this first meeting we will provide a VirtualBox image with
Centos6.4 and Wakame-vdc installed. Using that image we will demo
Wakame-vdc and give a workshop on how to use it. You are encouraged to
bring your own laptop with VirtualBox installed so you can use the image
and follow along with the workshop but this is not required. Feel free
to invite others who you think may be interested in playing and using
Wakame-vdc as well.

Meeting info and access published onhttp://wakameusersgroup.org.

Time: Friday July 26th, 2013

22:00pm.
Admission: Free!!!
