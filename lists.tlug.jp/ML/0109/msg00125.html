errr..
I said boot disk not rescue disk.
also
>panic : VFS : Unable to mount root fs on 03:01
you usually got that error when your "/" partition location has changed (location is different from lilo)
in lilo.conf there is a line which says "root=/dev/hda1" ( the "hd??" part  could be different in your case) 
which tell the kernel where your "/" is.
 
so in your case I think 3 thing could have happened
1.) Your rescue disk is incorrect
2.) Your partition table has been changed
3.) Your disk might be corrupted
 
It will really help us to help you if you can tell us exactly what happen and what did you do
also what is your linux knowledge level
