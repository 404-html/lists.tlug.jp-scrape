here is the way I repaired from this sort of thing
boot from a setup cdrom, at the shell prompt do the following

mount /dev/hda1 /mnt
(swap /dev/hda1 for the root device on your system)
cp /vmlinuz /mnt/vmlinuz
chroot /mnt lilo
reboot

this should effectivly bring everything back.
you may also want to check that your root partition is ok and maybe a few
other things.
