Hi Kris,
I am also new to Tokyo. I have found the ADSL service here is cool. 
Though yahooBB is offering the ADSL service at low cost but the waiting 
period is quite frustrating. I had applied for it almos 2months ago but 
till this date, I am only receiving the intimation from them that 
"please wait ..... blah blah ... it is going to start soon". Currently, 
I am using NTT flets with Zoot. It is cool me (i am a high bandwidth 
internet user). The ADSL service by NTT-FLETS costs 3800yen/pm and I got 
it within seven days. As all the application procedure is in Japanese, I 
took the help of my Japanese friends to complete it. Added to this ADSL, 
u have to subscribe for ISP. I am using Zoot and it is  giving static IP 
in 2000yen/pm. The application procedure is easy and there is no waiting 
time. I am using linux in my home setup (RH7.2beta). Infact, I have 
found that setting up the pppoe client is easy most from Linux. I am 
using rp-pppoe....
