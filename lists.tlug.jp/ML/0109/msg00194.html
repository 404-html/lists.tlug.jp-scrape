For DVD support have a look at mplayer, xine or vlc. Of course, if you
are living in the US you can get arrested for that. The players
mentioned above have nice DVD playback, but lack the DVD menus. There is
another project (from Sweden I think) that is supposed to have good
menu support, have a look at freshmeat to find it. The DVD filesystem
(UDF) is supported in the 2.4.x kernels, for "encrypted" DVDs you need
libcss (or what they call it now). For ac3 you need a52dec (formerly
ac3dec). For more information you can look at www.linuxvideo.org.
