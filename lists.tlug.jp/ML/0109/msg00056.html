    Theodore> I have Netscape setup to read Japanese, but I haven't

    Theodore> suggestions would be appreciated.

apt-get install xemacs21-mule-canna-wnn
xemacs -l vm -f vm

Not only do you get working Japanese, but people won't give you grief
for poor formatting and/or HTML in your email.  Disclaimer: GNU Emacs
is fine with me despite my obvious bias, but VM works better with
XEmacs.

historically been very unstable with Japanese.  And Java.  And any
other feature starting with J (jwz jinx, anyone?)
