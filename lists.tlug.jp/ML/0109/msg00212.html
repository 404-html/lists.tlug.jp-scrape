> Ok, does it mean that I'm going to change the filesystem to "ext3" ? If so,

If it's already ext3 then the grep should show nothing and you don't
have anything todo. In that case it is a bit weird that you have to fsck
your partition as this usually shouldn't be necessary with ext3...
