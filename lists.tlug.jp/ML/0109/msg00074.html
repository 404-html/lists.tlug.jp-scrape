> How can a execute a comand, let say, every 5 minutes (something like
> './analog > gigi.html')?

Try cron. Edit your crontab by 'crontab -e', and put whatever job.

*/5 * * * * /path/to/analog > /path/to/gigi.html

See 'man 5 crontab' for crontab format.
