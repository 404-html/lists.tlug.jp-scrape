There's actually no website involved, unless you mean FB's. The
notifications which popped
up (they've stopped now that I've turned them off for the desktop)
were just text - no links or
anything - and were the same as the ones I get on my phone and tablet.

My conclusion is that FB invoked some sort of notification mechanism which led
to FF using libnotify. I guess I should be calling them "push
notifications" rather
than "pop-ups". (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Push_technology)
