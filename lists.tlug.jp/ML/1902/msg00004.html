       TLUG - Tokyo Linux Users Group February Nomikai
       =========================================================


       Date:    February 8 (Friday)
       Time:    19:30-21:30
       Place:   Sudacho Shokudo
                Akihabara UDX building 3F,
                4-14-1, Sotokanda, Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo, 101-0021
                https://gurunavi.com/en/g095630/rst/
       Cost:    around 4000 yen

       To help us make sure that there will be room for everyone
       to seat, please register your attendance here:

       https://tlug.doorkeeper.jp/events/86787

       This event is open to all Linux users and friends, and is
       to promote casual Linux (and non-Linux) related discussions
       over a few cold ones. Even if you're not a Linux user or a
       LUG member, but want to hang out with Linux people, please come.

       If you get lost,
       or simply want to get in touch with us, please call:

          Justin............050-5806-9898

       For more information, access the TLUG web page at
       http://tlug.jp/wiki/Meetings:2019:02

       See you there!

Reminder:
Technical Meetings are held on the 2nd Saturday of odd-numbered months and
the Nomikai Meetings are held on the 2nd Friday of even-numbered months.
There are, of course, exceptions to this rule, so it is best to keep an eye
on the mailing list.
