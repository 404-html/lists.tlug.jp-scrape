For me it has varied from computer to computer. I was looking at computers during the pre-christmas sales and found a Dell Insperion 14-5000 I'm quite happy with. I even found instructions on youtube that showed how to upgrade to an SD disk. I also looked at an XPS 13 that I couldn't find the right combination for installing ubuntu.
