> I never tried a USB keyboard - I don't have one with

A mouse should actually be enough to get you logged in and let you
pair your BT keyboard. Remember that you need to use a USB-OTG cable,
not a regular cable. Most USB-OTG cables are pigtails with a USB A
host recepticle on the other end into which any normal USB keyboard or
mouse can be plugged in.
