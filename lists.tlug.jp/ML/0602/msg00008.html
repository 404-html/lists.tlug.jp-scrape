I was just thinking - I think the way that Josh handled the magazine 
article was just about perfect:

1) He wrote and posted a first draft version.

2) Several people used that as a base and sent back their suggestions 
for changes.

3) Bits and pieces from these suggestions were incorporated by Josh into 
the 2nd draft, which was posted.

4) Several people repeated step #2.

5) Josh repeated step #3.

6) Steps #2 & #3 were repeated a few times and....

7) Josh posted a semi-final version open to last minute suggestions

8) At least one was sent.

9) The article was finalized and sent out to the publisher and to the group.

I mean... hey... that went really smoothly I think!  People had their 
say, the editor (Josh) did a good job of taking the good bits and 
incorporating them into the master copy, and it all went so smoothly 
that maybe we forgot to consider that it could have been otherwise.  I 
think we all owe a big Thank You to Josh for how he handled it and for 
his youthful enthusiasm about everything in general.  Go Josh!

Sincerely,

Lyle
