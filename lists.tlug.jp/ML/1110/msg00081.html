Half-serious, for sure. But it does show (if not prove) two things:
touch/swipe/pinch interfaces are way more natural than
keyboard/mouse/trackpad ones; and people (i.e., kids now) whose first
contact with computing has been a touch screen device will see thing very
differently from you and me.

a 1000x1000 cell Excel grid, but that sort of usage is even now the
minority.
