> No how do I do it automaticaly? That is, most probably, from the command

In the past I used 'mplayer -dumpstream' and mencoder/oggencode for this.

This is not a 'fire single command and forget'-approach thou - you
have to install some requirements (i.e. x264 or other encoder libraries).
Once everything is in place you can ofcourse create a script doing 
everything in one run.

My notes on the setup are at 
http://fluxcoil.net/doku.php/snippets/mencoder .
