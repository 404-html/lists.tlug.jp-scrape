Hello Everyone,
My new employer, ISI-Dentsu (IT solutions provider subsidiary of Dentsu), is looking to hire two people. I believe both of these positions are in Shinagawa in the Open Innovation Lab which was started earlier this year. The Open Innovation Lab fuses advance technology applied research with business development to develop new businesses via leading applications of advanced technologies. These new business opportunities are then transferred to the appropriate sales and marketing teams within ISI-Dentsu and Dentsu. Basically they are making various prototypes of new technologies and services and doing some cool stuff. 

1) Mobile/Web Application Programmer
Description: Design and coding of HTML5 web applications, including design and coding of client-facing screens as well as server side development. Desired Skills: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, iOS, Google App Engine, Java, Slim3 (as many as possible) Desired Experience: Development of professional web sites using jQuery, and/or iPhone app development experience 
Knowledge of Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash 5, is a plus.
https://www.isidgroup.com/u/job.phtml?job_code=189 (Japanese)

2) UI/UX Designer
Description: Responsible for design and development of user interfaces. Specifically, you will make mock-ups, handle visual design and information design for both web application and smartphone application development. In addition to doing your own design, you may be responsible to work with external design companies to develop and create new user interfaces. Desired Skills/Experience: Discernment for good user interface design, experience developing effective user interfaces for web applications and mobile software; experience coding designs (HTML/CSS/JavaScript/ActionScript/etc); PhotoShop, Illustrator, Flash 5 skills Knowledge of ergonomics, statistical analysis, ISO guidelines is a plus, as are 3DCG and movie creation skills. 
https://www.isidgroup.com/u/job.phtml?job_code=141 (Japanese)
Please contact me if you are interested or have any questions: jimm@example.com 

ISI-Dentsu home page: http://www.isid.co.jp/english/

Thanks,
 -Jim
