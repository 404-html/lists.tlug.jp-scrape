   TLUG - Tokyo Linux Users Group "nomikai" 6/1
   ============================================

This is not a regular bi-monthly nomikai meeting, but
an extraordinary get-together on the occasion of the
LinuxWorld/Expo Tokyo 2001. We have invited several
special guests who are in Japan for this event:

	Rob "CmdrTaco" Malda	slashdot
	Scott McNeil		FSG, VA Linux
	Bruce Perens		evangelist

Date:     June 1st, 2001 (Friday)

Time:     19:00

Place:    Amataro Shinbashi-ten
           Minato-ku, Shinbashi 3-23-1
           STEC 2nd Bldg., B1/B2 F
           phone: 03-3431-5621
           http://www.colowide.co.jp/tempo/amataro/60.html

Event:    drink and eat (primarily a social activity)

Costs:    will be divided up among participants
           (approximately 4,000 yen/person)

This event is open to all Linux users and friends, and is
to promote casual Linux (and non-Linux) related discussions
over a few cold ones. Even if you're not a Linux user or a
LUG member, but want to hang out with Linux people, please come.

If you're coming, send an e-mail to tlug-admin@example.com
just to let us know so that we can reserve space at Amataro. If you
want to come at the last minute and don't send an e-mail, it's
okay - we may have to squeeze a bit, but please come!

If you get lost, or simply want to get in touch with us, please call:

    Alberto Tomita......070-5644-3680 (PHS)

For more information, access the TLUG web page at http://www.tlug.gr.jp

See you there!
