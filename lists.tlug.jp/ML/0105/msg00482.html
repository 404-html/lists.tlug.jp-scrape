I already mentioned this event to some of you, but here are the details:

After the LinuxWorld/Expo Tokyo 2001 (to be held from 5/30 to 6/1 at Tokyo
Big Sight http://wwww.idg.co.jp/expo/lw), IBM-J TEC-J(*) will sponsor a
seminar on Linux Internationalization focused on i18n of glibc, with a talk
to be delivered by Ulrich Drepper, the current glibc mantainer.

(*) TEC-J = Technical Expert Council-Japan
     a cross-divisional organization composed by technical leaders from
     R&D, business, SE and services departments of IBM Japan

This seminar will be open to the public (pre-registration required) and
everyone is welcome to participate.

    Date: June 5 (Tue)
    Time: 14:30 - 17:00
    Place: IBM Japan
           Yamato Laboratories Auditorium (*)
           (Bldg. A, 1st floor)
           http://www.ibm.com/jp/ibm/map/yamato.html

    Speaker:
         Mr. Ulrich Drepper
         glibc maintainer
         topic: current and future developments in glibc internationalization

         Mr. Adrian Havill
         Director, Japanese Engineering, Red Hat
         topic: newest features of RedHat 7.1

    Pre-registration:
         e-mail <kamata@example.com> by June 1st with the following 
information

         name
         affiliation

(*) entering the main gate, bldg. A will be at your right. Proceed to the
     reception at the 1st floor and once your participation at the seminar is
     confirmed, you will receive a visitor's badge (please wear it all the time
     while in the facilities)

(**) no video/photo or audio recordings will be permited
