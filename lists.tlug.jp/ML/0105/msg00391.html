Japanese does not work "out of the box" on Linux, any distro.  You
need a good admin hanging about to keep the natural behavior of even
users who can and do RTFM from breaking it (eg by installing a new and
interesting Japanized package).  You need an I18N guru hanging about
to have any hope of getting your new app to work reliably even on the
(nonexistent) standard-conformant systems, let alone the hodge-podges
that you can actually download from the 'net.

This thread is about why that is and what we OSS developers should do
about it.
