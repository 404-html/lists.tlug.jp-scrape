Just out of curiosity, why would you want to use MMS or SMS in Japan?
I have to yet meet anyone here using it.
Everybody uses MMS. When you send an "email" from (many) keiteis it is sent over MMS, and if it is to another keitei then it is delivered over MMS.
The MMS transport will use SMS to send a short notification to the recipient phone indicating that a message should be fetched. The phone will then fetch the message (over MMS/HTTP).
The problem with Softbank is that they refuse to let you use MMS over HTTP unless you set your HTTP User-Agent to one they explicitly allow.
