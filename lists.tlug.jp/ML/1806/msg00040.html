And that's the problem (BTW, face this all the time). If you set "RAID" at the BIOS, that's "hardware RAID" board taking over, and that means (almost certainly) a Linux software driver for the distro mandatory. I'm already hearing somebody will come up here saying you don't need hardware RAID and I can tell you, that's just a lie if you're in for the best your hardware can do (traditional Linux world excuses).
