For what it's worth,

3.3 is out.  High points of the release are:

* Support for Matrox Mystique
* Support for all current S3 chipsets
* Support for high and true color on many ET4000/W32 cards
* Improved speed for many of the drivers, using XAA
* All known Xlib/Xt vulnerabilities are closed
* glibc-2.0 binaries are available

It should have filtered through to most of the mirrors by now.

--	Chris (wileyc@example.com)
