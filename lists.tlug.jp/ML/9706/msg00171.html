Re: 8 bit versus 7 bit encoding
日本語テストです。

For Japanese, I've been sending messages with Content Type of 
        Text/Plain; charset=iso-2022-jp with
        Content Transfer Encoding set to 7 bit.

The Mew documentation says that iso-2022-jp should use 7 bit
Content Transfer Encoding.  However, I've noticed that my
Japanese gets mangled.  I'm sending this one as 8 bit, to see what
will happen.   I've heard two different opinions regarding
iso-2022-jp and Transfer-Encoding.  One side says that Japanese
should always be transmitted at 8 bit.  The other side says
that iso-2022-jp should be transmitted as 7 bit.  What is the
correct Transfer-Encoding for iso-2022-jp?
