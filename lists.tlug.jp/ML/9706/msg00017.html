Hello.  There is another window maker that people may want to try.  
>From the README:

-----

WindowMaker is a window manager designed to emulate the look
and feel of part of the NEXTSTEP(tm) GUI. It's supposed to be fast,
relatively small, feature rich and easy to configure, with
a simple and elegant appearance borrowed from NEXTSTEP(tm).

Memory usage is apparently smaller than fvwm+equivalent 
modules (FvwmButtons, FvwmWinList etc.).

-----

Its still in development, but its very stable.

Its packed with features, unfortunately, many aren't well documented
yet. 
It has full I18N support built-in.  Coming soon in a few weeks,
multilingual warning messages and dialog boxes. (Initially Japanese,
Portuguese, and English)     


Some nice features:
-------------------

- On-the-fly, menu setup.
- Pinnable menus with tearable sub-menus.
- A real NeXT-style dock, that is fully drag and drop 
  configurable.
- Shade (less buggy than AfterStep)


Its still missing some features.  Its not fvwm-derived so everything
must be written from scratch.  


Web pages:
----------

http://www.helix.net/~tannoy/wmaker/
http://home.abac.com/jfr/windowmaker/ 
http://www.inf.ufrgs.br/~kojima/wmaker/index.html (Author's homepage)
http://mushi.colo.neosoft.com/wmaker/ (mirror of above)
