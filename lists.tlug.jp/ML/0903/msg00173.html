I've decided to organize a Barcamp for May. If you'd like to help please email me off-list. 

The details are at http://barcamp.org/BarCamp-Tokyo2009
The Twitter feed is http://twitter.com/tokyobarcamp

If you're not sure what a Barcamp is then the wikipedia page is a good place to start: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barcamp Basically it's a 1-2 day unconference of workshops, discussions and talks. Everyone is encouraged to participate in some way. Topics are usually related to Linux, FOSS, web technologies, social networking and programming. For some reason barcamp often seems to be dominated by ruby-on-rails...

If it doesn't sound like your kind of thing, but you someone who may be interested, please forward this email to them.

Thanks in advance,
