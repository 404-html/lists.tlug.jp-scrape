> In any case, how would I go about diagnosing what is throttling the
> network connection?

You could try "netstat -s"

Lots of stats, but look for high numbers for lines mentioning
"discarded", "failed", "errors", etc.  (I find the output most useful
when comparing two machines, just one of which is misbehaving.)
