On 26/01/11 10:46, Stefan A. wrote:

Does someone know a place that lets you try stuff for compatibility
before buying? Or could someone recommend a headset model that is
available in Japan and is known to work without too much hacking? Any

My first post, but probably useless. :-) I don't know the answer to your questions...but something perhaps obvious which I'll say anyway...
My suggestion is to buy an international brand. Something that is also sold overseas. And then Google for people trying to get it working on Ubuntu in an English speaking country.
Even if they released model 123 in the USA but 124 in Japan in about the same year, you're probably ok. On the other hand, if one is 123E and the other is 123J, your confidence is probably even higher.
Alternatively, places like Amazon USA can sometimes ship to Japan. Or if Amazon USA does not, Amazon UK [or some other Amazon] might...
