Not sure if it's a case of gawd protecting the children and newbies, but I've been in similar places in Ubuntu a couple of times, usually with very little idea of how I'd gotten there... My fuzzy understanding is that this utility program is actually a lower level of apt-get, but my personal goal is to stay as much at the high levels as possible.
