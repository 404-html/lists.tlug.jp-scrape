Since I received more than one question about Plan 9 from folks at the
last TLUG tech meeting, I want to extend an invitation to all those
interested to attend the next TIP9UG's next meeting:

Date: Sunday, February 4th 2007
Agenda: LP49: Embedded system OS based on L4 and Plan 9.
Time: 1300 to 1700
Global Scientific Information and Computing Center,
Tokyo Institute of Technology, O-okayama campus.
http://www.gsic.titech.ac.jp/English/Access/Campus/index.html
