First post here, hello everybody!

I think that if you don't mind playing with Python, you should have a look at PIL (http://effbot.org/zone/pil-index.htm )
It is a very simple but very nice way to create pictures programmatically. There is a recent addition of support for the AGG library.
If you check AGG homepage ( http://www.antigrain.com/screenshots/index.html) you will see that most of what you want is well supported.
