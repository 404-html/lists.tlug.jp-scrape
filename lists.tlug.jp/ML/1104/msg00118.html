I may have finally hit on one small clue.
I was working at my office machine this evening, wrapping up about 21:15 (4/28), at which time I rsynced to the campus server. When I got home about an hour later and rsynced down to my home system (4/28-22:15), I checked the files that I had most recently saved, and they were dated 5:16 am, 4/29 (seen in the bottom two items at http://www.acmuller.net/download/dolphin3.png).
I guess this means that at the very least I have to double check the clock settings on my system at the office next time I go there (but probably after GW), but I suppose I still have to check how things work in the other direction. But it still doesn't explain why Rsync copies every single file half the time.
