P.S. (Getting off-topic, into web design)
But it seems this design is fragile anyway: each divider is a separate
image, using a slightly different background gradient and they are out
of sync too (try playing with zoom, set to "zoom text only"; my guess is
you'll see this in any firefox).

Not all the designs are so sensitive to zoom or min-font-size, but they
still seem to be affected. E.g. the default design, 001, I can get to
break when I zoom (zoom-text-only) far enough that single words no
longer fit in their allocated column space. But even without
zoom-text-only, zooming too far hides the first three paragraphs under
the right column (this can be seen in both firefox and chromium).
