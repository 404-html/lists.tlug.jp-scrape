I think the difference that the original poster was referring to is the 
ordering of the search results, which is definitely affected by the "hl" GET 
parameter of the search.  Since the query was Japanese anyway in the case in 
question, the difference is much less pronounced, but the "hl" parameter has 
significant effects on non-Japanese queries.  Here is an illustrative 
example:

English search:
http://www.google.co.jp/search?hl=en&q=karaoke

Japanese search:
http://www.google.co.jp/search?hl=ja&q=karaoke
