"http://aoclife.ddo.jp/aoc/recs/";

/aoc/recs/TheLegionClan_AoC_Pack1_1582_Games.zip

"http://aoclife.ddo.jp/aoc/recs/";

"http://aoclife.ddo.jp/aoc/recs/";

"http://aoclife.ddo.jp/aoc/recs/";

Anyone has any ideas how to hunt that site down?
