On Monday 28 January 2008 11:39:58 Dave M G wrote:
> So far as I can tell, it sees the SD card reader hardware (I'm guessing
> this is the SD card reader, because of the words "Card" and "SD"
> embedded in there):
>
> $ lspci
> CardBus bridge: Ricoh Co Ltd RL5c475 (rev 88)
> System Peripheral: Ricoh Ltd RL5c475 SD Bus Host Adapter

The results of a quick search on Google for "RL5c475" seem to indicate that 
said hardware is the PCMCIA controller, not the SD card reader.

The author of a "linux on cf-y2" page did not get the SD card reader to work:
http://www.mfraenz.de/cf_y2/linuxoncf-y2.html

The CF-Y5 SD card reader seems to work, however, with kernel 2.6.20 and later, 
according to the following websites:
http://www.cs.unm.edu/~moret/CFY5.html
http://davesource.com/Solutions/Linux-on-Panasonic-Y5.html

I don't know how different that model is from yours, but notes on the Y5 may 
give you clues of how to approach the problem on the Y2 (which has fewer 
Linux resources online).

Cheers,

Travis
