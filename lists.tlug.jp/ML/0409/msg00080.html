There will be a Tokyo Inferno/Plan 9 Users Group meeting on 16 October 2004.
The meeting will be held from 1300 to 1600 in the Global Scientific
Information and Computing Center, located at the Tokyo Institute of
Technology, O-okayama campus. Everyone is welcome, and there is no fee
to attend.

The "Global Scientific Information and Computing Center" is labeled as
number 4 on the following map:
http://www.titech.ac.jp/access-and-campusmap/e/o-okayamaO.html

The following is a railway map:
http://www.titech.ac.jp/access-and-campusmap/e/access.html

Topics for the forum will include election of Tokyo Inferno/Plan 9 Users
Group officials and creation of a Plan 9 group project(s) and
volunteers.

I hope to see you there,
