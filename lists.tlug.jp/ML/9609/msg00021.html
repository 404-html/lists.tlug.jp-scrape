This came up recently on the Red Hat Rembrandt list and the consensus
was the comment in the /etc/hosts file about not aliasing your machine

Happens automagically with pppd or diald when the link changes state.

For those that have not discovered it, diald is quite cool.  It will
do dialing on demand, drop the line after a time-out, force the line
up or down based on time of day, etc.  Definitely worth a look if
you haven't checked it out.  http://www.cs.toronto.edu/~schenk/diald.html
