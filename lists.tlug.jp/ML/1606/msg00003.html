      TLUG - Tokyo Linux Users Group July "nomikai"
      =============================================
     
      Date:     July 7, 2016 (Thursday)
      Time:     19:00-21:00

      Location: Mori no Beer Garden
                http://mbg.rkfs.co.jp/

      Address:  14-13 Kirigaoka-machi, Shinjuku-ku, Tokyo 
                http://mbg.rkfs.co.jp/#location
 
      Cost:     men: 4,200 yen / women: 3,900 yen 
                (purchase your ticket at the entrance)
                all-you-can-eat BBQ and all-you-can-drink 

      ***** IMPORTANT *****
      Beer gardens do not give much flexibility in seating, and they ask us
      to tell them EXACTLY how many people will attend. Please register your
      participation with Doorkeeper <http://tlug.doorkeeper.jp/>

      This event is open to all Linux users and friends, and is
      to promote casual Linux (and non-Linux) related discussions
      over a few cold ones. Even if you're not a Linux user or a
      LUG member, but want to hang out with Linux people, please come.
