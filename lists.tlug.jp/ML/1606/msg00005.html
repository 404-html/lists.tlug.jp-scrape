In my experience, most of the free Wifi services are rather inconvenient. Often it takes several minutes to go through the sign on process. In quite a few places they limit the time you can use it, like 20 or 30 minutes per day. In some cases they block services and protocols you may want to use.

However, you can buy a prepaid SIM card at Narita airport. Those are specifically made for visitors with a fixed validity period. If I remember correctly they are about 3500 yen for a month. If you don't have a smart phone you can probably buy one on eBay second hand for much less than what you would spend on rental.

You can also buy a second hand smart phone at various shops specialising in second hand phones in Akihabara. If you don't insist on the latest version of the most fashionable product with tons of storage, then you can get something a little older but still quite usable for a very reasonable price.
