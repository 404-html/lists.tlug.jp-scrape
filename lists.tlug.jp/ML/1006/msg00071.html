The way I use CF card (via a card reader) and other FAT32-based
storage device is (quite old-fashioned):

0. Open a terminal
1. Disable automount
2. Plug the device
3. mount it
4. Copy/edit etc over to device
5. unmount (it would take a while depends on the amount of data to be written)

This way, I am able to control what I am doing and what if something goes wrong.
Never had a single problem so far.
