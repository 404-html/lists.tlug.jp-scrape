Well, there is no "we are working on" plattform. 
<advertisment>
Our company is specialized on the difficult stuff. Ie our customers
usualy come to us after failing to find anyone else who'd work
on that project, or after trying three other engineering compnanies
who all failed on that project (i'm not exagerating!). Hence the
kind of projects we do is very broad, starting from EEG recording
devices that fit on the head of a mouse, over analog mesurement
amplifiers with programmable filters and amplification over multiple
decades to medical implants with wireless power supplies.
</advertisment>

Thus the type of uC we use is also quite different, depending on
the project we are on. Currently it's about 50% ARM7TDMI (SAM7 family
from Atmel), 20-30% MSP430 and various PIC, a few 8bit uC and also
some full fledged x86 based systems. There are also the first projects
where we use a Cortex-M3 uC, but those still are not bugfree enough
for general use.
