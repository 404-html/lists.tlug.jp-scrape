I do errands here for people.  Usually it involves making arrangements for concert tickets, buying only-in-Japan goods, acting as a forwarding service... you get the idea.  People reimburse my costs and usually tack on the price of a Starbucks frappucino, and they do so by sending me yen using Paypal.  No one's ever been charged anything (well, outside of exchange fees/rates...)

I also use Paypal to receive payments from people for voiceovers I've done for them.  If they pay with a checking account or Paypal balance it's not charged anything.  When they want to pay with a credit card I usually request they tack on an extra amount (I calculate it beforehand for them) to cover Paypal's fees.

Is that what you were looking for?
