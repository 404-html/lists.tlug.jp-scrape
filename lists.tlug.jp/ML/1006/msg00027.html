On 06/03/2010 07:04 PM, Ian Wells wrote:
Can you generally get shell access on Android machines or are they locked down by default?
Shell access is available via local app or USB using ADB but access is limited to non-root privileges. Rooting is possible for most devices but the procedure differs for each device and Android version. Also, upgrading to new official ROMs will remove privileges so wait to upgrade until rooting procedures for the new ROM is known.
