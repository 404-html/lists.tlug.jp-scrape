On 4/22/05, Kenneth <emry@example.com> wrote:

> Does the group have a forum, or social mailing list?

Not as such, but this list can get quite social at times. Since one
important aspect of a LUG (IMO, of course, but as the Listmaster, my
opinion matters at least somewhat) is having a good time with your
fellow geeks, "social" posts are generally considered on-topic for the
list.

The general rule of thumb is that since people can easily ignore  a
thread, as long as you do not break threads or, even worse, hijack
them, you should be OK. At worst, you might be asked to take the
thread off-list.
