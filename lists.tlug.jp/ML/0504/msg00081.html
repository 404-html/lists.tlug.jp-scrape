I'm here in Tokyo, but I didn't get over to that area this year.  I did 
see the flowers in some other areas, but they pale in comparison to how 
it was by the moat I see!  Thanks for posting those - I almost feel like 
I was offered a time machine trip back a few days.  Now the rains have 
come, it's pretty much over until next year.  I don't think the timing 
this year could have been more perfect.  Thursday, Friday, Saturday & 
Sunday - and I don't think I've ever seen the trees in better 
condition!  The cherry blossom trees of 2005 will be something to remember!

as the flowers were beginning to come out and took this picture a little 
past 11:00 p.m.:
