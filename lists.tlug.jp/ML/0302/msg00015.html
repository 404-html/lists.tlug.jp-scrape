even though red-hat is arguably not a nice distribution, I don't think you 
need to change distribution to enable streaming audio.  realplayer should be 
a short download.

If you want to try out suse, you can download a trial cd-rom from their 
server.  This is however a live-system, and so not really very usefull and 
just gives you a glimpse.

You can also download a basic install disk, and then do an ftp install.  It 
will take some time to get started using this method, but you get the full 
product without the cost.

Lastly, I think buying the boxed distro is a good idea for starters like you.  
Besides 7 CD-ROMS with most packages you ever need, you also get a bootable 
DVD with the same content, as well as two pretty good manuals (one for 
administration and one for the user side).
