> recovering with LILO I put in the recovery CD/Floppy and get to use the

Watch it, your linux prejudice is showing. So you want them to stick to
"standard" names? GRUB loads a lot of OSes so which standard names did
you have in mind:

solaris: /dev/dsk/c0t0d0s0
netbsd:  /dev/wd0a
dos:		c:
WinNT:  multi(0)disk(0)rdisk(0)partition(1)

If you ask me, Grub is doing a good job of being agnostic in a sane
fashion. It ain't a linux world and while lilo works Grub is definitely
the more advanced, flexible boot loader.
