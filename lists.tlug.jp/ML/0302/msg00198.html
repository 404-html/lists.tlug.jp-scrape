This page contains info on how the get DNS based spam blocking going:
http://www.ordb.org/faq/#usage

It works quite nicly for me (exim). Spamhaus is also DNS based and just as
easy to set up. Having not set Spamassassin up, I dont know about it, but
people I know how haved used it say its good, just not for large quantites
of mail (resource intensive).

headers/body and drop it depending on that as well.
