> > ReiserFS is actually a great choice for a root partition, as it is great

> I agree. All the partitions on my main system are XFS except for / and
> /usr (/usr/local is XFS) since both of their average file sizes are way

In my experience, ext3 is better than XFS in the small files department, and
better than Reiser in the large. But not by much.

I use ext3 because it is in stock kernels, and because I know a good bit
about ext2, so when the shit hits the fan, I am slightly less helpless than
I would be with Reiser or XFS.
