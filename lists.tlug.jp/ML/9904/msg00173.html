On Tue, 20 Apr 1999, Jyorudi wrote:

> I am an exchange student, and I just arrived to Japan. Here at home, they have a
> ISDN external adapter, that says NTT ISDN, INS V-8DSU. I've got no idea how to set
> it up. Can anyone help me with that?

If it helps, this is probably an NEC Aterm with an NTT label on it.  Is
it about 12 to 15 cm high and beige colored, with 3 analog RJ-11 jacks
on the back?  If so, any NEC instructions you can find should work.

The other good news is that you may not need to do anything to it.
Set up your dial-up connection and give it an init string (I can give
you an NEC init string, but I don't have it here), and try it.
Especially if this TA has already been in use, you probably won't have
to do anything to it.

Good luck,
