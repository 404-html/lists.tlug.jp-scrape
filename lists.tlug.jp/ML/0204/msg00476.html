Yes, the latest Klez is pretty crafty. It has quite a few variants,
does it's little header trick, etc.  The day I live in fear of is
if some spammerware writers and some virus writers get together
and collaborate on a worm that spreads itself and hibernates waiting

such an ISP nightmare scenario.  In fact, I suspect the only
reason it hasn't already happened is that spamware writers and
virus writers come from two very different camps, which probably
despise each other.
