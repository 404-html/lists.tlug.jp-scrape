How are membership fees used?
Primarily to fund the development of the Mandrake Linux distribution.
 It will also pay the salaries of employees who are dedicated to "external"
Free Software projects such as the Linux kernel, KDE, GNOME, Prelude,
and others.
Be used for the development of community web sites such as Mandrake
Linux.com, MandrakeUser.org, MandrakeForum.com and the development
 of Internet services specifically for the benefit of Club members.

(yearly payment):
 Standard Member ($5 month/$60 year)
Silver Member ($10 month/$120 year)
Gold Member ($50 month/$600 year)
Platinum Member ($100 month/$1200 year)
 [Note: Euro currency is provided to Euro zone citizens during payment
process.
