Ximian Setup Tools doesn't look too bad. Have a read of the
blurb at

	http://www.ximian.com/devzone/projects/xst-devel.html

and also some of the latest screen shots at

	http://primates.ximian.com/~chema/xst/

Click on the shares icon for the section on managing fstabs (called imports)

Haven't used it myself, but it does look pretty decent and easy to
use as well as easy to revert changes. I used linuxconf once when
I first installed linux and broke networking. Never touched it since.

Tom.
