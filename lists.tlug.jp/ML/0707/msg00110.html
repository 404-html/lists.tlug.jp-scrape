On 7/3/07, Michal Hajek <hajek1@example.com> wrote: 

Hello,

a simple question:

how do I cope in bash with filenames containing "-" character (i.e.
dash) ?

In my case, I have downloaded this file:
-umai-Doremi--Kenichi-10--B36CAB67-.avi.part1.rar


If you man rm, you will find the answer:

rm -- -filename
or
rm ./-filename

#man rm
#info coreutils rm

HTH
