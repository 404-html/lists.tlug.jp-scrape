On 03/07/07, Chris BALUTA <baluta@example.com> wrote:

find . -name "*Doremi--Kenichi*" -exec mv {} k1.rar \;
[...]
The {} and the \; are both absolutely needed. 

It is probably safer to quote or otherwise escape the {}, e.g.

find . -name "*Doremi--Kenichi*" -exec mv '{}'  k1.rar \;

or

find . -name "*Doremi--Kenichi*" -exec mv \{\}  k1.rar \;
