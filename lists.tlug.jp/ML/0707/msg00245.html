Corey Doctorow's "When Sysadmins Ruled the Earth" is freely available
on the 'Net:

http://baens-universe.com/articles/When_Sysadmins_Ruled_the_Earth

It is a fantastic story, in both senses of the word. A bit of a
downer, though, and I would recommend that new fathers steer clear for
six months or so.

It is the most realistic writing about What We Hackers Do since
"Stealing the Network: How to Own a Continent":

http://www.amazon.com/Stealing-Network-How-Own-Continent/dp/1931836051/
