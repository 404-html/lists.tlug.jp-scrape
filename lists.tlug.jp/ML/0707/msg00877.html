Thanks for mentioning that.  There's another issue involved though.
Editing.  With analogue tape, every time you edit, you lose quality.

cameras I used produced good-looking tapes that cannot afford to fall
much.  Analogue editing produced mediocre results.  I'm hoping to get
that stuff digitized and onto external hard drives, and then do
something with the digital version on HDD and from there make DVD's
not as a backup, but as a finished product.
