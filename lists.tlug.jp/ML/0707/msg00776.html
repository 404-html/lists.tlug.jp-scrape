TLUG,

I got an off-list suggestion that works great:

> If that's the case, make sure you have xpdf-utils installed, and try:
> pdfimages -f 1 -l 100 mydoc.pdf img

This command enabled me to extract the image as a ppm file, which opens in GIMP. From there I could manipulate the image and save into any format I wanted.

Thank you to everyone who made suggestions and provided advice.
