Also, you could try the following command (as root) and see if you are
presented with a list of locales to choose from. If you are then
choose anything with a "JP" in it and what else grabs your fancy:

ja_JP.EUC-JP EUC-JP

but ignored a whole bunch of other pre-selects that I can't see a need for in this lifetime only to keep things as close to original as possible for a few more minutes.

I then ran

locale-gen

When it asked me which I wanted to be default, I selected ja_JP.UTF-8 UTF-8 but do not know what problems I may now have created for myself.
