Charles Muller writes:

 > problem. One question, though. When you wrote this line
 > 
 >  > export XMODIFIERS='@im=SCIM"
 > 
 > you probably mis-typed and used single quotes on one side and double on 
 > the other. Obviously it has to be matched, but does it matter which one 
 > I use?

No, since the content is intended to be a literal and contains no
active characters (like $ or `).  In fact, you don't actually need
quotes at all, I think.  (The "=" looks weird but what happens is that
the first one in a left-to-right scan is taken as the assignment
operator.)
