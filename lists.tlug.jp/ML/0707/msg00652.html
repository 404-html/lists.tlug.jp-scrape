  • Date: Sun, 15 Jul 2007 14:25:27 +0900

  • Subject: [tlug] Re: Open Office Kanji problem
steven smith <sjs@example.com> wrote:

I just opened Open Office for the first time.  It doesn't
seem to handle Kanji.   I used Koichi Mincho font and in a
straight text file this works fine.   This is in Open Office
2.2  as installed with the Ubuntu 7.04 release right out of
t>he box.A I get some nice little boxes but no text.
Cut/past from Firefox behaves the same. 

Sounds like the classic setup issue: AFAIK you need to go to the
Language Settings (Tools/Options) in OO general language options, and
enable Asian languages and choose Japanese (if you want a default as
well). Then restart OO, and go to OO Writer options where you should
now have a new option called "Asian fonts (basic)", and choose some
Asian fonts there.
