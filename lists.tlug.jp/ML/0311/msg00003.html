> I would be very happy if I could hear from anyone who has
> a significant amount of experience with both Red Hat and Debian who
> might be able to outline some of the differences in terms of demand in
> technical skills for handling the two systems.

With Debian you will probably be happier with the software 
updating system and "free" atmosphere, whether you stay with 
the stable branch or move to "unstable." In particular, I 
think you will prefer debs to rpms. With RH you will 
probably be happier with the tools that detect/recognize 
the hardware. Might want to keep a RH CD around, just for 
that purpose. 

And don't forget the other flavors.
