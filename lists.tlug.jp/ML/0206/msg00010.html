On Fri, May 31, 2002 at 06:54:34PM +0900, Nguyen Hung Vu wrote:
> Anyone knows how to hide welcome message in wu-ftp when an user log in?

Check the "Informational Capabilities" section of 'man ftpaccess'.

You may want to set 'greeting terse' to control the amount of
information displayed before login, to eliminate the 'banner'
command, and to change the 'message' command to change what
happens on the 'LOGIN' trigger.
