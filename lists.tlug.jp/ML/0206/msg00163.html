On Fri, Jun 07, 2002 at 12:05:21PM +1000, Jim Breen wrote:
> 
> A Mozilla question about printing Japanese text. Mozilla happily
> shows the text on screen, displays it right in print preview, but seems
> only to send the code-points and font details to the (Postscript)
> printer. Since few printers in this land actually have Japanese fonts 
> installed,  the results are not very useful.

You should have a look at your print magicfilter and find out where and
how Ghostscript is invoked (the details depend heavily on the print
spooler you use). Then append a '-I' option specifying the Japanese
fonts path.

Cheers,
