I was installing NetBSD on my server at home which has
a hpt370 raid controller on the mobo. The installer failed
to find the two disks in raid, which i had set up in the 
bios?. It just saw them as two separate disks. Well, this 
caused me to search the mail archives, and low and behold
I found out that this in fact software raid not hardware
raid. I was realy shocked. I mean it seems hard to find
a mobo without ide raid. I feel it's very misleading by
mobo manufacturers. I don't know much about raid controllers
so I hope I'm not making a mistake here...

Has anyone else found this to be true?
