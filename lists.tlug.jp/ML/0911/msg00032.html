Yes, it's always good to check the supported hardware list closely before buying if your goal is to run one of these firmwares.
http://www.dd-wrt.com/wiki/index.php/Supported_Devices

Some devices, like the Linksys WRT line, have gone through hardware revisions some of which are not capable of running the full DD-WRT and the revision numbers aren't always printed on the box.
