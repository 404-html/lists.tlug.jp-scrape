The November meeting of the Tokyo Society for the Application of
Currying, a group for people interested in functional programming,
will be held at the usual location at 7:30 p.m. on Thursday, November
26th, 2007. This will be the last meeting of the year (unless anybody
is *really* interested in meeting on Christmas or New Year's eve :-));
meetings will resume again in January.

Full information, including a map, is available here:

  http://www.starling-software.com/en/tsac.html

There's currently no set topic, so if anybody has anything on which
they'd like to speak (even if it's trivial), please let me know.

Also, if there are TLUG members who saw Travis' presentation on Haskell
at the last TLUG technical meeting and have questions or would like to
discuss things further, please feel free to come by.
