   securities company has two vacancies for Unix system administrators.

   do a certain amount of menial work (installs, moves, etc) while
   learning.

   depends heavily upon technology for trading.  =8-O

   female, black, pink, white or a combination on all five).

   Fluency in both Japanese and English is preferred, but not
   essential. Pay and conditions are good. Honestly.

   The perks include first choice of Linux-capable hardware as it
   is pushed out of our inventory system. :-)  :-)
