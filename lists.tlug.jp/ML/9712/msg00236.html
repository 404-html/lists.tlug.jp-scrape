 Searching for volunteer to be Kanji-san
 ---------------------------------------
Responsibilities
  o  get rough head count by saying on list, "Hey, are you coming?"
     a few times
  o  reserve place at Tengu.  Say, "Big table, big table," a few
     times
  o  stand up at meeting and say, "hey, thanks for coming, and now
     a few words from John Little who will express his thanks."
     John will say, "Douzo, nonde kudasai," and begin some chant.
  o  get bill, bring out your Linux kernel based calculator and
     divide bill by how much it is.  If it is an odd number, we
     usually round up and donate the change (like 100 yen or something)
     to TLUG.  Ideally, the calculator would be written in assembler
     code before the meeting and automatically calculate the number of 
     people at the meeting by the difference in air temperature between
     ambient and human body temperature.(joke about the assembler code).
  o  at the end of the night, say, "man, this kanji-san work is hard,
     who wants to buy me a drink?"  

No preparation of an agenda or events is required.  I'll convince
someone else to do this.  The Kanji-san will get a place for the 
TLUGers, get there money, and send the lads and ladies out into 
the Tokyo night.
