If the Jan meeting is a nomikai, what is wrong with a "regular" Feb
meeting--or is it better for everyone to wait till March? Either month is
fine for Temple.

The walk to TUJ from Tamachi is just about 15 minutes, and I can provide a
much easier to follow map than the one on the TUJ web site!!  But meeting
as a group there and taking people the first time is easy also.

Several busses stop almost in front of TUJ.  From Shibuya, the 06 bus takes
20 minutes to get to Furukawa Bashi, and is one block further at San no
Hashi--Instructions to cab drivers are just "San no Hashi" or "Minami Azabu
san no hashi."  There's a bus from Shimbashi, from Yotsuya and Tokyo
station.  If we have a meeting at TUJ, I'll post the numbers and where the
exact stops are.

Steve
