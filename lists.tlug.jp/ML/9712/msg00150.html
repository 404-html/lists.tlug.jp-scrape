On Mon, 15 Dec 1997, Alan B. Stone wrote:

> As I said earlier, I have just installed the RedHat Linux 5.0
> distribution.  I'm not happy with the sound configuration and want to
> remove all sound files.  However, when I do I continually get errors. 
> Can anyone tell me how to remove something from the kernel?  Thanks,

In /usr/src/linux ...

make menuconfig -- remove the sound support here
make dep
make clean
make zImage

... then configure lilo to boot the new kernel image.
