A friend of mine who uses to draw "manga" in his free hours gave the
following suggestions:

>  I would change the colors in Mt. Fuji.  Make it a little bit darker, and
> draw it more like a real mountain.  The penguin looks like he's sitting
on a > dung heap.  The base should be surrounded by lower mountains and
clouds to
> emphasize the size of Fuji. The Chinese did some great ink paintings with
> mountains, check some of those out.
> 
> The upper clouds should be more dimensional to suggest space.  Smaller
clouds > in a distant background.
> 
> The words "Tokyo", "Linux" and "Users' Group" sit too far away from each
> other.  I would have "Tokyo" and "Users' group" in the same font with
Users' > group written in Japanese.  Since "Linux" is a logo onto itself, I
would
>  leave it alone.
> 
> Since this is going on a T shirt, it should be more vertical than horizontal
>  to use more of the available space.  
> 
> The penguin is too "dead center".  Move him to one side, and on the other
>  side string "Tokyo Linux Users' Group" vertically.  Rotate Linux 90 degrees
>  to make it fit with the Japanese.
> 
> Choose the color of the text carefully to make sure it balances visually
> against the penguin and mountain.  The brown isn't working.
> 
> You might have the text start and end slightly outside the boundaries of the
>  drawing to make it more prominent.
