What are you using for your print driver?  

There are two ways to print from TeX, dvi.  One is to use a driver
that converts the dvi directly to something like PCL for HP printers.
The other is to convert the dvi to PostScript and then use something
like GhostScript to covert the Japanese fonts to something the printer
can understand.  

If you are using the dvi to hp driver, are you sure you have the
proper driver to match your printer?

If you're using PostScript, can you display it on your graphics
terminal using gs, gv, or ghostview?  If you can display it,
it is pretty easy to get it to print.  If you can't display it,
you have to back track to find the source of the problem.
