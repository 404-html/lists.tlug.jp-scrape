Hi,

There are no requirements to come.  There are no membership
fees.  No prior knowledge is required.  No money is required.
If you want to eat lunch, you must buy your own lunch,
probably under 1,500 yen.  Last one was 650 yen for lunch
I think.  The chinese restaurant will probably be more than
650 yen, but we don't anticipate heavy expenses.    

It does not matter if you feel that you know nothing about
linux.  No one will care.  

If you are shy and feel uncomfortable talking to people, no one
will care.  If you want to learn more about Linux, but feel that
the technology talk is too much, no one will notice.   If you
have hesitated about bringing a raw SCSI disk out at a party 
before or wanted to ramble on about your new computer, but no
one in your office would listen, now is your chance.    

On the other hand, if you are a Linux wizard, you may get a larger
size bumper sticker or an extra penguin sticker.

Fun for everyone.  Bring your friends.

Regards,
Craig
