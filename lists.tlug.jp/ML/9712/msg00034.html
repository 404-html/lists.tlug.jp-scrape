I think that's a little unlucky.  I've had my Pilot over a year and I carry
it with me all the time in my over-one-shoulder courier bag every day.  I
carry it with my when I travel, I carry it with me when I am pushed on to a
rushhour yamanote, I carry it with me in the rain and in the shine.   (Er,
"in the shine"?  :-)

I have one of the original ones that had this defect where a bit of
plastic breaks off easily (not a functionally important part), and that
finally happened to me this last summer.


I've had a friend or two who has ended up with a broken Pilot, but the 
vast majority of the people I know are happily using them.


BTW- current street price for the Pilot professional is like $350.
