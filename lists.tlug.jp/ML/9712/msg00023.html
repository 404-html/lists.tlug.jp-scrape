> a shot and see how it goes.  One thing that turns me on in

Oh, I should warn you.  Floppy disks often have bad sectors.
This is especially critical for the install diskettes.  
If you are having problems with the install, try another
diskette.  Don't underestimate the importance of having 
good data sectors on your install diskettes.
