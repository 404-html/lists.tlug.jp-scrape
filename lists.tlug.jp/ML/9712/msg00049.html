I was reading USENET and it appears that the Free-BSD team moved
JLUG out of the leaders list for the RC5 crack game.  If anyone
from TLUG is interested in crypto, they might want to make an announcement
at the next TLUG meeting or on the list to see if there is interest
forming a TLUG team to join the JLUG team in the crack efforts.

in participating, but can't read Japanese, I'm sure we can 
arrange a quick translation into English.
