Just an idea...

The TLUG community is a mixture of people from many areas and cultures.
People who speak English and Japanese natively are obviously present,
but I know of people here whose native languages are Dutch, French,
Spanish and (IIRC) German. There are probably representatives of other
languages too.

What do TPTB at TLUG think of starting a mailing list purely for
language issues? Having recently started to learn Japanese I'm sure I'd
have plenty of questions about that language. OTOH, I could help for
questions regarding English and French, and (to a lesser extent) German
and Dutch.

Is this feasable or have I just made everyone's whackjobometers go off
the scale?
