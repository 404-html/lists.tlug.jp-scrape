> It seems that since the kernel upgrade, my SD card
> reader is no longer 
> reading SD cards.

After you plug in the SD card, open a terminal and
post output of the following:

/bin/uname -a
/sbin/lsmod
/bin/dmesg | tail

Note, you can redirect output to a file:

/sbin/lsmod > lsmod.txt
