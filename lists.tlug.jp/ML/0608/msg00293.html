So, here is the announcement.

It's a bit of an experiment to see if it'll work, really, but there's
now a TLUG mailing list for discussing (spoken) languages. Many of us
here at TLUG are learning at least one foreign language and, although
there are plenty of learning "forums" (I know, a mailing list isn't a
forum strictly speaking, but you know what I mean) already around, I
thought it could be a neat idea to create one with what could be a more
relaxed environment in that its members already know each other.

Josh agreed to give the idea a spin, so here we are.

As things are right now I've recently started learning Japanese, so I'll
doubtless have a few questions to ask once/if the mailing list gains any
momentum. English being my mother tongue I can help there, and with
French (I'm bilingual). I can probably help with German and Dutch too,
to a lesser extent.

So, those who are interested can subscribe by pointing their browsers
here:

  http://www.tlug.jp/mailman/listinfo/lingo

or by mailing:

  mailto:lingo-request@example.comsubject=subscri??
