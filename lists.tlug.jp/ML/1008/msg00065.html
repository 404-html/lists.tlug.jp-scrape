      TLUG - Tokyo Linux Users Group August "nomikai"
       =======================================================

      Date:     August 20 (Friday)
      Time:     19:30-21:30

      Location: Nihonbashi Mitsukoshi Dept Store "honkan" Rooftop Beer Garden
                http://www.mitsukoshi.co.jp/store/1010/beergarden/

      Address:  Nihombashi Muro-machi 1-4-1 Chuo-ku, Tokyo
                http://www.mitsukoshi.co.jp/store/fcs/english/1010/map.html
 
      Cost:     men: 3,601 yen / women: 3,001 yen (purchase your ticket at the entrance)
                all-you-can-eat-drink 

      IMPORTANT: beer gardens do not give much flexibility in seating, and they ask us
                 to tell them exactly how many people will attend. Please register your
                 participation with Doorkeeper <http://tlug.doorkeeper.jp/>

      This event is open to all Linux users and friends, and is
      to promote casual Linux (and non-Linux) related discussions
      over a few cold ones. Even if you're not a Linux user or a
      LUG member, but want to hang out with Linux people, please come.

      Even if you want to come at the last minute it's okay - we
      may have to squeeze a bit, but please come!

      If you get lost,
      or simply want to get in touch with us, please call:

         Alberto Tomita..............080-3463-9256
         Edward Middleton............090-3689-0292 

      For more information, access the TLUG web page at
      http://www.tlug.jp

      See you there!
