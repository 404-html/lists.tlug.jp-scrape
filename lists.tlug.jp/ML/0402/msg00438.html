rackspace.com(***)
verio.net(***)
etc.

From a statistical standpoint, anything coming from the IP space of these
networks will be spam, and any e-mail referring to a website hosted on these
networks will be spam. Some of it will be legitimate, sure, but I still
don't want it. What I *do* want is for legit, above-board clients of these
rogue ISPs who use them as human shields to turn around and either light a
fire under the ISP so that it cleans its act up or to move to a clean
nethood.
