> another of the many sources.  The main one is still the CPU, and since this 
> didn't happen until after your upgrade, the CPU/mobo are your main culprits.  

Which includes the way the motherboard connects to the outside
world. There is a large variation in the quality of decoupling
of the external ports from one motherboard to another (even
within the same company).

Having helped get several computers through RFI testing, I'm of
the opinion that it is best to be methodical and try to
localize the source rather than using a shotgun approach.
At least for repeatability.  :-)
