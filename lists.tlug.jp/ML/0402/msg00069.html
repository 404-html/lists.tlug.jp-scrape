I'm not finished like Mauro.  I would be writing after I finish.  I
think the recipe in short would be like this:

* Order PVR-350 :-)
* Subscribe ivtv mailing list for the update info.
* Prepare your hardware.
  (VIA chipset is not recommended according to the mailing list.  I
  used i815 mobo for P-III.)
* Install Gentoo Linux on your hardware.  When you build your kernel,
* follow the instruction at:
  http://ivtv.writeme.ch/tiki-index.php?page=HowTo
  especially
  http://ivtv.writeme.ch/tiki-index.php?page=GentooHowTo
* Get ivtv and utilities from:
  http://ivtv.sf.net
* Follow the instructions in the tar ball.
* Follow the instructions at:
  http://ivtv.writeme.ch/tiki-index.php?page=TvOutHowto

I just got PVR-350 and followed GentooHowTo to install ivtv driver,
then I successfully got test MPEG files from the board.  I believe I
can get TV-Out if I continue.
