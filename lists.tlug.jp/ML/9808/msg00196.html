Kei Furuuchi wrote:
> So I am wondering two cpus are asigned with the resources via a
> separate bus?
My understanding is with two CPUs:
one takes a master position giving programs to the second CPU, 
  (so if your programs aren't written to take advantage of
multithreading, 
  they just run on the first CPU, giving absolutely no speedup)
each gets half of the interrupts to handle, 
and both share the same bus to all the rest of the computer.

So bus speed is still a major consideration, as is cache size on the
CPUs.
