On Fri, Aug 07, 1998 at 03:29:52PM +0900, Shimony Anat/Yuval wrote:
> I am trying to get my Linux 2.0.0 / 2.0.34 versions to
> read the following MO/SCSI combination.
> Logitec LMO-640E / Fujitsu M2513EL (Akihabara purchased)
> 
> Anyone knows if it is possible ?

Yes, but you need to patch the 2.0.x kernel to add support for the 2048 byte
sectors used on 640MB MO media.  (You can use 230MB and 540MB MO disks in
your drive without change, since they use 512 byte sectors.)

http://liniere.t.u-tokyo.ac.jp/2048.html

Jim

P.S. Alternatively, you can switch to any recent 2.1.x series kernel, since
they already have 2048 byte/sector support.
