> I have a question though, what does the:  "X-Authentication-Warning:
> petra.mb.infoweb.ne.jp: fwiw3980 owned process doing -bs" mean? 

Means that fwiw3980 is not considered to be a trusted user by sendmail.
Add that userid to either /etc/sendmail.ct (if it exists) or
/etc/sendmail.cf (as Tfwiw3980).
