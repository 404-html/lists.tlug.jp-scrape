One of the areas that I would like to see expand in the TLUG library is
Japanese-language resources (currently there are none).  There are some
excellent Linux reference books by Japanese authors, as well as a vast number
of translations, including much (or all?) of the O'Reilly UNIX titles.

To that end, I am interested in receiving suggestions for Japanese-language
books or magazines that you would like to see appear in the TLUG library.

Respond to the list or mail me privately, if you prefer.

Japanese text follows (hopefully not too katakoto  :-)    ).  If it's really a
mess, reposting by a native speaker is welcome :-)
===============
日本語版

現在、TLUGのライブラリーは日本語版のLinux本や雑誌はありませんが、TLUGのライブ
ラリーを日本語版のしたいと思っています。いろいろの日本語オリジナル本や雑誌、ま
た和訳された英語版の本も多い(O'Reilly出版社のUNIX本など)ので、皆さんのTLUGライ
ブラリーに出て欲しいおすすめする日本語版の本や雑誌を教えてくださいませんか。

TLUGのMLでも私宛のメールでもOKですので、お返信、御意見をお待ちしております。

宜しくお願いします。
======================

Cheers,

Jonathan
