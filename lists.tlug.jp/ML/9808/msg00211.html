Shigeo Honda <shige@example.com> wrote,

shige> After I added some MIME types to /etc/mime.types, I tried to restart
shige> Apache on TL2.0J from remote telnet as super user mode. I typed as in
shige> manual.
shige> 
shige>  kill 'cat /usr/local/etc/httpd/logs/httpd.pid'
shige> 
shige> then I got 'No such pid ....' message.
shige> 
shige> Where is http.pid on TL2.0J and how to stop and start again Apache? 
shige> Please let me know.

How about 

  /etc/rc.d/init/httpd stop

or in your case 

    /etc/rc.d/init/httpd restart

That is more elegant, I guess.

BTW, http.pid is in

  /var/run/httpd.pid

(You can easily find such files by doing a `locate <filename>'.)
