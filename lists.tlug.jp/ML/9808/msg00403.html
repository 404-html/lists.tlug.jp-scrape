the stock xanim doesn't display all the .avi codec formats correctly, btw.
The TL one does (no flames, please :) ), so you may want to try that, as
I've patched in support for the 'optional' .avi codecs.  Or just get the

also do sound in videos that support it.  QT (QuickTime) movies DO work,
btw.
