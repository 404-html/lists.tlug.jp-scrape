Subject: tlug: Recommendations for LCD Monitors?

> can anyone recommend a not-too-expensive LCD monitor in the
> 14-15" range?

I haven't used any [except those on demo in shops], or done any comparisons,
but last night I was in Media Valley (Machida) and happend to see the new
Apple Studio Display (15.1" diagonal viewable  - TFT display).   I was
surprised by the quality/brightness/colour of the image.    If only it was a
tad cheaper ......

Also the transucent blue might clash with other beige h/w ...... you'd need to 
buy a translucent kybd and mouse to match it ;)

> Would such a monitor coexist with my ELSA Winner 2000PRO/X?

It supports (amongst other modes):
1024 x 768 at 60Hz, 70Hz, 74.9Hz or 75Hz.    So I would imagine that it would
work.    Not sure if it has 15 pin D-sub to 15 pin mini D-sub converter
included.

As a bonus it has composite and s-video inputs [but they might only be NTSC]

http://www.apple.com/displays/studio/
