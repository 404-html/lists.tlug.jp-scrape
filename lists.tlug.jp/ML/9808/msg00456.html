All this talk of booting complete linux systems with one or two
floppies, interests the heck out of me.  You can find industrial single
board computers on the market that are cheap, tiny, contain lcd/video
drivers and 100BT ports, and 2 to 64 MB flashDisks all over the place.

IMHO, Linux microservers are destined to start popping up like mushrooms
after a rainstorm.  Cobalt (somebody just nominated davem as Linus's
fill-in if he gets run over, didn't they?) is probably just the first of
a big wave.
