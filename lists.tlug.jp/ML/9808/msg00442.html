> run it diskless and stuff it's disk into my TL box for a little more breathing

Not sure about Trinux, but you should take a look at the Linux Router
Project (http://www.psychosis.com/linux-router/) and Tom's Root/Boot
disk (http://www.clark.net/~toehser/rb).

beyond fabulous -- the mother of all rescue disks.  Tom's disk isn't
exactly configured as a firewall, but it wouldn't be to modify it
appropriately for your particular application.
