Hi,
I found what was causing the problem with acroread. For my
jap. Netscape to work with I installed kinput and put the following
lines in my .xsession file:

if [ -x /usr/X11R6/bin/kinput2 ] ; then
    /usr/X11R6/bin/kinput2 &
    XMODIFIERS="@example.com=kinput2"
    export XMODIFIERS
fi

This causes acroread to crash. To avoid that, you have to put a line
like:
export XMODIFIERS=
into the acroread batch file.

Easily fixed, just hard to find :).

Marcus
