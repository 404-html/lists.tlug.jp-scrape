> However, it is quite arguable that GPLv3 should be DFSG-unfree and not
> OSI-certifiable because it explicitly discriminates against a specific
> field of endeavor.  Namely, "making 'technical measures' protectable
> under the DMCA."
>
> I can't imagine either Debian or the OSI would decertify the GPLv3 for

non-free.  I remember similar issues when the GNU Free Documentation
