I recently worked on a project where we started off with a plan to use
the "loong" cables and a video splitter (no need for multiple video
cards if it is the same screen you are exporting) approach. However,
in the end for various reasons went with thin clients close to the
TVs. To do this though you need to ensure the thin clients can support
the resolution required.
