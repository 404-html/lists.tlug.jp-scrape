Is /dev/sdc1 the correct device?

After you plug in the keitai into USB, dmesg should ideally show a few lines where usb-storage gets loaded, recognizes the memory card, and assigns it a SCSI device name.  It's possible the SCSI device name may change each time you unplug and plug in the keitai.
