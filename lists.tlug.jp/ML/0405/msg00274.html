Godwin Stewart wrote:

> I do have both /dev/dsp and /dev/dsp1, but whichever one I use as an output

It _should_ be /dev/dsp1.
You can verify that by trying to use both /dev/dsp and /dev/dsp1, which
of course will only work if they are physically different channels,
since the card doesn't do hardware mixing.
