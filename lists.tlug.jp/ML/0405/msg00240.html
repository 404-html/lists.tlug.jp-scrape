I upgraded from SuSE 9.0 Professional to SuSE 9.1 Professional.  It all 
went smoothly enough, but if you do an upgrade, be prepared for as much 
as a two or three hour operation what the on-line update at the end.  
And...

For whatever reason, the sound gets lost, so you have to manually go in 
and set it up.  (This is mentioned in the release notes, so it's not a 
freak incident.)  The mouse way of getting sound back is as follows:

Control Center
  YaST2 modules
    Hardware
      Sound
        (root priv.)
           Quick automatic setup
