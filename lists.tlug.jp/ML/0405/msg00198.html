As have Nvidia cards. My experience is, that the ati drivers are still a bit
buggy and do not work with cards of all manufacurers...

quality of the video Signal isnt always the best (according to German c't
magazine)

So I would recommend buying some mid range product, perhaps a ATI 9600 card
(u can get them without active cooling) in 2 weeks (the new generation of
ati cards will be launched) for a discount price.
