Lyle,

	Something you might want to consider is the RHCE is available in
English in both Bangkok and Manila. Although the classes in Bangkok are
taught in Thai, I was told the RHCE could be taken in English. I believe in
the Philippines the instruction is in English. The RHCE costs $749 in
America, but last time I checked it was about $400 in Bangkok and $300-350
in Manila. The way I look at it, the difference between the cost in the US
opposed to these other locations could be applied towards a trip there. I'd
suspect the pricing in Japan is similar to the U.S.

	The following is the "Asia/Pacific" area training link (based in
Australia that can provide information regarding training.

http://www.apac.redhat.com/training/

Mike
