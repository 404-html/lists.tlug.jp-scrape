> You can also arrange for the charset to be determined by the extension

That would be
http://httpd.apache.org/docs-2.0/mod/mod_mime.html#addcharset

You probably want

AddCharset UTF-8       .utf8
AddCharset EUC-JP      .euc-jp
AddCharset shift_jis   .sjis
