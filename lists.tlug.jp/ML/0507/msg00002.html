I've recently rediscovered Linux Magazine <http://www.linux- magazine.com/>
and been favorably impressed.  It seems to cover a broader range of topics
than Linux Journal, including better surveys of "what's new."  An annual
subscription is US$110 airmailed to Japan, but you can sign up for a three
issue trial subscription for US
$15 <http://www.linux-magazine.com/Trial/> .  They also make older issues
freely available online as PDFs of individual articles.
