That's the general carrier strategy everywhere: only new phones get
the latest software version, so that people would buy more new phones.

features, it's a heck of a lot faster, too.

(mostly due to JIT support) it's no replacement for a faster CPU.
Use a newer (1GHz) phone for half an hour and you will never want
to go back to your 03.

Galaxy is pretty good actually, despite trying to look like an
iPhone. Unlike the stuff coming from the likes of Sharp.
