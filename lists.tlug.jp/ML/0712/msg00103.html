The T30 is about half again as thick as the T40. Neither is anywhere
near Libretto size.

Personally, if you want a very nice laptop that's quite
Linux/BSD-compatible (i.e., quite fast), I'd look around for a T41 or
T42 with the SXGA screen (1400 x 1050). Bump it up to a gig or 1.5 gig
of RAM and a 7200 RPM hard drive, and you've basically got a desktop
replacement. This T42 has been my day-to-day machine for development
and so on for a couple of years now. New it was about 160,000, with the
