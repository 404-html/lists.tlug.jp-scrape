I bought a 2605dn about a year and a half ago. It has been a great 
printer for personal use. The paper feeder jams often enough that i 
wouldn't use it as a shared printer in an office though. My apartment 
tends to get rather humid in the summer though, and that probably has 
something to do with the paper jams. I've never had a problem with the 
duplexer; i set it to always duplex in cups and have to specifically 
turn it off on the rare instances i actually want something simplex.
