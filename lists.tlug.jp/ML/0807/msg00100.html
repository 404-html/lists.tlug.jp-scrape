Curt Sampson wrote:

> On 2008-07-12 22:31 -0400 (Sat), jep200404 wrote:
> 
> > The fixed width I want is of characters, not pages. 
> > That's mainly so that columns of text line up. 
> 
> So why not just start the columns at specific positions on the page,
> and not worry about the width of individual characters? 

I value simplicity. 

The text is plain text for very mundane stuff. 
I don't want to make it any more complex than that. 

There certainly are many ways to line up columns, 
from hard tabs characters (with tab stops on the printer), 
to HTML to TeX, but all those ways have more complexity 
than I want. 

In a word: KISS
