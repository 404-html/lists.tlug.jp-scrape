  Anyway, today as I encountered an effective(?) infomation
regarding the interesting symposium from  "Information Processing
Society of Japan", I would like to post it.

  From 14th March  to 16th, there will be an annual conference of
the above society in TAKUSHOKU-Univ. in TOKYO, and  additionally,
there will also be the specail symposuim regarding Open-Source,
(however, mainly on Linux maybe).  The detail matter is below.
If you feel interesting or some intention, please pay your attention
on it.

1)Date  : 16th March (Thus.)  15:15-17:00
2)Place : Takushoku-Univ in HACHIOJI
               Room number: 101 Building D. (2nd main meeting place.)
3)Title   :  The possibility, limitation of Open-Source Programs.
               (What, How can we expect them as real business ?)

4)Outline: (From info in Japanese,)
  As the background of the recent expansion of Open-Software,
there are certain activities called "Open-Source Activities or
Intiatives(?)",
in which we have expected the appearance of the improved sitiation in
this domain, by disclosuring source codes, free to use them, make their
clones,  re-distribution through the Internet.
  Due to above, there is a new trand to generate or create new businesses
from above world,  they intend to make the established monopoly by
traditional enterprises by using intelligence properties be negative.
  (Hard to express in English "Out of date" seems to be suitable.)

  On the other hands, the major players in this industory have also been
recognazing this fact recently, accepting and trying to contacts with this
rich frontier, with some expectation as the door to their future.
  However, at same time, we also suspect that there seems to be many
remaining obstacles and issues to establish our expectation, in other
words, no border, hamonaizing between glassroot Open-Source world
and traditioanl, established business world.  Hence, we seem to need
so many days more & more.

  Therefore in this pannel discussin, the below topics will be picked up
to identify the remaining issues.
   1)Common matters and differences between "Open-Source world" &
      "Business world".
   2)What is an innovation in Linux business ?
       And What is a Trigger ?
   3)How should we make the relationship between community and
      companies ?
   4)The concrete instances to be unsuitable to using "Open-Sources".

Through the discussion on the above themes, we expect some
contributions to us, members of above society, and their research.
