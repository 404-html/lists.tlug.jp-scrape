Just a reminder from your friendly local TLUG server admin team.

We're been seeing a small rash of non-member submission bounces lately,
from people who are not subscribed to tlug-post.

In order to post to the tlug list, you must subscribe to tlug-post,
as noted on the mailing list information page on our website.  This must
be done for any address from which you would like to post, and you may
subscribe more than one address to tlug-post, even if you only receive
tlug at one address.

To subscribe to tlug-post, send a mail to majordomo@example.com, with:

subscribe you@example.com

in the body.  Of course, substitute your actual address in place of
you@example.com :-)

We're sorry to have to require this extra work, but the list started to
get spammed often enough that we had to institute a separate posting
list in order to keep the spam out.

Thanks,

Jonathan
On behalf of the TLUG server admin team
