While in Tokyo, I purchased two old Toshiba laptops for use as email
terminals for my wife and her sister.  I brought one of them in on the bus
this morning with the intention of doing a little work toward installing

The shop is on the main drag, where Yamagiwa and T-Zone are lined up, on

storefront sign.  It's next to a shoe store.

If anyone can help with this, please write to me privately (or pillory me
