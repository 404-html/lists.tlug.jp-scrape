At 14:32 00/2/3 +0900, you wrote:
> I've got an IBM Thinkpad 390 that I installed RH 6.1, dual booting 98.
> Sound seems to be a real problem, though, despite having found a couple
> of sample configs. 

http://www.jp.ibm.com/linux/lsc/linux_hw/tp39er61.html
says you should config the low-level driver to ESS Technology's ES1969
Solo-1 Audiodrive. It will recognize the card, but won't produce any
sound! (apparently only the comercial OSS sound driver works well with
this card)

I suggest you try ALSA instead of OSS (http://www.alsa-project.org)
The ES1969 Solo-1 is listed in the supported sound cards table:
http://www.alsa-project.org/~goemon/
