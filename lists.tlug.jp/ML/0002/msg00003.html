On Mon, 31 Jan 2000, Tony Laszlo wrote:

>    Still trying to get things back to normal 
>    in X. What would reduce the virtual screens 
>    in X from four to two (besides the obvious 
>    answer of "an inept user")? 
>    

May be you somehow lost the user specific kwmrc
(~/.kde/share/config/kwmrc) so it is falling back on the compiled in
default which could be 2.  (I get 4 virtual desktops by default,
though). Anyway,  look for the kwmrc and the line NumberOfDesktops=n.
You can change it back to 4. You can configure it also from
K->Panel->Configure.
