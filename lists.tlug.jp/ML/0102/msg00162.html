Ladies and gentlemen,

It looks like it's time for a lesson on TLUG mailing list behavior.

* It is considered rude to post (or report) portions of private conversations
  to the TLUG list without prior consent of all parties.

* It is considered rude to engage in personal attacks or character
  assassination.

* TLUG tries to be a community.  As such, personal bickering between members
  will be conducted via personal email or not at all.  Anything else is
  detrimental to the community.

The listmaster reserves the right to expel subscribers from the mailing list
for sufficient cause.  Act accordingly.

-- Chris
