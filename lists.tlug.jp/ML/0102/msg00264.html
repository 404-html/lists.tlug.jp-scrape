Debian (main distro) and Turbolinux (JP distro, at least) both install
reasonable configs of kterm, canna, kinput2 "out of the box".

http://www.turbolinux.co.jp/
http://www.debian.org/

Netscape is a mess, mozilla is worse.  I use lynx in a kterm for kanji
input if it's a lot, mozilla if I'm doing something really disgusting
like looking for 叶姉妹.  Yet another thing I hope you won't tell my
wife. ;-)
