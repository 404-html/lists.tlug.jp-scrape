Glimpse is a neat full text indexer/search engine. Basically, it builds
a huge dictionary of all the words in the texts you index, and then
lets you search this full-text index. As for the source, it's included
in the Harvest proxy cache distribution. Or you if you run Debian,
you can get a package in non-free.
