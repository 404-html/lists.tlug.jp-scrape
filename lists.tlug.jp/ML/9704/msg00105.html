Hi All,

On Monday 21st April, the tlug mailing list was truncated to only a
handful of subscribers.  Today Jim Tittsler brought this problem to our
attention.  The full list has been restored from backup this afternoon.  

Any posting made last week will therefore not have been distributed to the
full subscriber base.  Anyone who made a posting to this list last week
may wish to resubmit it.  The problem has been tracked down to a swap
space shortage, and majordomo not checking write status. 

My apologies to all subscribers, and thanks to Jim for alerting us.

Regards,
Paul Gampe.
