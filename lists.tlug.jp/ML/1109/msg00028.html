I got /64 from KDDI.

en0: flags=8863<UP,BROADCAST,SMART,RUNNING,SIMPLEX,MULTICAST> mtu 1500
	options=27<RXCSUM,TXCSUM,VLAN_MTU,TSO4>
	ether 00:25:4b:bd:5a:8c
	inet6 fe80::225:4bff:febd:5a8c%en0 prefixlen 64 scopeid 0x4
	inet6 240f:8:1cc4:1:225:4bff:febd:5a8c prefixlen 64 autoconf
	inet6 240f:8:1cc4:1:b41f:2e4d:5160:b86d prefixlen 64 autoconf temporary
	inet 192.168.0.5 netmask 0xffffff00 broadcast 192.168.0.255
	media: autoselect (1000baseT <full-duplex>)
	status: active
and the address is routeable:
