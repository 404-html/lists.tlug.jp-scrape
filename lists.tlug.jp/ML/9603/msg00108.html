  We're drifting into socio-economic issues here.

1.	Bmail is the 2nd or 3rd most popular email package in the world.  Please 
don't be ethnocentric and discount it because it's not widely used by 
Japanese Linux users.  However, I agree it's the worst mailer I've ever 
used.

2.	I can do something about it, I may not be able to figure out how to stop 
the quoting, but I can always create a new message instead of replying.

3.	Cyber is the cause for the blank subjects, but various Cyber related

and he should send me his bank info so I can wire a few more months payment 
for my home acct.

4.	If your mailer is getting too much data, from this or any mail list, 
consider going client server, i.e. don't use Eudora, etc. which downloads 
the mail to your pc or workstation, use Pine, mail, elm, or something that 
stores your mailbox on the remote server.
