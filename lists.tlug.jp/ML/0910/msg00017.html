> ..and he thinks he might rather run Ubuntu than the openSUSE that I

This is as simple (for you) as two words: "install away!" :-)

Serously, this might really be the lesson that brings him to a true
understanding of IT.

As far as the network problem, so long as the Live CD had Internet
access, it's almost certainly solvable without too much difficulty.
However, the devil is in the details, of which there are not a lot in
your post.

I suggest you start by logging in and then running "sudo
/etc/init.d/dhclient restart". That should provide some informative
output, and possibly even get you connected. The output of "ifconfig -a"
would also be helpful.
