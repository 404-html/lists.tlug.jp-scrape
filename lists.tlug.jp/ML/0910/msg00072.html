I'm thinking in terms of getting an eBook reader in order to avoid filling
up my rapidly dwindling space with more books.

formats around that are supported by different eReaders, and that there's
also a certain amount of DRM involved.

It's the DRM that's worrying me a little. Am I right in assuming that I'm
going to have to use proprietary software (read: Windows software) to
transfer any eBooks I download onto the eReader?

and a Sony eReader that looks like the PRS-505, so I assume the former are
compatible with the latter (or am I giving bean counters too much credit

compatible with Linux.

Meanwhile I'm writing to Waterstones to find out exactly what format they
sell their eBooks in and exactly what model the eReader is.
