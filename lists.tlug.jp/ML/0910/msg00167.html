As some of you may be aware we had a problem with the venue reservation, 
they had limited us to 40 persons and a sit-down event.

Fortunately, I just got a call from Alberto and he said that the manager 
had conceded their mistake and had worked to get things back to the 
original reservation.

This means that we are no longer limited to 40 persons and the event will 
be a standing buffet as originally planned.

If wish to come along, but aren't in the 40 confirmed below, then please 
contact Ed (090-3689-0292) and get your name put on the list!
We should be able to handle 50-60 persons with the current catering 
arrangement.

Venue info, etc:
http://www.tlug.jp/wiki/Meetings:15th_Anniversary_Event
