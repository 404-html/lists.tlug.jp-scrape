Sorry about the late notice on this one. The next meeting of the Tokyo
Society for the Application of Currying, a group for those interested in
functional programming, will be tomorrow, Thursday October 29th, at 7:30
p.m. at the offices of Starling Software.

This month Travis Cardwell will be presenting "Exploring Category Theory
with Haskell." But don't run away! This presentation is suitable for
those with only undergraduate mathematical experience, as they say.

Linked on the page below is a little problem you might want to try
solving before the talk. Travis will be demonstrating a simple solution
using category theory.

Full information, including maps and directions, is available here:

  http://www.starling-software.com/en/tsac.html
