       TLUG - Tokyo Linux Users Group 15th Anniversary Party
      =======================================================

     Date & Time: October 22 (Thursday), 19:30-22:00

     Place:	  Chiocciol@ Pizzeria

     Address:	  Akihabara UDX 1F
                  Chiyoda-ku, Sotokanda 4-14-1
		  http://www.pizzeria.jp/2008/02/chiocciolpizzeria.html

     Menu:        antipasto, pizzas margherita and quattro formacio,
                  spaghetti, insalata mista, dolce

     Drinks:      wine (red & white), draft beer, orange juice,
                  oolong tea

     Event:       standing party with buffet dinner and
                  all-you-can-drink (primarily a social activity)

     Cost:        5,000 yen/person

     This event is open to all Linux users and friends, and is
     to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the creation of the Tokyo
     Linux User Group, over a few cold ones. Even if you're not a Linux
     user or a LUG member, but want to hang out with Linux people,
     please come.

     Please reply ASAP as positions are limited.

     If you get lost,
     or simply want to get in touch with us, please call:


        Edward Middleton............090-3689-0292
        Alberto Tomita..............080-3463-9256


     For more information, access the TLUG web page at
     http://tlug.jp/


     See you there!
