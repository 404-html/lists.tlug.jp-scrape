Yes, Daniel Robbins had previously worked on FreeBSD, and Gentoo's
portage is rather similar to FreeBSD's ports.  The way it works is that
you would choose your package, for example, firefox, then do something
like

emerge firefox

(I'm fairly sure that's the syntax, but if you get involved in Gentoo,
you'll find the docs are right there.)


Although there are sometimes broken ebuilds (their term for the makefile
that will do the work, generally, that emerge command will..

(those source files) including the dependencies, and compile all of
them, in proper order.
