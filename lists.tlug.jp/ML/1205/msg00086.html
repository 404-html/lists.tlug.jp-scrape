All attendees of TPC/TLUG Sawanoi Sake Brewery tour,

May 19 - the day of brewery touring, JR East Chuo Line will
be suspend or reduced largely due to his construction work.

According announcement [1] , Chuo line train will be suspended
 or reduced between Higashi-Koganei and about Kunitachi from
14:15 May 19 to 7:00 May 20.

It will make influence our backing way to Tokyo. Please consider
planning alternative backing way to your home.

For some example of backing way to Shinjuku,

Sawai ---(JR Chuo Line)--- Haijima ---(Seibu-Haijima Line)--- Kodaira
---(Seibu-Shinjuku Line)--- Takata-no-baba ---(JR Yamanote Line)--- Shinjuku

Sawai ---(JR Chuo Line)--- Tachikawa ---(JR Nanbu Line)--- Bubai-gawara
---(Keio Line)---  Shinjuku

And JR Chuo Line will be crowed due to reduce or suspend.
We have to pay some attention of train route.
