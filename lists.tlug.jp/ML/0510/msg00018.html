Laox (www.laox.co.jp) The Computer-Kan moved the bookstore from the
grand (1st) floor to the top floow (7th?). But still stays near to the
Bank of Tokyo Mitsubishi. However, Bank of Tokyo Mitsubishi closed the
bank counter, and left the ATM only, at the Chuou-Dori street
crossing. 

T-Zone Minami was closed and you can only find Donki Houte.
