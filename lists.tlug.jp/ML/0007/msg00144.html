I've attached an email from Donald Becker to the linux kernel list on
a related problem.  I would suggest you add this entry in 3c59x.c, and 
recompile your kernel:

	{"3c555 Laptop Hurricane",{ 0x505510B7, 0xffffffff },
	 PCI_IOTYPE, CYCLONE_SIZE, IS_TORNADO|HAS_NWAY, },
