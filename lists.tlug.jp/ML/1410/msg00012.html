The Japanese Haskell community in Tokyo is quite large.  Note that most
people do not use Meetup, though.  If you would like to find more
attendees, the key is to be listed in IT勉強会カレンダー:

https://www.google.com/calendar/embed?src=fvijvohm91uifvd9hratehf65k@example.com

Many event organizers manage their events in ATND or similar services, and
the events are then included in IT勉強会カレンダー automatically.
