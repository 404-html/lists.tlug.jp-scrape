A) NTT Data, a multi-billion dollar corporation in Japan, recently 
released Mini Kernel Dump on SF.net.     Mini Kernel Dump is a 
Linux-based mini-kernel that runs underneath Linux kernels on IA-32 
systems (other architectures pending), providing a stable environment 
for capturing and dumping crash dump data.
Home Page: http://mkdump.sourceforge.net/
