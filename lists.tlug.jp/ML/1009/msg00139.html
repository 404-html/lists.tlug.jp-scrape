We’ve had a new urgent requirement from our client, which is a Fortune 50 firm.

Cloud Computing Architect – Tokyo – 9M to 15M jpy/yr

 

The candidate will join a team which works closely with application development groups to understand their use of technology and identify solutions that best meet their application and infrastructure requirements.

 

The main responsibilities of the role are to act as an architect in technical projects, providing knowledge, guidelines, and hands-on creation of technical solutions. Strategic engagement with application development teams to provide end-to-end delivery of appropriate infrastructure solutions. Provide technical guidance on established infrastructure best practice principals.

 

Skills:

 

-Proficient in infrastructure architecture of scalable, automated HPC deployments

-In-depth knowledge of various enterprise systems: Linux/Unix, and Windows

-Experience with virtualization technologies

-Understanding of SAN/NAS and associated protocols

-Strong scripting knowledge (Perl, shell, Python, C) or Dtrace/Systemtap is a plus

-Broad knowledge of high performance, highly-available computing environments and integration of technical products into complex environments.

-Must be able to work in a fast-paced environment

-Skills in running technical program management exercises

-Excellent analytical and problem solving skills.

 

Please apply if you fit the bill.
