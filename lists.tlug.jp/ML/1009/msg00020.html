This error has come completely out of the blue. All my networked computers were working fine up until a day ago. I haven't changed anything - so far as I know - with network settings or anything related.

Here is the /etc/fstab entry:
# Mythbuntu
192.168.0.4:/home/media /home/dave/Mythbuntu nfs rsize=8192,wsize=8192,timeo=14,intr
