Using Adobe Acrobat reader 3.0 "acroread" is a very nice way
to read pdf files under Linux.  I can get a lot of really good
technical brochures on my Linux box. It looks really good.  
Adobe Acrobat blows away xpdf in terms of user interface, scaling
of pictures and general readability of of pdf docs.
