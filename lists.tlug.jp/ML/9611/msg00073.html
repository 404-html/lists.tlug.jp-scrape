  Hi all,

  I finally got around to trying out TkMan yesterday.  It is a superb program!
It's makes xman look like something out of the stone age.  If you ever read
manpages, you want it.

  In case you missed the recent posting on linux-announce, here's where to
get it:

	A new version of TkMan, 1.8, is now available for anonymous ftp at
	<URL:ftp://ftp.cs.berkeley.edu/ucb/people/phelps/tcltk/tkman.tar.Z>
	You also need RosettaMan, a manual page filter:
	<URL:ftp://ftp.cs.berkeley.edu/ucb/people/phelps/tcltk/rman.tar.Z>
	TkMan also requires Tcl 7.5 and Tk 4.1, or later versions, which are
	available on ftp.smli.com in the /pub/tcl directory.

  You'll probably also want to get glimpse, if you don't have it.  There are
precompiled binaries for Linux (as well as source) at:
  http://glimpse.cs.arizona.edu:1994/

  Enjoy!
  
Dennis McMurchy, 
Tojinmachi, Fukuoka
