I just found the elisp interface to ejdic.  It  looks great but when I
try to  use it with the  mule-delta I am  getting a list of what looks

(setq coding-system-alist

	("\\.elc$" insert-file-contents . no-conversion)

	("\\.jp$" insert-file-contents . coding-system-iso-2022-jp)
