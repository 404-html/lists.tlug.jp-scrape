On Mon, 16 Aug 1999, Uva Coder wrote:

> At first glance, I would suggest that you check your /etc/resolv.conf
> file for errors.
> You should have an entry similiar to the following:
> 
> domain  gol.com
> nameserver 203.216.12.3


Umm, GOL's namer servers are:

ns01.dns.gol.com        internet address = 203.216.4.4
ns02.dns.gol.com        internet address = 203.216.4.5
ns03.dns.gol.com        internet address = 203.216.70.70
ns04.dns.gol.com        internet address = 203.216.71.70

Put any two in your resolv.conf (GOL customers only, please  ;-)  ),
preferably either ns01 or ns02 and either ns03 and ns04 (so that you
have nameservers on different subnets).

203.216.12.3 is not a name server.
