At 09:51 99/8/21 -0400, you wrote:
> eject: open failed for '/dev/hdc': No such device

Looks like a ATAPI CD-ROM attached to the secondary IDE 
controller.

> I had my CDROM working until 8 AM this morning. All I did was changed my 
> video card and closed the box.

Chances are that your new video card is taking the IRQ from
the IDE controller (IRQ 14 or 15). Check your /proc/irq to confirm.
