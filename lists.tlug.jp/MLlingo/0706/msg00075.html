I like these, and they remind me of one of my favourites:

冗談じゃねぇぞ！

Which is translated as, "this ain't no fuckin' joke, man!". :) 

Or you could impersonate an indignant country dweller.

an da pei sa... joudan darou ka!

"what you on about, you gotta be kidding me".
