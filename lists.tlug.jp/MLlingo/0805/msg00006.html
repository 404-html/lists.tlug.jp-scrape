On 04/05/2008, steven smith <sjs@example.com> wrote:
>  There is a Ghibli film titled おもひでぽろぽろ .  The
>  english name for this film is "Only Yesterday."  I can't
>  figure out what the Japanese title means.  Can you guys help?
>  http://www.aic.gr.jp/anime/ghibli/1991o.shtml

If you look up 思い in a good 国語辞典 you'll see something like:
"おもい　おもひ ..【思い】 ....."

おもひ is the old kana version of 思い/おもい. Used now in literary
contexts to give a sense of long ago. I'll let you work out the
ぽろぽろ....
