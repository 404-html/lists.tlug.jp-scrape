Two bookmarks I have are:

http://www.geocities.jp/niwasaburoo/index.html

(a book style guide) and

http://www.nihongo2.com/

in particular:

http://www.nihongo2.com/dictionary/dictionary.html

(a list style guide)

They're both in Japanese, I can't really say much about their
accuracy, but at least they have volume on their side...
