Note that hisashiburi also adds a touch of nostalgic flavour in most cases.

> 東京は久しぶりだね　- It's been a while since we last been to Tokyo.

Or, 「久しぶりに東京に行った。」 - "I went back to Tokyo (after many years--oh how I
missed it)".
